# Lesson 11: End of Unit 2 Assessment: Research Process
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students complete the End of Unit 2 Assessment. They make decisions about a hypothetical research project surrounding a familiar topic (the Hippo Roller from the Mid-Unit 2 Assessment) and answer selected response and short constructed response questions about their choices.

**Focus Standards:** W.6.7, W.6.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can refocus my search for more relevant results. (W.6.7)
- I can determine the credibility of possible sources. (W.6.8)
- I can paraphrase responsibly and provide basic bibliography information for sources. (W.6.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return Mid-Unit 2 Assessments (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 2 Assessment: Research Process – W.6.7, W.6.8 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – W.6.7, W.6.8 (10 minutes)
