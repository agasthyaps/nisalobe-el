# Lesson 8: Conduct Research: Gather Additional Sources
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students participate in a mini lesson, learning to effectively search for sources to complete research projects. Then, students search for sources, evaluate sources for their relevance, and record bibliographic information on their note-catchers.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.7, W.6.8, SL.6.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can conduct research to answer questions about another design solution that solved a critical problem. (W.6.7, W.6.8)
- I can determine the relevance of possible sources. (W.6.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.8 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mini Lesson: Search for Sources – W.6.7 (10 minutes) → Research: Search for Sources – W.6.7, W.6.8 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
