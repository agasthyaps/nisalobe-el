**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Next, read the following sentence from chapter 11 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind* (192). First, determine the type of figurative language it uses. Then, sketch two drawings that illustrate the literal and figurative meanings of the sentence.

“The radio sat on the ground between us, sizzling like an egg.”

What type of figurative language is used in this sentence? (Choose one.)

1.  <u>simile</u>

2.  metaphor

3.  personification

4.  hyperbole
