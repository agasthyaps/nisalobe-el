# Lesson 2: Close Read: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 11
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** A close read will invite students to analyze a portion of chapter 11 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind* for its central idea and how that central idea is conveyed through key details. Then, students participate in a Language Dive, analyzing words and phrases that signal text structure and interpret

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.3, RI.6.4, RI.6.5, L.6.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine meanings of words and phrases as they are used in the text. (RI.6.4)
- I can determine the central idea of a text and how it is conveyed through details. (RI.6.2)
- I can analyze how a key event is elaborated in a text. (RI.6.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Close Read: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Pages 196–201 – RI.6.2 (25 minutes) → Language Dive: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Page 200 – L.6.5c (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
