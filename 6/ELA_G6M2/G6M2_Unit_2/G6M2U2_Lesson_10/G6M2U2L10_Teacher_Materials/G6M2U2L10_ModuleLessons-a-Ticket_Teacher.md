**Research Scenario**: For the Critical Problem and Design Solution Research Project, you have chosen to research Lumos, a company that makes a bicycle helmet with lights to improve biker safety.

Assess the credibility of the following source. Does it seem reliable? Explain your thinking.

![](media/image1.emf)

Google and the Google logo are registered trademarks of Google LLC, used with permission under the public license located at [https://www.google.com/permissions/products/.](https://www.google.com/permissions/products/)

Answers will vary. Sample response: This source seems reliable because the title of the website sounds like a publisher who focuses on the sport of bicycling. Their reviewers likely know a lot of topics related to biking. The publication date shows that the review is recent, so the information will be current. The name of the publisher and the author of this particular review are included. The caption makes clear that this review is focused on the Lumos helmet, which is the correct information needed for this research scenario.
