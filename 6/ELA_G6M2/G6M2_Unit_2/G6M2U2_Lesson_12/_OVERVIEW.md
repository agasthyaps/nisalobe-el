# Lesson 12: Independent Reading Research Share
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** Students participate in a Language Dive to determine the meaning of a conjunction as it is used in a sentence from the text and determine the precise meaning of a word with multiple meanings. Students complete a QuickWrite in which they integrate information presented in different formats to reflect

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.4, RI.6.7, L.6.4a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine meanings of words and phrases as they are used in the text. (L.6.4a)
- I can share my independent research reading with my peers (RI.6.10, SL.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.7 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Page 265 – L.6.4a (10 minutes) → Share Independent Reading Research – RI.6.10 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
