# Lesson 13: Prepare for Performance Task: Rehearse and Refine
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** Students continue preparing for the performance task by composing the presentation prompt answers and rehearsing their presentation.

**Focus Standards:** SL.6.1, SL.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can respond to symposium guests’ presentation prompts. (SL.6.1c)
- I can give kind, helpful, and specific feedback to my peers. (SL.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.10 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Prepare Performance Task: Presentation Prompts – SL.6.1 (15 minutes) → Rehearse Performance Task – SL.6.4 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
