# Lesson 10: Problem-Solution Essay: Peer Critique and Revision
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** Students engage in the Peer Critique Protocol, in which they pair up with another peer to critique the use of sentence variety in the essays they wrote for the Mid-Unit 3 Assessment.

**Focus Standards:** W.6.2, W.6.5, SL.6.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can provide kind, specific, and helpful feedback to peers. (SL.6.1)
- I can use feedback to revise my problem-solution essay. (W.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Peer Critique: Problem-Solution Essay – W.6.5 (20 minutes) → Revise Problem-Solution Essay – W.6.2 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
