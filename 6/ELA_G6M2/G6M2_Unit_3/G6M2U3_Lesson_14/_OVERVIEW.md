# Lesson 14: Performance Task: Solution Symposium
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** Students participate in the performance task: Solution Symposium. The students function as presenters as well as audience guests. The students complete a Solution Symposium note-catcher to document their learning from the symposium.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.7, W.6.7, W.6.8, SL.6.1, SL.6.2, SL.6.4, SL.6.5, SL.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can effectively present my performance task and interact with my audience during the Solution Symposium. (RI.6.7, RI.6.10, W.6.7, W.6.8, SL.6.1, SL.6.2, SL.6.4, SL.6.5, SL.6.6)
- I can interpret information presented in diverse formats by my peers during the Solution Symposium. (SL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Performance Task: Solution Symposium – SL.6.4 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Prepare for End of Unit 3 Assessment – SL.6.1 (10 minutes)
