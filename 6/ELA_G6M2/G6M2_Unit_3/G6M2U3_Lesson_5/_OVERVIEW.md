# Lesson 5: Collaborative Problem-Solution Essay: Draft Proof Paragraph 2
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students continue to draft their collaborative essay, focusing on Proof Paragraph 2 (the solution).

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.2a, W.6.2b, W.6.2c, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.8, SL.6.1, SL.6.2, L.6.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of the model essay solution paragraph. (W.6.2b)
- I can collaboratively write the solution paragraph for an essay about William. (W.6.2b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Proof Paragraph 2 – W.6.2b (10 minutes) → Collaborative Writing: Draft Proof Paragraph 2 – W.6.2b (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
