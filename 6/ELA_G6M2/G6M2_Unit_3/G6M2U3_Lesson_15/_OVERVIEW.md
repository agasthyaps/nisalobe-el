# Lesson 15: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Fishbowl Discussion: Habits of Character to Solve Critical Problems
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 15**

**Summary:** Students participate in a modified fishbowl discussion in which they synthesize their learning from the module by discussing, “How do habits of character help people solve critical problems?”

**Focus Standards:** SL.6.1a, SL.6.1b, SL.6.1c, SL.6.2, SL.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can use evidence to support my ideas during a discussion. (SL.6.1a, SL.6.2)
- I can follow discussion norms to have an effective text-based discussion. (SL.6.1b)
- I ask questions to better understand what others are saying and to explore the topic. (SL.6.1c)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return Mid-Unit 3 Assessments (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 3 Assessment, Part I: QuickWrite – W.6.10 (5 minutes) → End of Unit 3 Assessment, Part II: Fishbowl Discussion – SL.6.1, SL.6.2, SL.6.6 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
