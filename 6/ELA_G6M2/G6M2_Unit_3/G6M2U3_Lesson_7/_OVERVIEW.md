# Lesson 7: Plan a Problem-Solution Essay: Research
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students use what they learned during the collaborative writing process to independently plan their problem-solution essay based on their research in Unit 2.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.8, SL.6.1, SL.6.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan a problem-solution essay about my research. (W.6.2)
- I can give kind, helpful, and specific feedback to my partner. (SL.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Independent Practice: Plan a Problem-Solution Essay – W.6.2 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Peer Critique: Problem-Solution Writing Planner – SL.6.1 (10 minutes)
