# Lessons 8–9: Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Write a Problem-Solution Essay
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lessons 8–9**

**Summary:** Over the course of two lessons, students complete the Mid-Unit 3 Assessment for which they independently draft their problem-solution essays based on their research in Unit 2.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.6, W.6.8, W.6.9b, SL.6.2, L.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can draft a problem and solution essay about my research. (RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.8, W.6.9b, SL.6.2, L.6.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return End of Unit 2 Assessments (10 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Write a Problem-Solution Essay (65 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – W.6.2 (15 minutes)
