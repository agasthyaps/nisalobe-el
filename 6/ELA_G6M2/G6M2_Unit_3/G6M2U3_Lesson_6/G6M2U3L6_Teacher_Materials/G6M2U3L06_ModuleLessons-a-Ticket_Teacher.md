Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 6

L.6.3a

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Then, read these sentences based on Avery Bang’s TED Talk, “Building Bridges and Connecting Communities.”

“Bridges that connect rural villages to needed resources can lead to a 30% increase in household income, a 12% increase in school attendance, and an 18% increase in health care treatment.”

Notice the author used the word **increase** several times. Rewrite this sentence to avoid repetitive wording. Use multiple sentences to convey the information, if necessary. Do not change the meaning of the statement.

Answers will vary. Sample response: Bridges that connect rural villages to needed resources create a number of benefits. A household’s income can increase by 30%, school attendance by 12%, and health care treatment by 18%.

Source: Bang, Avery. “Building Bridges and Connecting Communities.” *TED* Archive. 7 May 2018. <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6Sr0qQlK2g4>
