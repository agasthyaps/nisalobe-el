# Lesson 6: Collaborative Problem-Solution Essay: Draft Conclusion
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students draft the conclusion of their collaborative essay before engaging in a peer review of their writing.

**Focus Standards:** W.6.2f, W.6.4, W.6.5, L.6.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of the model essay conclusion. (W.6.2f)
- I can determine the characteristics of a strong reflection by analyzing the model. (W.6.2f)
- I can collaboratively write a conclusion with a strong reflection for a problem-solution essay about William. (W.6.2f)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → The Painted Essay®: Sort and Color-Code the Parts of the Conclusion – W.6.2f (10 minutes) → Language Dive: Model Problem-Solution Essay: “Bridges to Prosperity,” Conclusion – W.6.2f (10 minutes) → Collaborative Writing: Draft Conclusion – W.6.2f (15 minutes)
