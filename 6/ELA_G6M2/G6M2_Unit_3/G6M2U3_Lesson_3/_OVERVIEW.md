# Lesson 3: Collaborative Problem-Solution Essay: Draft Introduction
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students use their writing plan to continue working on their collaborative essay, focusing specifically on drafting the introduction.

**Focus Standards:** W.6.2a, W.6.4, W.6.5, SL.6.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of the model essay introduction. (W.6.2a)
- I can determine the characteristics of an effective focus statement by analyzing the model. (W.6.2a)
- I can collaboratively write an introduction with a strong focus statement for a problem-solution essay about William. (W.6.2a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → The Painted Essay®: Sort and Color-Code the Parts of an Introduction – W.6.2a (15 minutes) → Collaborative Writing: Draft an Introduction – W.6.2a (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
