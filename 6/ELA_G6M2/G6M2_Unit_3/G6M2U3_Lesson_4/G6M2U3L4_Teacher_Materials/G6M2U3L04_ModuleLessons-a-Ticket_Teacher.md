Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 4

L.6.3a

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Then, read this sentence from Avery Bang’s TED Talk, “Building Bridges and Connecting Communities.”

“I imagined other communities around Fiji, and likely the world, that could be transformed simply by having connection, and on a more personal note, I saw an opportunity to use my skills and experience to build bridges, both figuratively and literally.”

Revise this complex sentence into two or more simpler sentences without changing the meaning of her statement.

Answers will vary. Sample response: I imagined other communities around Fiji, and likely the world, that could be transformed simply by having connection. On a more personal note, I saw an opportunity to use my skills and experience to build bridges, both figuratively and literally.

Source: Bang, Avery. “Building Bridges and Connecting Communities.” *TED* Archive. 7 May 18. Web.
