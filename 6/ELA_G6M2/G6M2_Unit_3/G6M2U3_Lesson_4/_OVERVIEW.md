# Lesson 4: Collaborative Problem-Solution Essay: Draft Proof Paragraph 1
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students draft the first Proof Paragraph (the problem) of their collaborative essays.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.2a, W.6.2b, W.6.2c, W.6.4, W.6.5, SL.6.2, L.6.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of the model essay problem paragraph. (W.6.2b)
- I can collaboratively write the problem paragraph for an essay about William. (W.6.2b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Proof Paragraph 1 – W.6.2b (10 minutes) → Language Dive: Model Problem-Solution Essay: “Bridges to Prosperity,” Proof Paragraph 1 – W.6.2c (10 minutes) → Collaborative Writing: Draft Proof Paragraph 1 – W.6.2b (15 minutes)
