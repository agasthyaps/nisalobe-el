Synopsis: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind,* Chapter 2

**Name:** **Date:**

- William’s Uncle John suddenly dies.

- William’s Uncle Socrates loses his job at a tobacco estate, so Uncle Socrates moves back to Masitala.

- Uncle Socrates has a dog named Khamba.

- William is not interested in the dog and constantly tells Khamba to “get away.”

- Khamba takes a liking to William and follows him around the farm.

- William stops trying to get rid of Khamba, and the two go hunting together.

- The two friends share the spoils of their hunt together.
