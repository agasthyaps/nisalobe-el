# Lesson 4: Analyze William: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 2
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 2 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. Then, they determine the central idea by revisiting an exercise similar to that of the previous lesson.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find the gist of chapter 2 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*.
- I can analyze how William is developed in the text. (RI.6.3)
- I can determine a central idea of a text and how it is conveyed through particular details. (RI.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 2 – RI.6.3 (15 minutes) → Determine Central Idea of a Chapter – RI.6.2 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
