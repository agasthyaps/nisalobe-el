7.  How do the section titles help to develop a central idea of this article? (RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.5, W.6.10)

The section titles help to mark the different stages of William’s design thinking and reinforce the \[problem-solution/chronological-sequential\] structure of the article. These work together to lay out the impact of William’s resourceful and diligent nature in solving the problem. The sequence of events in the article highlight the difficulty of William’s success with the windmill from the inception of the idea to the global exposure received through TED. The section titles highlight the difficulty but also the perseverance William exhibited by tackling each obstacle throughout the windmill design and build process.
