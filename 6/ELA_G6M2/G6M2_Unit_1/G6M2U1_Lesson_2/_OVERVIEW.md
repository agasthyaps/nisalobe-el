# Lesson 2: Establish Reading Routines: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 1
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students read the chapter 1 excerpt of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind* and determine its gist. Then, they practice analyzing how a key individual (the narrator, William) is introduced in the text and in a related TED Talk.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how William is introduced and developed in *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. (RI.6.3)
- I can analyze how William is introduced in the TED Talk. (RI.6.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 1 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Analyze Introduction of Key Individual: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind* – RI.6.3 (10 minutes) → View TED Talk: “How I Built a Windmill” – RI.6.3 (10 minutes)
