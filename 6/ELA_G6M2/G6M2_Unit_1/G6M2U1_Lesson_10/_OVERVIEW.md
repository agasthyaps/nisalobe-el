# Lesson 10: Analyze Figurative Language: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 6
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 6 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. Then, they participate in a Back-to-Back and Face-to-Face protocol to interpret figures of speech and determine the figurative meanings of words as they are used in chapter 6.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.3, RI.6.4, L.6.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how William is developed in the text. (RI.6.3)
- I can interpret figurative language and use connotations to determine meaning in the text. (L.6.5)
- I can write an effective summary of chapter 6 using key details. (RI.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 6 – RI.6.3 (20 minutes) → Analyze Figurative Language – L.6.5 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
