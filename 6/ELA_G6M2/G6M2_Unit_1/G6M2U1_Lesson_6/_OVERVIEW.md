# Lesson 6: Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Central Idea and Development of an Individual: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 4
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students complete the Mid-Unit 1 Assessment and then track their developing abilities to identify central ideas; analyze how key individuals, events, or ideas are introduced in a text; and determine the meaning of words as they are used in a text.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.3, RI.6.4, L.6.4a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine a central idea of chapter 4 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind* and how it is conveyed through particular details. (RI.6.2)
- I can analyze how William is developed in chapter 4 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. (RI.6.3)
- I can determine the meaning of words and phrases as they are used in chapter 4 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. (RI.6.4, L.6.4a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Central Idea and Development of an Individual: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 4 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – RI.6.1 (10 minutes)
