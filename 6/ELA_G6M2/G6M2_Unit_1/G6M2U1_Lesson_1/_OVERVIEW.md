# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: Design Thinking
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students participate in the Infer the Topic protocol by engaging with resources related to the topic of the module. Then, the performance task and module guiding questions are introduced, and the anchor text, *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, is launched.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from the resources. (RI.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Infer the Topic – RI.6.1 (15 minutes) → Introduce the Performance Task and Module Guiding Questions (10 minutes) → Launch the Text: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind* (10 minutes)
