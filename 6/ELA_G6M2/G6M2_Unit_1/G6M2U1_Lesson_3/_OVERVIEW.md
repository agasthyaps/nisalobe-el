# Lesson 3: Determine Central Idea: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 1
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students read the chapter 1 excerpt of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. Then, they analyze the central idea of chapter 1 by locating recurrent details and distilling them into a single idea. They represent these details and central idea(s) with a simple visual.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find the gist of chapter 1 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*.
- I can analyze how William is introduced and developed in *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. (RI.6.3)
- I can determine a central idea in the text and how it is conveyed through particular details. (RI.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 1 Excerpt – RI.6.3 (25 minutes) → Determine Central Idea: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 1 – RI.6.2 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
