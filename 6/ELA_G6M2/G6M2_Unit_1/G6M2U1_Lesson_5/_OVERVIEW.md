# Lesson 5: Summarize *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 3
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 3 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. Then, they review principles of summary writing and use sentence strips to produce an effective summary of the chapter.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find the gist of chapter 3 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*.
- I can analyze how William is developed in the text. (RI.6.3)
- I can write an effective summary of chapter 3 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. (RI.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 3 – RI.6.3 (25 minutes) → Review Summary Writing – RI.6.2 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
