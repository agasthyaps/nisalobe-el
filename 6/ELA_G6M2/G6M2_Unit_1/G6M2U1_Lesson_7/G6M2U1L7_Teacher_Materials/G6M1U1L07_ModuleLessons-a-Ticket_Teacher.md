(For Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Next, read the sentence from *The Lightning Thief*, and answer the following question.

“On this side of the house, the valley marched all the way up to the water.” (61)

What picture does this sentence create in your mind? Draw it below.

Answers will vary. Students should depict a valley as a lower area of land touching water.
