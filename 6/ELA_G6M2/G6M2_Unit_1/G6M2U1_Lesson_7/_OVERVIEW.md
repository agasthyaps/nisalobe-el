**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students participate in a Language Dive that invites them to interpret the authors’ use of personification in a sentence from *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*. **Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.3, RI.6.4, L.6.4, L.6.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can interpret figurative language to determine meaning in the text. (L.6.5)
- I can determine a central idea in the text and how it is conveyed through particular details. (RI.6.2)
- I can analyze how William is introduced and developed in “William Kamkwamba’s Electric Wind.” (RI.6.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Page 68 – L.6.5 (15 minutes) → Close Read: “William Kamkwamba’s Electric Wind” – RI.6.2 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
