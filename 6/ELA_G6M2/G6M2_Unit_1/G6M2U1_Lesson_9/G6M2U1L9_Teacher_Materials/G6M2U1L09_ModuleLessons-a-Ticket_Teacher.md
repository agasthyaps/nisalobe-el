Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 9

L.6.4c, W.6.10

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions**: As you enter class, read the learning targets. Then, complete the following activity.

Read the definition of **compassion**: a feeling of concern for another person’s suffering and the desire to help.

What does **compassion** look like?

Refer to the Work to Become Ethical People Anchor Chart (example for teacher reference). Sample student response: Compassion looks like hugging a friend or classmate who is upset.

What does **compassion** sound like?

Refer to the Work to Become Ethical People Anchor Chart (example for teacher reference). Sample student response: Compassion sounds like asking someone, “Is there anything I can do to help?”
