# Lesson 9: Analyze Language: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 5
**Grade 6: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** After students finish reading chapter 5 of *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, they participate in a Language Dive to determine the figurative and connotative meanings of words as they are used in the text.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how William is developed in the text. (RI.6.3)
- I can determine the meaning of words as they are used in the text by identifying context clues. (L.6.4a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4c (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Chapter 5 Excerpt – RI.6.3 (20 minutes) → Language Dive: *The Boy Who Harnessed the Wind*, Page 92 – L.6.4a (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
