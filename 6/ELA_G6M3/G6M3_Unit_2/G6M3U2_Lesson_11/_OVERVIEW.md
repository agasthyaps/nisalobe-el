# Lesson 11: Gather and Analyze Evidence
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students continue practicing correct pronoun use, specifically in creating agreement of number and person between pronouns and their antecedents. Students then use their Point of View and Character note-catchers to gather evidence for why Cal should return to Challagi at the end of the novel and for

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, W.6.1b, W.6.4, W.6.9a, L.6.1c, L.6.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can vary sentence patterns for meaning and style. (L.6.3a)
- I can correct inappropriate shifts in pronoun number and person. (L.6.1c)
- I can gather evidence about why Cal should and should not return to Challagi. (W.6.1b, W.6.9a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mini Lesson: Pronoun Person and Number – L.6.1c (15 minutes) → Gather Evidence: Cal Returns to School – W.6.1b, W.6.9a (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
