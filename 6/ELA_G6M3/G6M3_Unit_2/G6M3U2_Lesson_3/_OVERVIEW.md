# Lesson 3: Analyze Point of View and Theme: *Two Roads*, Chapter 22
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students continue building on their awareness of the themes in *Two Roads*. A summary of chapter 22 helps students to connect their ability to identify key details and determine theme in a structured paragraph.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.5, RL.6.6, L.6.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine how Cal’s point of view is conveyed in chapter 22. (RL.6.6)
- I can analyze how the structure of chapter 22 helps to develop a theme of *Two Roads*. (RL.6.2, RL.6.5)
- I can plan an objective summary of chapter 22 of *Two Roads*. (RL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Two Roads*, Chapter 22 – RL.6.6 (20 minutes) → Analyze Structure and Theme – RL.6.2, RL.6.5 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
