# Lesson 4: Recognize Correct Pronoun Use
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students revisit Language standards by continuing to build their understanding of the different types of and uses for pronouns.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.3, RL.6.6, L.6.1a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine how Cal’s point of view is conveyed in chapter 22. (RL.6.6)
- I can recognize and correct pronoun case. (L.6.1a)
- I can use specific steps to ensure that pronouns are in the proper case. (L.6.1a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Write a Summary – RL.6.2 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Two Roads*, Chapter 23 – RL.6.6 (15 minutes) → Mini Lesson: Pronoun Case – L.6.1a (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
