# Lesson 13: End of Unit 2 Assessment: Revise Narrative Writing for Pronoun Use and Sentence Variety
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** Students take the end of unit assessment, in which they revise their narrative letter for pronoun usage and sentence variety.

**Focus Standards:** L.6.1a, L.6.1c, L.6.1d, L.6.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can use unambiguous pronouns with correct case, number, and person in a narrative letter. (L.6.1)
- I can vary sentence patterns for meaning and style in a narrative letter. (L.6.3a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Revise Narrative Writing for Pronoun Use and Sentence Variety (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Reflection (10 minutes)
