# Lesson 12: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Rehearse and Refine Performance Task Recording
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** As part of the end of unit assessment, students record themselves reading aloud their preface, their selected text, and their reflection to be featured during the audio museum performance task.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.6, SL.6.2, SL.6.6, L.6.3b, L.6.6 (optional L.6.1)

**Learning Targets:**
- I can assess and revise my performance task recording. (SL.6.6, L.6.6)
- I can reflect on, assess, and give feedback on my peers’ recording. (SL.6.2)
- I can correctly use pronouns and vary my sentences for meaning and interest. (optional) (L.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Part I (10 minutes) → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Part II (10 minutes) → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Part III (15 minutes)
