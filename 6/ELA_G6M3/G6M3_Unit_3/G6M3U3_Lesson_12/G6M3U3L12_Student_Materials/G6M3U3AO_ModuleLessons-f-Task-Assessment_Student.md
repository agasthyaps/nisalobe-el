**Part III**

**Directions:** Listen to your partner’s second recording. Then reflect on and assess their recording by responding to the items below. Refer to your partner’s planner and source text as needed when responding.

**Introduction and Reading Sections** (SL.6.2)

What is the context?

Who is the writer of the excerpt?

What are the key ideas in the excerpt?

**Reflection Section** (RI.6.1, RI.6.6, SL.6.2)

What perspective or point of view toward the experience of the boarding schools is being conveyed in the excerpt? What words from the excerpt helped me determine that point of view?

Why is the excerpt meaningful?

What can we learn from the excerpt?

How does this perspective compare and contrast with the perspective of my own excerpt? Of the module texts?
