# Lesson 7: Gather Evidence and Plan Independent Literary Argument Essay
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students use what they learned during the collaborative writing process to independently plan their argument essay.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, W.6.1a, W.6.1b, W.6.1e, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can gather and evaluate evidence in order to make a claim for a literary argument essay about whether Cal should return to Challagi. (RL.6.3, W.6.1)
- I can plan a literary argument essay about whether Cal should return to Challagi. (W.6.1, W.6.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Gather and Evaluate Evidence – RL.6.3, W.6.1 (10 minutes) → Plan Literary Argument Essay – W.6.1, W.6.5 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
