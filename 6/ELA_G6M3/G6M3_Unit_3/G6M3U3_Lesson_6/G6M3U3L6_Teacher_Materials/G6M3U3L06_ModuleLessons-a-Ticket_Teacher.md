Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 6

W.6.1a, W.6.1e

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Next, provide an answer for the following two statements.

Explain one way you think a conclusion and an introduction for a literary argument essay are different.

Answers will vary, but may include: A conclusion is meant to bring an essay to a close, but an introduction is meant to grab a reader’s attention and make the reader want to continue reading the essay.

Explain one way you think a conclusion and an introduction for a literary argument essay are similar.

Answers will vary, but may include: Both a conclusion and an introduction present the writer’s main claim, which expresses the writer’s position on the argument.
