# Lesson 6: Collaborative Literary Argument Essay: Analyze a Model and Draft Conclusion
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students draft the conclusion of their collaborative essay.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RI.6.1, W.6.1a, W.6.1e, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the characteristics of a strong reflection by analyzing the model. (W.6.1e)
- I can collaboratively write a conclusion with a strong reflection for an essay about whether Cal should leave school to find Pop. (W.6.1e)
- I can provide kind, specific, and helpful feedback to peers. (W.6.1a, W.6.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1a, W.6.1e (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model Conclusion – W.6.1e (10 minutes) → Write a Collaborative Conclusion – W.6.1e (15 minutes) → Peer Review – W.6.1e, W.6.5 (5 minutes)
