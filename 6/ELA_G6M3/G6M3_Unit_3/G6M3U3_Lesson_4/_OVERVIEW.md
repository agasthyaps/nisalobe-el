# Lesson 4: Collaborative Literary Argument Essay: Analyze a Model and Draft Proof Paragraph 1
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students draft the first Proof Paragraph of their collaborative essays.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RI.6.1, RI.6.8, W.6.1a, W.6.1b, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of Proof Paragraph 1 of the model essay. (W.6.1b)
- I can determine the characteristics of an effective support paragraph by analyzing the model. (W.6.1b)
- I can collaboratively write Proof Paragraph 1 for an essay on whether Cal should leave school to find Pop. (W.6.1b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Proof Paragraph 1 – RI.6.8, W.6.1b (20 minutes) → Collaborative Writing: Draft Proof Paragraph 1 – W.6.1b (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
