# Lesson 3: Collaborative Literary Argument Essay: Analyze a Model and Draft an Introduction
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students use their completed writing planner to continue working on their collaborative essay, focusing specifically on drafting the introduction.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RI.6.1, W.6.1a, W.6.1d, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the characteristics of an effective main claim by analyzing the model. (W.6.1a)
- I can collaboratively write an introduction with a strong main claim for an argument essay about whether Cal should leave school to find Pop. (W.6.1a)
- I can provide kind, specific, and helpful feedback to peers. (W.6.1a, W.6.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1d (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Model Introduction – W.6.1a (10 minutes) → Language Dive: Model Essay Claim – W.6.1a (10 minutes) → Collaborative Writing: Draft an Introduction – W.6.1a (15 minutes)
