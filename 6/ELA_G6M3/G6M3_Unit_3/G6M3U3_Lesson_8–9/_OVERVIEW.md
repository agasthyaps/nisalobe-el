# Lessons 8–9: Mid–Unit 3 Assessment: Write a Literary Argument Essay
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lessons 8–9**

**Summary:** Over the course of two lessons, students complete the Mid-Unit 3 assessment for which they independently draft their Literary Argument essays based on their evidence gathering and planning.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.3, W.6.1, W.6.4, W.6.6, W.6.9a, L.6.2, L.6.3, L.6.6.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write a literary argument essay about whether Cal should return to Challagi. (W.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Work Time → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – W.6.1 (15 minutes) → Homework → Preread Performance Task Texts: Students preread the options within the Performance Task Recording: Text List in preparation for choosing a text during the next lesson.
