# Lesson 1: Analyze a Model Literary Argument Essay
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students begin work on the literary argument essay by analyzing a model.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, W.6.1, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find the central idea of a model literary argument essay. (RI.6.2)
- I can determine the purpose of a literary argument essay. (W.6.4)
- I can apply my knowledge of the Painted Essay® to analyze the structure of a model literary argument essay. (W.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model Literary Argument Essay – W.6.1 (15 minutes) → Debrief Literary Argument Writing Checklist – W.6.1 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
