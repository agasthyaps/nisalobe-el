# Lesson 4: Close Read: Analyze Point of View and Unfamiliar Words: “The Land of Red Apples”
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students read another supplemental informational text, a primary source from a student who attended Carlisle Indian boarding school. Students continue to practice RI.6.6by analyzing a different author’s point of view and how it is conveyed through her personal narrative.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.4, RI.6.6, SL.6.1d, L.6.4a, L.6.4c, L.6.4d, L.6.5a, L.6.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can paraphrase the key ideas and demonstrate understanding of the perspective being conveyed in Zitkala-Sa’s text. (SL.6.1d)
- I can analyze Zitkala-Sa’s point of view and how it is conveyed in the text. (RI.6.6)
- I can determine the meaning of figurative and connotative language as it is used in the text. (L.6.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Point of View: “The Land of Red Apples” – SL.6.1d (15 minutes) → Close Read: “The Land of Red Apples” – RI.6.6, L.6.5 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
