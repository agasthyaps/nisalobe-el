# Lesson 14: Analyze Structure and Point of View: *Two Roads*, Chapter 17
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 17 of *Two Roads*. Students continue to analyze Cal’s point of view and how his point of view is developed.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.5, RL.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how chapter 17 fits into the overall structure of the novel and contributes to the development of the plot of *Two Roads*. (RL.6.5)
- I can analyze Cal’s point of view in chapter 17 and how it is developed by the author. (RL.6.1, RL.6.6)
- I can share my independent research reading with my peers. (RL.6.10, RI.6.10)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Two Roads*, Chapter 17 – RL.6.5 (15 minutes) → Analyze Point of View: Cal – RL.6.6 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
