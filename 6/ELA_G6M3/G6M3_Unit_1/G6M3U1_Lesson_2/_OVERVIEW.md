# Lesson 2: Analyze Plot and Character: *Two Roads*, Chapters 4 and 5
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students spend some time discussing their reflections on the module guiding questions. They continue in the anchor text, *Two Roads*, picking up with the story at the end of chapter 4 and begin tracking how the main character, Cal, responds to and changes as a result of plot events.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.3

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find the gist of chapters 4 and 5 of *Two Roads*.
- I can analyze how Cal responds to and changes due to the events in chapters 4 and 5. (RL.6.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Two Roads*, Chapter 4 Excerpt and Chapter 5 – SL.6.1 (25 minutes) → Analyze Character: Cal – RL.6.3 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
