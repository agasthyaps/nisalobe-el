Instructions for Character Window

(For Teacher Reference)

1.  Fold an 8.5" × 11" piece of cardstock or construction paper in half widthwise.

<img src="media/image1.png" style="width:3.50486in;height:2.20208in" />

2.  Turn sideways so the top flap flips up. Students should be able to “open” the window to reveal the blank side underneath by lifting up the top flap.

3.  Draw a simple picture of a window on the top flap.

<img src="media/image1.png" style="width:3.70625in;height:2.20208in" />

4.  Write the model responses on the window side.

5.  “Open” the window to reveal the bottom flap. Write the model response in the blank spot on the bottom flap.
