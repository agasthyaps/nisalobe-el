# Lesson 8: Analyze Point of View: *Two Roads*, Chapter 9
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students return to the anchor text, *Two Roads*, at chapter 9 and continue reading. They begin filling in a note-catcher to guide them in their analysis of point of view and character development.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.6, RI.6.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find the gist of chapter 9 of *Two Roads*.
- I can analyze Pop’s point of view in chapter 9 and how it is developed by the author. (RL.6.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Two Roads*, Chapter 9 – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Analyze Point of View – RL.6.6 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
