# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: American Indian Boarding Schools
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students participate in the Infer the Topic protocol and engage with literary resources, informational texts, and information presented in diverse media related to the topic of the module. Students are also introduced to the module’s guiding questions and performance task.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RI.6.1, SL.6.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from resources. (RL.6.1, RI.6.1, SL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Infer the Topic – RI.6.1, SL.6.2 (10 minutes) → Introduce the Performance Task and Module Guiding Questions (10 minutes) → Launch the Text: *Two Roads*, Chapter 1 Excerpts – RL.6.1 (15 minutes)
