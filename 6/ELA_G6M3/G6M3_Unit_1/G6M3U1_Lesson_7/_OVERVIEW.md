# Lesson 7: Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Point of View and Integrate Information
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students take the Mid-Unit 1 Assessment.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.4, RI.6.6, RI.6.7, L.6.5a, L.6.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze Zitkala-Sa’s point of view and how it is conveyed in the text. (RI.6.6)
- I can determine the meaning of figurative and connotative language as it is used in the text. (L.6.5)
- I can integrate information from historical photographs to better understand “The Cutting of My Long Hair.” (RI.6.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Point of View and Integrate Information (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – RI.6.1 (5 minutes)
