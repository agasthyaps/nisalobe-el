Synopsis: *Two Roads*, Chapter 15

**Name:** **Date:**

**Chapter 15, “Infirmary”**

- Cal enters the infirmary.

- Mrs. Wilting, the nurse, introduces herself in a sharp tone and begins to give Cal instructions.

- The nurse cuts off Cal’s hair with shears. As she does so, Cal reminisces about how his mother taught him to care for his hair.

- Mrs. Wilting inspects Cal’s hair and sees no lice, but she still runs a comb dipped in kerosene through Cal’s short hair.

- Cal also receives several vaccinations.

- Mrs. Wilting sits down and asks Cal questions: name, date of birth, parents’ names, father’s occupation.

- The nurse dismisses Cal and slams the door behind him.
