# Lesson 13: Analyze Structure and Language: *Two Roads*, Chapter 16
**Grade 6: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 16 in *Two Roads* and participate in a Language Dive to build upon their understanding of language varieties and character development.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.3, RL.6.5, L.6.1b, L.6.1e

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how chapter 16 fits into the overall structure of the novel and contributes to the development of the setting and plot of *Two Roads*. (RL.6.5)
- I can analyze the impact of the author’s use of language variety. (L.6.1e)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.1b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Two Roads*, Chapter 16 – RL.6.5 (20 minutes) → Language Dive: *Two Roads*, Page 167 – RL.6.3, L.6.1e (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
