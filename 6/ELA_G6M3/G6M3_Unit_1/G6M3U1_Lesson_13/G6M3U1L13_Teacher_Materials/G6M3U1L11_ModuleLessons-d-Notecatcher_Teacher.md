Part II

How does Possum’s language change depending on whom he is talking to and what he is talking about?

Answers will vary, but may include: Possum’s speech is considerably different with Superintendent Morrell than with Cal. When speaking to Superintendent Morrell, Possum is straightforward and uses shorter sentences. Possum’s speech with Cal is more relaxed and joking. Possum uses longer sentences and friendly words when speaking with Cal.

Part III

What can the reader infer about Possum based on his language variety?

Answers will vary, but may include: Possum is careful to speak in a respectful manner to adults of authority. Possum is comfortable speaking his mind when with his friends. Possum understands that he cannot express himself as openly with adults of authority as he does with his friends.

Source: Bruchac, Joseph. *Two Roads*. Penguin Young Readers, 2018.
