Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 13

L.6.1b

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Then, answer each of the following questions.

Rewrite this sentence properly using an intensive pronoun:

**Possum used a phrase that was the sort of thing Pop would say.**

Answers will vary, but may include: Possum himself used a phrase that was the sort of thing Pop would say.

Rewrite the following sentence properly using an intensive pronoun:

**Possum made the treasure box.**

Answers will vary, but may include: Possum made the treasure box himself.
