# Lesson 8: End of Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Argument and Point of View: “An Argument against the Moon Mission”
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** In the second half of this unit, students read excerpts of informational texts and primary source documents, building background knowledge about the Space Race and analyzing the author’s point of view and use of connotative and figurative language. This lesson continues those routines in an assessme

**Learning Targets:**
- I can trace and evaluate the argument in the text “An Argument against the Moon Mission.” (RI.6.8)
- I can analyze an author’s point of view and how it is conveyed in the text “An Argument against the Moon Mission.” (RI.6.6)
