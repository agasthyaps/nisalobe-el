# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: Remarkable Accomplishments in Space Science
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students participate in the Infer the Topic protocol, using evidence from informational texts (e.g., excerpts from historical documents) to draw inferences about the topic of the module. They are also introduced to the module guiding questions and the parameters of the performance task.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.4, SL.6.2, L.6.4, L.6.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from resources. (RI.6.1, SL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Infer the Topic – RI.6.1, SL.6.2 (15 minutes) → Introduce the Performance Task (10 minutes) → Review the Module Guiding Questions – L.6.4 (10 minutes)
