Directions for Infer the Topic

RI.6.1, SL.6.2

**Name:** **Date:**

1.  With your partner, choose a starting resource. Take your I Notice/I Wonder note-catcher with you.

2.  Look carefully at the resource. Be respectful of others who might be looking at the same thing. Record what you notice (what you see) and what you wonder (questions you have) on your note-catcher. (3 minutes)

3.  Share your notices and wonders with your partner. Explain what you think you are going to be learning about in this module. (2 minutes)

4.  With your partner, choose a new resource, and repeat steps 2 and 3 until time is called.
