Questions We Can Ask during  
a Language Dive Anchor Chart

(Example for Teacher Reference)

<table>
<colgroup>
<col style="width: 100%" />
</colgroup>
<thead>
<tr class="header">
<th>Questions about the Sentence</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>What is the meaning of this sentence? Why do we think that?</p></li>
<li><p>How does the sentence add to our understanding of the guiding question?</p></li>
<li><p>How can we say this sentence in our own words?</p></li>
<li><p>Can we divide this sentence into two or more sentences? What do we have to remove or change?</p></li>
<li><p>Can we say this sentence in a different order? How?</p></li>
<li><p>What other questions can we ask that will help us understand this sentence?</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>Questions about the Chunks</td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>Who or what is this chunk about? What is the person or thing doing in this chunk?</p></li>
<li><p>What does this chunk tell us? Why do we think that?</p></li>
<li><p>How can we say this chunk in our own words?</p></li>
<li><p>Can we act out the chunk? Can we sketch the chunk?</p></li>
<li><p>Why did the author write this chunk?</p></li>
<li><p>What if we remove _____ in this chunk? Does it still make sense? Why do we think that?</p></li>
<li><p>What if we replace _____ in this chunk with _____? How does that change the meaning?</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>Questions about Words</td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>What do we think this word means?</p></li>
<li><p>What is the translation of this word?</p></li>
<li><p>Is there a familiar word or affix in this word? How does that help us understand the unfamiliar word?</p></li>
<li><p>What are some synonyms of this word? How are they different in meaning?</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td>Questions about the Language Dive</td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><ul>
<li><p>How can we use this sentence structure in our speaking and writing?</p></li>
<li><p>Does the language in this sentence remind you of the language or chunks in other Language Dives? How?</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
