**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students participate in a Language Dive to analyze a sentence from the conclusion of “This Is How the Space Race Changed the Great Power Rivalry Forever.” This sentence reiterates a main claim and central idea of the text.
