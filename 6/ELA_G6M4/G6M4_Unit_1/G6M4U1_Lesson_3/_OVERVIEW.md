# Lesson 3: Analyze Point of View: *Team Moon*
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** This lesson features a text that describes the final events before the Apollo 11 launch. Students engage with the text using a variation of the Annotating Text protocol, which students first experienced in Lesson 2. **Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.4, RI.6.6, RI.6.8, L.6.4, L.6.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the author’s point of view and purpose and how they are conveyed in the *Team Moon* text. (RI.6.6, L.6.5)
- I can determine an author’s claim made in an excerpt of *Team Moon*. (RI.6.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Team Moon* Excerpt – RI.6.6 (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *Team Moon*, Paragraph 4 – RI.6.6, L.6.5 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
