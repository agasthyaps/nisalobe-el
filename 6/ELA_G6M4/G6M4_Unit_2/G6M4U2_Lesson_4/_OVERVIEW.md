# Lesson 4: Author’s Point of View and Use of Pronouns: *Hidden Figures*
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students participate in a Language Dive to analyze a sentence from chapter 6 of *Hidden Figures (Young Readers’ Edition)*. They build on pronoun work from Module 3 by analyzing the author’s point of view toward the hidden figures and considering how Shetterly conveys her point of view.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.4, RI.6.6, L.6.1d, L.6.4a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can find and correct a vague pronoun that has an unclear antecedent. (L.6.1d)
- I can determine the author’s point of view toward the hidden figures and how it’s conveyed in chapter 6. (RI.6.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.1d (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Hidden Figures*, Chapter 6 (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *Hidden Figures*, Page 46 – L.6.1d (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
