# Lesson 6: Analyze Point of View and Habits of Character
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students continue to practice the relevant skills of argument analysis and point of view before the Mid-Unit 2 Assessment in Lesson 7. For the assessment, they identify the author’s claims about Dorothy, evidence and reasoning to support those claims, and determine the author’s point of view toward 

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.6, RI.6.8, W.6.1b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the author’s point of view toward Dorothy Vaughan in *Hidden Figures*. (RI.6.6)
- I can use evidence and reasoning to support a claim about Dorothy Vaughan. (W.6.1b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.8 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Hidden Figures*, Chapter 8 (10 minutes) → Chalk Talk: Habits of Character – RI.6.6 (15 minutes) → Write a Claim: Vaughan’s Habits of Character – W.6.1b (10 minutes)
