<td><p>Differences</p>
<p><strong>• Reeher’s point of view toward Dorothy seems to be that, above all, she was committed to her family and community (“She said her mom was much more than an accomplished mathematician”; “‘She wanted us to do the best we could and be the best we could be’”; “‘She always made my clothes and my sisters’ clothes’”).</strong></p></td>
