# Lesson 14: Research Focus Figure
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** Students read two more chapters in *Hidden Figures* and, as the text is coming to a close, begin to synthesize their learning by revisiting the module guiding questions. The rest of the lesson allows time for students to begin their initial research on their focus figure in preparation for the indep

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, W.6.1, W.6.7, W.6.8, W.6.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can gather evidence and develop reasoning for an argument essay about Katherine Johnson’s or Mary Jackson’s remarkable accomplishments. (W.6.1)
- I can conduct a short research project, draw on several sources, and gather relevant information to narrow down my focus figure options. (W.6.7, W.6.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.7, W.6.10 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Gather Evidence: *Hidden Figures*, Chapters 20 and 21 – W.6.1b (20 minutes) → Research Focus Figure – W.6.7, W.6.8 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
