# Lesson 2: Analyze Word Definitions: *Hidden Figures*, Chapters 2–3
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 2 and an excerpt of chapter 3 from *Hidden Figures (Young Readers’ Edition)*, continuing their gist work. They participate in an activity to determine the connotative and figurative meanings of multiple-meaning words as they are used in the text.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.4, L.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the figurative, connotative, and technical meanings of multiple-meaning words as they are used in the text. (RI.6.4, L.6.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Hidden Figures*, Chapters 2–3 (20 minutes) → Analyze Word Definitions: *Hidden Figures*, Chapters 2–3 – RI.6.4, L.6.4 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
