# Lesson 1: Introduce *Hidden Figures (Young Readers’ Edition)*
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students begin their reading of the anchor text, *Hidden Figures (Young Readers’ Edition)*, and participate in a Language Dive that focuses around a sentence from the prologue. This sentence allows students to practice analyzing the author’s point of view, determining the meaning of unfamiliar words

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.4, RI.6.6, L.6.1d, L.6.2a, L.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the author’s purpose and point of view and how it is conveyed in the prologue and chapter 1 of *Hidden Figures*. (RI.6.6)
- I can analyze the connotative meanings of words as they are used in *Hidden Figures*. (RI.6.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.10 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Hidden Figures*, Prologue and Chapter 1 – RI.6.6 (10 minutes) → Back-to-Back and Face-to-Face – RI.6.6 (10 minutes) → Language Dive: *Hidden Figures*, Pages 2–3 – RI.6.4, RI.6.6 (15 minutes)
