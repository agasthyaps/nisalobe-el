# Lesson 16: End of Unit 2 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Presentations of Events: *Hidden Figures* and “Katherine Johnson: A Lifetime of STEM”
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 16**

**Summary:** Students complete the End of Unit 2 Assessment, for which they compare and contrast two texts that describe similar events in Katherine Johnson’s life, focusing on similarities and differences in the content conveyed, the authors’ methods used to convey that content, and points of view.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare and contrast two authors’ presentation of events in terms of content, author methods, and point of view. (RI.6.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.10 (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 2 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Presentations of Events: *Hidden Figures* and “Katherine Johnson: A Lifetime of STEM” (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – RI.6.10 (5 minutes)
