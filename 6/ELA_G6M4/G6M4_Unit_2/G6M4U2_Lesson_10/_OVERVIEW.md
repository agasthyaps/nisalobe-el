# Lesson 10: Compare and Contrast Authors’ Presentations of Events: Mary Jackson
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** Students continue to develop their knowledge of hidden figure Mary Jackson. They read a new passage about Mary from the anchor text, which outlines an important event in Mary’s life. Then, they read a supplemental text that describes the same event, but from a different author’s perspective. In prep

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, RI.6.6, RI.6.9, W.6.1, W.6.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can gather evidence and develop reasoning for an argument essay about Mary Jackson’s remarkable accomplishments. (W.6.1)
- I can compare and contrast two authors’ presentations of events in Mary Jackson’s life in terms of content, author methods, and point of view. (RI.6.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Compare and Contrast Content and Authors’ Methods – RI.6.3, RI.6.9 (20 minutes) → Compare and Contrast Authors’ Points of View – RI.6.6, RI.6.9 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
