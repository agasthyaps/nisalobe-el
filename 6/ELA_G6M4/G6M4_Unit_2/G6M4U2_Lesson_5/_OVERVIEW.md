# Lesson 5: Participate in Collaborative Discussion: Module Guiding Questions
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students synthesize their understanding of the main ideas of “Moon Dust and Black Disgust” and chapters 4–6 of *Hidden Figures (Young Readers’ Edition)* in a collaborative discussion. They then read chapter 7 of *Hidden Figures*.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.8, SL.6.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can paraphrase and reflect on a module guiding question during a collaborative discussion. (SL.6.1)
- I can trace the claim, reasons, and evidence in an excerpt of *Hidden Figures*. (RI.6.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.10 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Debrief Module Guiding Questions – SL.6.1 (15 minutes) → Read *Hidden Figures*, Chapter 7 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
