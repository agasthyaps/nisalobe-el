# Lesson 11: Discover a Hidden Figure: Katherine Johnson
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students “discover” a new hidden figure: Katherine Johnson. They read a new chapter that introduces Katherine, and they participate in a Language Dive to discuss the way in which Margot Lee Shetterly characterizes Katherine in chapter 12 of the text.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, RI.6.9, W.6.1, W.6.9b, L.6.2a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the way in which an author introduces and elaborates on Katherine Johnson’s character in the text. (RI.6.3)
- I can gather evidence and develop reasoning for an argument essay about Katherine Johnson’s remarkable accomplishments. (W.6.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.9 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Hidden Figures*, Chapter 12 – RI.6.3 (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *Hidden Figures*, Page 96 – RI.6.3, L.6.2a (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
