# Lesson 15: Compare and Contrast Authors’ Presentations of Events: The Moon Landing
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 15**

**Summary:** Students continue the research on their focus figure. Students will also read the final chapter of the anchor text and compare two authors’ presentations of the moon landing.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare and contrast two authors’ presentations of the moon landing in terms of content, author methods, and point of view. (RI.6.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.3 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Content, Author’s Methods, and Point of View: *Hidden Figures*, Chapter 23 – RI.6.3, RI.6.6 (10 minutes) → Analyze Content, Author’s Methods, and Point of View: “July 20, 1969: One Giant Leap for Mankind” – RI.6.3, RI.6.6 (10 minutes) → Compare and Contrast Presentations of Events – RI.6.9 (10 minutes)
