# Lesson 3: Write Collaborative Argument Essay: Introduction and Proof Paragraph 1
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students review the structure of an argument essay’s introductory paragraph, analyze an argument essay’s first Proof Paragraph, and draft the introduction and Proof Paragraph 1 of their collaborative argument essay.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, RI.6.8, W.6.1, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9b, L.6.2, L.6.3, L.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of a model argument essay Proof Paragraph. (RI.6.8, W.6.1b)
- I can introduce a claim and support that claim with clear reasons and relevant evidence drawn from informational texts. (RI.6.1, W.6.1a, W.6.1b, W.6.9b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1a, W.6.1.d (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Proof Paragraph 1 – RI.6.8, W.6.1b (10 minutes) → Write a Collaborative Introduction and Proof Paragraph 1 – W.6.1a, W.6.1b (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
