# Lesson 5: Present Collaborative Argument Essay: Delineate and Evaluate an Argument
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, partners pair up with a group who wrote their essay on a different individual to delineate the arguments and specific claims presented by their classmates.

**Focus Standards:** SL.6.1d, SL.6.3, SL.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can present my argument about why Mary’s or Katherine’s accomplishments were remarkable. (W.6.1, SL.6.4)
- I can delineate my peers’ arguments about why Mary’s or Katherine’s accomplishments were remarkable. (SL.6.3)
- I can paraphrase and reflect on our multiple perspectives about Mary’s and Katherine’s accomplishments. (SL.6.1d)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.8 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Present and Delineate Argument – SL.6.3, SL.6.4 (20 minutes) → Share Peer Feedback – SL.6.4 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
