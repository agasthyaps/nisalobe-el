# Lesson 7: Plan Independent Argument Essay
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students use what they learned during the collaborative writing process to independently complete their evidence gathering and then plan their argument essay based on their research about their focus figure.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, W.6.1, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write a claim based on reasons and relevant evidence from my research. (W.6.1a)
- I can plan an argument essay about why my focus figure’s accomplishments are remarkable. (W.6.1, W.6.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1a (10 minutes) → Work Time → Plan Argument Essay – W.6.1, W.6.5 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Debrief: Argument Writing Checklist – W.6.1c, W.6.8 (5 minutes)
