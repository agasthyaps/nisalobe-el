Argument Writing Checklist

|-------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| W.6.9, W.6.1b           | My claim is supported by reasons and evidence from credible sources and shows a solid understanding of the topic or text. | **The claim includes the name of my assigned figure and states why her accomplishments are remarkable.**                                                                                      |                |
| W.6.1a                  | I state my claim clearly, and my writing stays focused.                                                                   | **The claim includes two specific points to support the claim. The points come from the anchor text, research sources, or other supplementary texts we read in class.**                       |                |
| W.6.1a                  | I have an introduction that gives readers the context they need to understand the topic or text.                          | **I introduce any background information critical to my reader’s understanding, such as her biographical details or the political and social context of the time when my figure was living.** |                |
| W.6.1e                  | I have a conclusion that is clearly related to my argument and restates my claim.                                         | **My conclusion restates my main claim and includes my own thinking about why my figure’s accomplishments are remarkable.**                                                                   |                |
| W.6.1a                  | My reasons and evidence are organized in a way that makes my argument clear and easy to follow.                           | **Each point is described in its own Proof Paragraph. The evidence included in each paragraph connects to the point or reason stated.**                                                       |                |
| W.6.1c                  | I use words and phrases to show that my reasons are connected to my claim.                                                | **I use transitions from one idea to the next and in between evidence about the figure’s accomplishments and reasoning for why they are remarkable.**                                         |                |

| W.6.4 (partial), W.6.1d | I use a formal style.                                                                                                     | **I remove slang and conversational language to sound more serious and thoughtful**.                                                                                                          |                |

| L.6.1                   | My words and sentences follow the rules of writing.                                                                       | **I have checked my work for grammatical errors. My writing is smooth and easy to understand.**                                                                                               |                |

I have used resources, such as a dictionary or an online spellchecker, to polish my work.**                                     |                |
