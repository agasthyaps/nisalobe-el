# Lesson 4: Write Collaborative Argument Essay: Proof Paragraph 2 and Conclusion
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students have been analyzing a model argument essay and have used their analysis to draft the introduction and first Proof Paragraph of a collaborative argument essay. This lesson continues those routines to guide students in collaboratively drafting their second Proof Paragraphs and their conclusio

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, RI.6.8, W.6.1b, W.6.1c, W.6.1d, W.6.1e, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9b, L.6.2, L.6.3, L.6.4b, L.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of a model argument essay conclusion paragraph. (W.6.1e)
- I can support a claim with clear reasons and relevant evidence drawn from informational texts. (RI.6.1, W.6.1.b, W.6.9b)
- I can provide a concluding section that follows from my argument. (W.6.1e)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.6.8, W.6.1b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: Model Essay Conclusion – W.6.1e (10 minutes) → Write a Collaborative Proof Paragraph 2 and Conclusion – W.6.1b, W.6.1e (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
