# Lesson 1: Analyze a Model Argument Essay
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students have worked with the Painted Essay® structure in the previous modules. In this unit, they use the same structure but evaluate how it changes when applied to writing an argument, similar to their work in Module 3 with literary argument writing. Students began research on their focus figure a

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, W.6.1, W.6.7, W.6.8, W.6.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of a model argument essay by applying my knowledge of the Painted Essay®. (W.6.1)
- I can conduct a short research project, draw on several sources, and gather relevant information on my focus figure. (W.6.7, W.6.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.1 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model Argument Essay – W.6.1 (15 minutes) → Research Focus Figure – W.6.7, W.6.8 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
