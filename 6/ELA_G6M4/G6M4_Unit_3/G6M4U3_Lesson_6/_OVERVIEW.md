# Lesson 6: Research Focus Figure
**Grade 6: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to conduct research about their chosen focus figures in preparation for planning and composing their independent argument essays over three lessons.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.3, W.6.1, W.6.7, W.6.8, W.6.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can conduct a short research project, draw on several sources, and gather relevant information about my focus figure. (W.6.7, W.6.8)
- I can share my independent research reading with my peers. (RL.6.10, RI.6.10)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.8 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Research Focus Figure – W.6.7, W.6.8 (20 minutes) → Debrief: Focus Figure Research – W.6.7 (5 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
