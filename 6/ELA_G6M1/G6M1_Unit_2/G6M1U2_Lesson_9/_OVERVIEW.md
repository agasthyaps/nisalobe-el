# Lesson 9: Compare and Contrast Essay: Plan Proof Paragraph 1
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 17 of *The Lightning Thief* and begin planning the first paragraph of their compare and contrast essay.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.7, W.6.2a, W.6.2b, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 17 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can compare and contrast what I see and hear when I read the text to what I perceive when I watch the same scene of the film. (RL.6.7)
- I can plan the first Proof Paragraph of a compare and contrast essay. (W.6.2b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.2a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 17 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Plan Proof Paragraph 1 – W.6.2b (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
