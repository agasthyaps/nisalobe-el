# Lesson 7: Compare and Contrast Film and Text: *The Lightning Thief*
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 16 of *The Lightning Thief* and engage in a deeper comparison of the film version of the novel.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.4, RL.6.7, W.6.5, W.6.9a, L.6.4a, L.6.4d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 16 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can compare and contrast what I see and hear when I read the text to what I perceive when I watch the same scene of the film. (RL.6.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 16 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Compare and Contrast Text and Film Scene – RL.6.7 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
