# Lesson 4: Compare and Contrast Themes: Greek Myths and *The Lightning Thief*
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 15 of *The Lightning Thief*, making connections between the themes of the novel and the Greek myths they have been studying.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.9

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 15 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can compare and contrast the approach to similar themes in *The Lightning Thief* and Greek myths. (RL.6.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 15 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Compare and Contrast Themes: Greek Myths and *The Lightning Thief* – RL.6.9 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
