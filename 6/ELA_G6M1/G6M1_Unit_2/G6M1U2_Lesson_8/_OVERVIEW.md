# Lesson 8: Compare and Contrast Essay: Plan Introduction
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students engage in a Language Dive to better understand the structure of a focus statement. They also begin planning the introduction to their compare and contrast essay.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.7, W.6.2a, W.6.2b, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare and contrast what I see and hear when I read the text to what I perceive when I watch the same scene of the film. (RL.6.7)
- I can plan the introduction of a compare and contrast essay with a strong focus statement. (W.6.2a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Compare and Contrast Text and Film Scene – RL.6.7 (5 minutes) → Language Dive: Compare and Contrast Model Essay Focus Statement – W.6.2a (10 minutes) → Plan an Introduction – W.6.2a (20 minutes)
