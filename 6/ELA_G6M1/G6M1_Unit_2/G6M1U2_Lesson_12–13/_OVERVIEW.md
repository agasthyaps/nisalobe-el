# Lessons 12–13: End of Unit 2 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Film and Text: *The Lightning Thief*
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lessons 12–13**

**Summary:** Students complete the End of Unit 2 Assessment by synthesizing their draft paragraphs into a cohesive essay.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.6, W.6.9a, L.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write an essay in which I compare and contrast what I see and hear when I read the text to what I perceive when I watch the same scene of the film. (RL.6.1, RL.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.6, W.6.9a, W.6.10, L.6.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return Mid-Unit 2 Assessments (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 2 Assessment: Write Compare and Contrast Essay (65 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
