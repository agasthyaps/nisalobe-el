# Lesson 1: Determine Theme and Summarize: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 13
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 13 of *The Lightning Thief* before practicing text-dependent questions on the themes present in the novel. Students analyze a model summary to generate criteria for an effective summary.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.4, L.6.4a, L.6.4b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 13 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can determine a theme and how it is conveyed through details in chapter 13 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.2)
- I can analyze a model to identify characteristics of an effective summary. (RL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 13 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (15 minutes) → Analyze Theme: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 13 – RL.6.2 (10 minutes) → Analyze a Model Summary – RL.6.2 (10 minutes)
