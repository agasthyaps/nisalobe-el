Synopsis: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 13

**Name:** **Date:**

- Annabeth, Grover, and Percy ride the train west. During a layover in St. Louis, they get off to see the Gateway Arch and ride to the top.

- As Percy is preparing to leave the observation deck of the Gateway Arch, he is attacked by Echidna, the Mother of Monsters, and a chimera.

- The chimera succeeds in disarming Percy and poisoning Percy with a bite to the calf.

- Percy asks Poseidon for help, jumps off the Gateway Arch, and plummets down toward the Mississippi River.
