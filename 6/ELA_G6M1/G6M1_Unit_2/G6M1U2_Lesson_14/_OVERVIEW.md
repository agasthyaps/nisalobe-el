# Lesson 14: End of Unit 2 Assessment: Revise Compare and Contrast Essay
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** Students engage in a Language Dive to better understand transition phrases. They also complete a peer critique, giving and receiving kind and constructive feedback.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.6, W.6.9a, W.6.10, L.6.2b, L.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can provide kind, specific, and helpful feedback to peers. (W.6.5)
- I can use feedback to revise my essay. (W.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: Appropriate Transitions – W.6.2c (10 minutes) → Introduce Peer Critique Protocol – W.6.5 (10 minutes) → Peer Critique – W.6.5 (10 minutes)
