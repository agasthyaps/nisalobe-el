# Lesson 6: Compare and Contrast Essay: Analyze a Model
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students begin composing an essay comparing and contrasting The Lightning Thief to clips from the film version. Students apply the Painted Essay® structure to a model essay.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.7, W.6.2, W.6.4, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the purpose of a model essay. (W.6.2)
- I can compare and contrast the experience of reading a scene in a novel to viewing a film version of the same scene. (RL.6.7)
- I can use the Painted Essay® structure to analyze a model. (W.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.10 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read a Model Painted Essay® – W.6.2 (5 minutes) → View Clip from Film Version of *The Lightning Thief* – RL.6.7 (10 minutes) → Analyze a Model Painted Essay® – W.6.2 (20 minutes)
