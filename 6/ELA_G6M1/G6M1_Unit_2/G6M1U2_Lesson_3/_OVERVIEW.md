# Lesson 3: Close Reading Jigsaw: “Cronus” and “Medusa”
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students dive into additional Greek myths referenced in the novel and determine their themes. Students practice collaboration by working with multiple partners to share their findings.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write a summary of “Theseus and the Minotaur” without personal opinions or judgments. (RL.6.2)
- I can determine themes in “Cronus” and “Medusa” and how they are conveyed through details. (RL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Review Summary Writing – RL.6.2 (10 minutes) → Jigsaw Close Read: “Cronus” and “Medusa” – RL.6.2 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
