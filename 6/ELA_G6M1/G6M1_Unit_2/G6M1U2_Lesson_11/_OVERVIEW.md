# Lesson 11: Compare and Contrast Essay: Plan Conclusion
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students engage in a Language Dive to better understand the structure of a concluding paragraph before beginning to compose their own.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.7, W.6.2a, W.6.2f, W.6.4, W.6.5, W.6.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare and contrast what I see and hear when I read the text to what I perceive when I watch the same scene of the film. (RL.6.7)
- I can plan the conclusion of a compare and contrast essay with a strong reflection. (W.6.2f)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.2a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: Reflection – W.6.2f (15 minutes) → Plan a Conclusion – W.6.2f (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
