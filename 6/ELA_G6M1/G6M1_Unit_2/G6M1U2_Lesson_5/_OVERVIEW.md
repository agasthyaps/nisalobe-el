# Lesson 5: Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Themes in Greek Myths and *The Lightning Thief*
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students complete the Mid-Unit 2 Assessment, in which they analyze themes in a Greek myth and *The Lightning Thief*.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.4, RL.6.9, L.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare and contrast the approach to similar themes in *The Lightning Thief* and “Prometheus.” (RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.9, W.6.9a, W.6.10)
- I can independently read, understand, and explain the meaning of a new text. (RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.4, RL.6.10, L.6.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return End of Unit 1 Assessments (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Themes in Literature – RL.6.9 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
