# Lessons 10–11: Prepare for the Performance Task
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 3: Lessons 10–11**

**Summary:** In the final lessons of this module, students create a multimedia presentation explaining their narrative choices: choice of Greek god (or a character from another traditional story), new character choices, choice of scene from *The Lightning Thief* to revise, and how and why they revised that scene

**Focus Standards:** SL.6.4, SL.6.5, L.6.3a

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Review Performance Task – SL.6.4 (15 minutes) → Prepare Presentations – SL.6.5 (60 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
