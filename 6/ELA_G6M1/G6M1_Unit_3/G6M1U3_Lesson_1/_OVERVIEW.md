# Lesson 1: Introduce the Performance Task
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 19 of *The Lightning Thief*, then learn the details of the performance task by analyzing a model. Students share their learning from the independent research reading assignment.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.4, SL.6.4, SL.6.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 19 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can analyze a model to identify characteristics of an effective slideshow presentation. (SL.6.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 19 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Analyze Performance Task Model – SL.6.5 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
