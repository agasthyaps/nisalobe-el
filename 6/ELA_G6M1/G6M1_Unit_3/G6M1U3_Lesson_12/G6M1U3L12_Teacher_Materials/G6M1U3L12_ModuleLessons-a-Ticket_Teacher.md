Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 12

(For Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** What is one word that comes to your mind about Greek mythology, now that we’re about to finish Module 1? Explain your thinking for choosing that word.

Answers will vary. Sample student response: Relevant. I realized that references to Greek myths are everywhere—in architecture, in names, in texts. Learning the origin of these references shows the far-reaching influence of Greek mythology.
