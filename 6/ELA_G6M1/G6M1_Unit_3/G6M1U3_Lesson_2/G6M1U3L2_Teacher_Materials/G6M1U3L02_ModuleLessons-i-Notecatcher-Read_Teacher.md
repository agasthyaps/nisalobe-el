Use the notes in the right-hand column to help you to explain the author’s main points accurately and concisely. After you have finished orally sharing your summary, write it in the space below.

Sample Model Summary

“Hestia” by E.M. Berens begins by explaining that the Greek goddess Hestia was the goddess of fire in the home. The text conveys the central idea that Hestia was important to the ancient Greeks because of the significance of fire. It explains how the fire was built in the center of the home and was so sacred that family members would gather around it to pray. The text concludes by explaining that Hestia was so important, the hearth in the common hall where government officials gathered was named after her.
