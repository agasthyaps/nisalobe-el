# Lesson 4: Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: “Helios”
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students complete the mid-unit assessment, in which they practice determining a central idea of a text and writing a summary, as they have done in previous lessons.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.4, L.6.4a, L.6.4c, L.6.4d, L.6.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can independently read, understand, and explain the meaning of a new text. (RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.4, RI.6.10, L.6.4a, L.6.4c, L.6.4d, L.6.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return End of Unit 2 Assessments (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: “Helios” (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress (15 minutes)
