# Lesson 6: Plan a Narrative: Character
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** Students begin planning their narratives, developing a character profile for their original character, a child of the Greek figure they researched in Lesson 3. They will also begin zeroing in on the scene in which they want to insert this character.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, W.6.3a, W.6.3e, W.6.4, W.6.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 21 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can create a character profile for my new character. (W.6.3a)
- I can choose an appropriate action scene for my character to join in *The Lightning Thief*. (W.6.3e)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 21 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Plan Character – W.6.3 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
