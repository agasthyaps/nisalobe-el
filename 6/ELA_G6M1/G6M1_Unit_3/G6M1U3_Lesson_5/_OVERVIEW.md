# Lesson 5: Plan a Narrative: Analyze a Model
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 20 of *The Lightning Thief*. They begin preparing for the end of unit assessment by analyzing a model narrative and how it differs from the original text.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.3, W.6.3, W.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 20 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can analyze a model to identify characteristics of an effective narrative. (W.6.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 20 Excerpt – RL6.1 (20 minutes) → Analyze a Model – W.6.3 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
