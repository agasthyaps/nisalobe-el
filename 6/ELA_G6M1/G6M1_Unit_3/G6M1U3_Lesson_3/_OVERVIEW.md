# Lesson 3: Research a Greek God
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students conduct research to find out more about a Greek god (or figure from a traditional story) on which to base their new character for their rewritten narrative scene.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.7, W.6.7, W.6.8, W.6.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can research my Greek god or goddess, gathering needed information for my project from informational texts. (W.6.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Research a Greek God – RL.6.7 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Pair Share – W.6.5 (10 minutes)
