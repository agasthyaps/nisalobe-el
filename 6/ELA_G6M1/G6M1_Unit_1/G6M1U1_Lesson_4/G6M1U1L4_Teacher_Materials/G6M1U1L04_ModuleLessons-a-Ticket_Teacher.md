Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 4

RL.6.4, L.6.4a

(For Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the learning targets. Next, read the excerpt and answer both questions.

“\[Gabe\] just kept on collecting paychecks, spending money on cigars that made me nauseous, and on beer, of course . . . Whenever I was home, he expected me to provide his gambling **funds** . . . ‘I don’t have any cash,’ I told him.” (31)

What does **funds** mean?

Answers may vary. Sample student response: money.

What strategy did you use to determine the meaning of this word? Explain your thinking.

Answers may vary. Sample student response: context clues. The words *cash* and *money* gave the context for *funds*.
