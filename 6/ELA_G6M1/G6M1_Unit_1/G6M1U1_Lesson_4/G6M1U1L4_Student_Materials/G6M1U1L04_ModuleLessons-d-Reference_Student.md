Synopsis: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 3

**Name:** **Date:**

- Percy goes home and is confronted by his stepfather, Gabe Ugliano, a dirty, lazy man who frequently derides Percy and verbally abuses Percy’s mother.

- Percy’s mother, Sally Jackson, arrives home. She and Percy talk about Percy’s tumultuous school year.

- Sally Jackson and Percy retreat to Montauk—their favorite place to spend time together.

- Percy and Sally build a fire and talk about Percy’s dad, a mysterious sailor who left before Percy was born. Sally explains that she must send Percy away to a special summer camp.

- When Percy goes to sleep he has a vivid dream—a white horse and golden eagle are fighting each other on a stormy beach.

- Percy wakes up to a raging storm and to someone knocking insistently on the cabin door.

- Sally Jackson opens the door, and there stands Grover. Grover and Sally converse. Sally urgently asks Percy to tell her what happened at school.

- Percy is shocked because, instead of feet, Grover has cloven hooves.
