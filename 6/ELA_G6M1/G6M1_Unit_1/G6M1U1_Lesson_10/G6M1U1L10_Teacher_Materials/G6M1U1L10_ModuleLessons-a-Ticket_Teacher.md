1.  ‘’the camper snorted” (109)

3.  “an enormous shadow with teeth” (124)

4.  “i guess i did okay” (109)

Which of the above are sentences (ignore the absence of capitalization and punctuation)? Explain your thinking.

A and D are complete sentences because they contain both a subject and a predicate.
