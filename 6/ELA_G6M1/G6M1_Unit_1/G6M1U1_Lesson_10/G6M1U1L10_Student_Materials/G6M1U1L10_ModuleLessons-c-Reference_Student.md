Synopsis: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 8

**Name:** **Date:**

- Percy begins to settle into a routine at the camp.

- Percy has his first sword-fighting lesson with Luke and, surprisingly, succeeds at a disarming maneuver.

- Grover and Percy sit near the lake, where they have a conversation about the cabin and the gods they represent.

- Grover tells Percy about the agreement between Zeus, Poseidon, and Hades to not sire any more children after World War II.

- During Capture the Flag, Percy is assigned border patrol. Some campers from the Ares cabin decide to take revenge on Percy for the bathroom incident.

- While the Ares cabin is distracted attacking Percy, Team Athena captures the flag.

- Suddenly, a hellhound appears and jumps on Percy. Chiron gets rid of the monster by shooting it with arrows.

- Annabeth tells Percy to step into the creek. Percy does not argue. His wounds heal.

- Poseidon’s trident appears above Percy’s head. Poseidon claims Percy as his son.
