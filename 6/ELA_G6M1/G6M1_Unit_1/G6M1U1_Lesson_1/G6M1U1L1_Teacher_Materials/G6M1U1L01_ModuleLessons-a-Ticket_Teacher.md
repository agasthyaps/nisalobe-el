**Directions:** As you enter class, write continuously to answer the following prompt:

Why do we have learning targets? What is the purpose of learning targets?

Answers will vary. Possible response: We have learning targets because they give us a goal. The goal is to be able to say “I can . . .,” which means has been that the target achieved.
