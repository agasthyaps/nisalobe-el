<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>1</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Accidentally Vaporize My Pre-Algebra Teacher”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy is “troubled,” dyslexic, and a demigod.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy’s math teacher turns into a winged monster and attacks him.</p></li>
<li><p>His Latin teacher and a friend who saw the attack act as if nothing happened.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>2</strong></p>
<p><strong>“Three Old Ladies Knit the Socks of Death”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy hears Mr. Brunner and Grover talking about him in secret.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy is expelled from Yancy Academy.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy sees three old ladies snip a cord of yarn.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>3</strong></p>
<p><strong>“Grover Unexpectedly Loses His Pants”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy’s mother, Sally, is warm and loving.</p></li>
<li><p>Gabe is smelly, rude, and selfish.</p></li>
<li><p>Sally mentions a mysterious summer camp.</p></li>
<li><p>Grover shows up unexpectedly to rush them to safety.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>4</strong></p>
<p><strong>“My Mother Teaches Me Bullfighting”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy is attacked by a minotaur as he arrives at Camp Half-Blood.</p></li>
<li><p>His mother appears to have died, and Percy is severely injured.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>5</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Play Pinochle with a Horse”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy learns the truth about his heritage as a demigod.</p></li>
<li><p>At Camp Half-Blood, Percy learns that Mr. Brunner, or Chiron, is a centaur. He is also introduced to the camp director, Dionysus, and Annabeth, a fellow demigod.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>6</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Become Supreme Lord of the Bathroom”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy gets a tour of Camp Half-Blood.</p></li>
<li><p>He is assigned to Cabin 11, which is for Hermes and the undetermined demigods.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>7</strong></p>
<p><strong>“My Dinner Goes Up in Smoke”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Mount Olympus is at the Empire State Building, and something troubling is happening with “the Big Three” around the summer solstice.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy learns that Annabeth has to live at the camp year-round for her protection but is still hoping for a quest.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>8</strong></p>
<p><strong>“We Capture a Flag”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>During a camp-wide game of Capture the Flag, Percy’s fighting instincts kick in, and he helps win the game.</p></li>
<li><p>He gains strength from the creek water when he falls in and is claimed by his father Poseidon.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>9</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Am Offered a Quest”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy learns that Zeus’s master bolt has been stolen. Zeus believes that Poseidon got Percy to steal it.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy is given a prophecy from the Oracle and must go on a quest to retrieve the master bolt.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>10</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Ruin a Perfectly Good Bus”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy and his friends set off on their quest with three magical gifts—the invisibility hat, the flying shoes, and the pen sword.</p></li>
<li><p>They are attacked by the three Furies from the Underworld. They barely escape and have brought attention to themselves from humans.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>11</strong></p>
<p><strong>“We Visit the Garden Gnome Emporium”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>The trio goes to a garden store where they meet Aunty Em, or Medusa. She has the power to turn people to stone statues with her eyes.</p></li>
<li><p>Medusa attacks them and Percy decapitates her, mailing her head back to Mount Olympus.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>12</strong></p>
<p><strong>“We Get Advice from a Poodle”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy dreams about a frightening voice in a deep chasm calling out for his help in exchange for his mother.</p></li>
<li><p>The trio returns a lost dog to get the reward money and buy a train ticket west.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>13</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Plunge to My Death”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>The trio goes to the top of the St. Louis Gateway Arch and is attacked by Echidna, the Mother of Monsters, and her son, Chimera.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy jumps off the Arch into the Mississippi River to escape.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>14</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Become a Known Fugitive”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy falls into the water and realizes he can breathe underwater.</p></li>
<li><p>A water spirit brings him a message that there is still hope for his mom.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>15</strong></p>
<p><strong>“A God Buys Us Cheeseburgers”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>The trio meets Ares, the god of war, who offers them a ride to L.A. if Percy can retrieve Ares’s shield from a local water park.</p></li>
<li><p>It turns out to be a trap set by Aphrodite’s jealous husband, Hephaestus, trying to catch her cheating with Ares.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>16</strong></p>
<p><strong>“We Take a Zebra to Vegas”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy learns the full story of Thalia’s death while traveling in a truck with abused animals.</p></li>
<li><p>The trio ends up trapped in the Lotus Casino in Las Vegas where time moves slowly. They lose five days of their trip there.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>17</strong></p>
<p><strong>“We Shop for Water Beds”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>A Nereid, or “a spirit of the sea,” gives Percy three pearls to help him escape danger.</p></li>
<li><p>Hiding in a waterbed store, the trio is trapped by Procrustes, aka “Crusty,” the Stretcher, and manage to escape after cutting off his head.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>18</strong></p>
<p><strong>“Annabeth Does Obedience School”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>The trio enters the Underworld and meets Charon, the ferryman across the River Styx.</p></li>
<li><p>Cerberus, the three-headed dog, can detect those who are not dead; Annabeth plays fetch with him so Percy can sneak in to see Hades.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>19</strong></p>
<p><strong>“We Find Out the Truth, Sort Of”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Grover’s magic shoes start to fly him down the entrance to Tartarus; he barely escapes.</p></li>
<li><p>The trio meets Hades. The master bolt mysteriously ends up in Percy’s backpack. Hades offers to trade Percy’s mother for the helm of darkness.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy uses the three magic pearls to escape Hades with his friends, leaving his mother behind.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>20</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Battle My Jerk Relative”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Back in LA, Ares admits that the shoes and backpack were enchanted. Percy was supposed to die to enrage Poseidon. Hades would be found with the bolt, angering Zeus.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy fights Ares; the Furies watch and realize Percy really isn’t the thief. The trio head back to Mount Olympus to determine the true thief and stop the war.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="odd">
<td><p><strong>21</strong></p>
<p><strong>“I Settle My Tab”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Percy goes to Mount Olympus, returns the bolt, and meets his dad.</p></li>
<li><p>Percy reunites with his mom, offers to help her with Gabe, and returns to Camp Half-Blood.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p><strong>22</strong></p>
<p><strong>“The Prophecy Comes True”</strong></p></td>
<td><ul>
<li><p>Luke reveals himself as the thief helping Cronus to start the war between gods.</p></li>
<li><p>Grover leaves for his quest, Annabeth goes home with her father and stepmother, and Percy returns.</p></li>
</ul></td>
</tr>
