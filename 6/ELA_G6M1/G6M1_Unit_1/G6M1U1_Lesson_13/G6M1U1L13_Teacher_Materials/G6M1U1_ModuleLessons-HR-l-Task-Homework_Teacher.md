**Part C**

How does Percy’s point of view about Auntie Em differ from Annabeth’s?

Percy trusts Auntie Em and thinks she is kind. He is thankful for the food she gives them. Annabeth, on the other hand, is far more observant and suspicious of her, and she notices the clues that Auntie Em is not who she is pretending to be.
