# Lesson 13: Generate Discussion Norms
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read the novel before observing a practice discussion. They then work together to generate norms for discussion as part of their preparation for the end of unit text-based discussion during Lesson 15–16.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.3, SL.6.1b, L.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 11 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.3)
- I can generate norms for a productive discussion. (SL.6.1b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 11 Excerpt – RL.6.3 (20 minutes) → Generate Discussion Norms – SL.6.1b (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
