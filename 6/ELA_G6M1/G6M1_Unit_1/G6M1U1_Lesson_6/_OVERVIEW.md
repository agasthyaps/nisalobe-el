# Lesson 6: Launch Independent Research Reading
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read the novel and choose independent research reading texts.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.4, RL.6.9, L.6.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 4 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can select a research reading text that I want to read.

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.6.4 (5 minutes) → Share QuickWrite – RL.6.9 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 4 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (15 minutes) → Launch Independent Research Reading (15 minutes)
