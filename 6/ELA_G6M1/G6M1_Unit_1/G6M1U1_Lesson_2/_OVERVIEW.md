# Lesson 2: Establish Reading Routines: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 1
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students reflect on the module guiding questions and begin reading the novel, using the reading routines introduced.

**Focus Standards:** L.6.4c, L.6.4d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can show empathy and respect as I listen to my classmates.
- I can find the gist of chapter 1 of *The Lightning Thief*.
- I can determine the difference between academic and domain-specific vocabulary. (L.6.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.6.10 (5 minutes) → Reflect on the Module Guiding Questions (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 1 Excerpt – SL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Launch Vocabulary Logs – L.6.4 (10 minutes)
