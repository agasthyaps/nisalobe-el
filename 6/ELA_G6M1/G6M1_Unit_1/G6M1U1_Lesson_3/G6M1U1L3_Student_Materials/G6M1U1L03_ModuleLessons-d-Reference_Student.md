Synopsis: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 2

**Name:** **Date:**

- Percy’s academic progress and behavior regress. His mother is sent a letter stating that Percy will not be invited back to school the next year.

- Percy goes to Mr. Brunner’s office to seek help in studying for the final exam, where Percy overhears Grover and Mr. Brunner talking about Percy’s safety, Kindly Ones, the summer solstice deadline, and Grover failing in his duties.

- The school year ends. Grover and Percy ride the same Greyhound bus into the city. On the bus, Grover looks nervous when Percy mentions the Kindly Ones.

- Percy confronts Grover about the conversation between Mr. Brunner and Grover.

- At that moment, the bus malfunctions, and the passengers have to get off.

- Across the street, Percy sees three women with a fruit stand. The old women are knitting a huge pair of socks. The old woman sitting in the middle rocking chair takes out a huge pair of scissors.

- Percy hears the snip of the scissors across the road.

- Percy asks if the yarn being cut means that someone is going to die. Grover does not answer but looks at Percy with a worried expression.
