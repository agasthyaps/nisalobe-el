QuickWrite: Point of View:  
*The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 1

RL.6.1, RL.6.4, RL.6.6, W.6.5, W.6.10, L.6.4

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Read the excerpt in chapter 1 on page 2 beginning at “But Mr. Brunner, our Latin teacher, was leading this trip, so I had hopes . . .” and ending at “. . . I wouldn’t get in trouble.”

QuickWrite to answer the following question (this means write whatever comes into your head based on what you have read in the text):

From reading this excerpt, what is Percy’s point of view toward Mr. Brunner?

Sample student response: Percy likes Mr. Brunner as a person and a teacher, and Percy seems to admire him. He thinks that Mr. Brunner is “cool” because he tells stories and jokes and allows students to play games in class. Percy also thinks he’s a good teacher, as he says that Mr. Brunner is “the only teacher whose class didn’t put me to sleep.”
