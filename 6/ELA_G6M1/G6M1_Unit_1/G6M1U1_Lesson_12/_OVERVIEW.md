# Lesson 12: Introduce Themes in *The Lightning Thief*
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** This lesson acts as an introduction to the literacy concept of theme. Students will engage in a deeper dive of this concept in Unit 2. Their examination of the narrator’s point of view thus far in the novel should begin to reveal the themes, or messages, that the author believes are important.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.2, RL.6.3, RL.6.4, L.6.4b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 10 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.3)
- I can identify themes in *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Determine Unfamiliar Words – L.6.4b (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 10 Excerpt – RL.6.3 (15 minutes) → Introduce Theme – RL.6.2 (15 minutes)
