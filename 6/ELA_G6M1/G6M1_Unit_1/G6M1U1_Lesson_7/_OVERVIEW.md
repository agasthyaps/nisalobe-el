# Lesson 7: Analyze Figurative Language: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapters 4 and 5
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read the anchor text, *The Lightning Thief*, and analyze the narrator’s developing point of view.

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.4, L.6.4, L.6.5a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 5 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can interpret figurative language in *The Lightning Thief*. (L.6.5a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.5a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 5 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (20 minutes) → Figurative Language: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 5 – L.6.5a (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
