End of Unit 1 Assessment Part II Rubric

Use this rubric to assess students’ responses to Part II of the assessment.

| Advanced                                                                                          | Proficient                                                                                                        | Developing                                                                                                | Beginning                                                                                                                    |
|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Identifies three key events and describes them accurately and concisely using well-chosen details | Identifies three events that are important to the story and describes them accurately using details from the text | Identifies two or more events that are important to the story, but may not adequately describe the events | The majority of events identified are not important to the storyline. Description may contain too much or too little detail. |
| Choice of events and description shows an insightful understanding of how the plot is unfolding   | Choice of events and description shows a clear understanding of how the plot is unfolding                         | Choice of events and description shows a sketchy or incomplete understanding of how the plot is unfolding | Choice of events and description shows a lack of understanding or a misunderstanding of how the plot is unfolding            |

Responses will vary. A sample event and description are included on the student assessment.
