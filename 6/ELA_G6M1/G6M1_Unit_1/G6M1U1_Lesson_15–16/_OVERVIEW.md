# Lessons 15–16: End of Unit 1 Assessment: Text-Based Discussion: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapters 1–12
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lessons 15–16**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue reading the novel and then complete the end of unit assessment, in which they complete a group discussion about how Percy responds to and changes as a result of the events during the first half of the novel and answer short response questions requiring them to quote

**Focus Standards:** RL.6.1, RL.6.3, SL.6.1a, SL.6.1b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt from chapter 11 of *The Lightning Thief*. (RL.6.1)
- I can use evidence from the text to support my ideas during a text-based discussion. (RL.6.1, RL.6.3, SL.6.1a)
- I can follow discussion norms to have an effective text-based discussion. (SL.6.1b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return Mid-Unit Assessments (10 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 12 Excerpt – RL.6.1 (15 minutes) → End of Unit 1 Assessment: Text-Based Discussion: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapters 1–12 (45 minutes)
