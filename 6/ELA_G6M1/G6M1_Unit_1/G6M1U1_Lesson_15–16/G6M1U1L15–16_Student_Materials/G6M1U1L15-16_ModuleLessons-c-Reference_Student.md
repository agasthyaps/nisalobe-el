Synopsis: The Lightning Thief, Chapter 12

**Name:** **Date:**

- Grover, Percy, and Annabeth retreat to the woods.

- Grover informs Percy of the great satyr, Pan, and Grover’s desire to find Pan.

- Percy has a dream in which a deep voice from the depths of a seemingly bottomless pit is trying to persuade Percy to help him, and, in turn, the deep voice states that it will help Percy get all he wants.

- The next morning, Grover introduces Gladiola, a pink poodle who has been separated from her family.

- Gladiola has agreed to be returned to her family so that Grover can get the reward money to fund the group’s travel west.
