Synopsis: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 9

**Name:** **Date:**

- Percy is moved to Cabin 3, which is the cabin for the children of Poseidon, although Percy is the only one there.

- The other campers steer clear of Percy.

- Chiron tells Percy that Zeus and Poseidon are fighting over Zeus’s master bolt. Zeus thinks that Percy stole the bolt. Chiron tells Percy that he must speak with the Oracle.

- Percy goes into the attic and receives his prophecy from the Oracle. He is granted a quest, which he accepts.

- Grover and Annabeth will be Percy’s companions on the quest.
