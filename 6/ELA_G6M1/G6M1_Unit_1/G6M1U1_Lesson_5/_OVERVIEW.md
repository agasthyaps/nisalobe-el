# Lesson 5: Close Read: Informational Text: “Why Ancient Greek Mythology Is Still Relevant Today”
**Grade 6: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students build their knowledge of Greek mythology and its relevance today by reading an informational text on the topic and participating in a Language Dive.

**Focus Standards:** RI.6.1, RI.6.2, RI.6.4, L.6.4a, L.6.4b, L.6.4c, L.6.4d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine a central idea of an informational text and how it is conveyed through details. (RI.6.2)
- I can summarize an informational text. (RI.6.2)
- I can determine the meaning of unfamiliar words and phrases in a text. (L.6.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.6.4b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Construct Anchor Charts (5 minutes) → Close Read: “Why Ancient Greek Mythology Is Still Relevant Today” – RI.6.2 (15 minutes) → Language Dive: “Why Ancient Greek Mythology Is Still Relevant Today,” Paragraph 10 – L.6.4 (15 minutes)
