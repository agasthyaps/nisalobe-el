Sample Model (oral summary of introduction and first section):

In the article “Why Ancient Greek Mythology Is Still Relevant Today,” Gerri Mlleff explains why Greek myths are still important today. MIleff says that myths are more than just old “tales of gods and goddesses doing impossible tasks on earth.” Myths can reveal morals, philosophies and warnings. They can also give us a glimpse of how the ancient Greeks lived and thought . . .
