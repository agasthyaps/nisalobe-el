Synopsis: *The Lightning Thief*, Chapter 6

**Name:** **Date:**

- Chiron gives Percy a tour of Camp Half-Blood and the houses where demigods live. Each cabin represents a Greek god; the demigods live in the cabin that represents their immortal parent.

- Chiron asks Annabeth to continue the tour with Percy. Annabeth introduces Percy to the campers in cabin eleven, where Percy meets Luke.

- Annabeth explains that many of Percy’s attributes, like his dyslexia and ADHD, are common in demigods.

- Another camper from the Ares cabin, Clarisse, approaches Percy and Annabeth. She begins to harass them and drags Percy to the bathroom.

- Clarisse is about to dunk Percy’s head into the toilet when water shoots out of the toilet.

- Clarisse, her friends, and Annabeth all get soaked, but Percy is dry.

- Annabeth and Percy are both shocked because they have no idea how Percy made the toilets and showers explode.

- Annabeth tells Percy that she wants him on her team for capture the flag.
