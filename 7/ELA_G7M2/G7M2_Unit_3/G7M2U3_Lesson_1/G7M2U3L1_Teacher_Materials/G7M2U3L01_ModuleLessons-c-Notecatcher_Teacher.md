Analyze a Model Podcast Note-Catcher

SL.7.2

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you listen to the podcast, take notes on any podcast features you hear. Some podcast features include

- a hook to grab the listener’s attention;

- a variety of voices;

- a charismatic, engaging tone;

- interviews;

- description;

- fieldwork to a site mentioned in the podcast;

- music;

- sound effects; and

- more than one voice speaking chorally.

Also, listen for the main ideas and supporting details.
