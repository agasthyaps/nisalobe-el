# Lesson 1: Create a Podcast: Analyze a Model Podcast
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students review the performance task of working in a triad to make a podcast about an epidemic that concerns them or their community. Then students listen to an exemplary podcast and note the features, techniques, and main ideas with supporting details. Finally, students join with their podcast tria

**Focus Standards:** SL.7.2, W.7.7, L.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can explore a podcast model and generate ideas for my own podcast. (SL.7.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Review the Performance Task (5 minutes) → Analyze a Model Podcast – SL.7.2 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
