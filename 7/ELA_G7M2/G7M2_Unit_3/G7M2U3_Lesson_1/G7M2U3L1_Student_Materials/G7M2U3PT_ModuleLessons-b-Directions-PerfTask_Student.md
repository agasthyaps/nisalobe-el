Performance Task Directions

RI.7.1, W.7.6, SL.7.4, SL.7.5, L.7.1, L.7.3

**Name:** **Date:**

You will create a podcast in groups of three with sound effects, music, and other podcast features. You’ll learn more about podcast features today, but some include:

- A hook to grab the listener’s attention

- A variety of voices

- A charismatic, engaging tone

- Interviews

- Description

- Fieldwork to a site mentioned in the podcast

You will publish your podcast by posting it on the internet, to a website, class blog, or some other site.

**The purpose of the podcast:**

- Teach others about an epidemic that is impacting your community.

**Your podcast will include**

- music and/or other sound effects;

- more than one voice;

- organization of the podcast with different sections and chapters or act titles.

**The content of the podcast will include**

- your podcast script, which includes the following four sections:

- A narrative nonfiction lead telling the story of the epidemic, including narrative elements such as characters, main events, and problem/solution. Also, include narrative techniques such as a hook, dialogue, transitional words, and pacing.

- The scientific and social ideas about the disease that people believe. These ideas might change over the course of the epidemic, and if so, you should explain the changes and if possible, what caused them.

- The tools, mindset, and habits of character of the people who responded to the epidemic.

- A deeper message or insight: what do we learn from the epidemic?
