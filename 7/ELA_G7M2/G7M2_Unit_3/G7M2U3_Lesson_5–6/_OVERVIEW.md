# Lessons 5–6: Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Research to Answer a Question
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 3: Lessons 5–6**

**Summary:** Students complete their mid-unit assessment by reading a new article, answering selected and constructed response questions to analyze how individuals, events, and ideas interact in the text. Students continue the assessment by conducting research to answer a question raised by the article read in t

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, RI.7.3, RI.7.4, W.7.7, W.7.8, L.7.4a, L.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can research to answer questions about epidemics. (W.7.7, W.7.8)
- I can analyze how individuals, ideas, and events influence one another. (RI.7.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Research to Answer a Question, Part I (30 minutes) → Language Dive: “Disease Detective” – RI.7.3, L.7.1a (10 minutes) → Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Research to Answer a Question, Part II (30 minutes)
