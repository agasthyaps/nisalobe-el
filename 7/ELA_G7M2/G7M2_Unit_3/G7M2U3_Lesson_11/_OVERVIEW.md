# Lesson 11: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Present Podcast Script
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students complete their end of unit assessment by making their podcast presentation for the class. Then students close the lesson by sharing about their independent research reading.

**Focus Standards:** SL.7.4, SL.7.6, L.7.3, L.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can present my findings in a focused and coherent manner, emphasizing salient points. (SL.7.4)
- I can use adequate volume and clear pronunciation. (SL.7.4)
- I can adapt my speech for a podcast, using formal English when appropriate. (SL.7.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Present Podcast Script (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Independent Research Reading Share – RI.7.10 (15 minutes)
