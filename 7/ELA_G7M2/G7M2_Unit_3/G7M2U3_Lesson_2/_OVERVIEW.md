# Lesson 2: Create a Podcast: Introduce Research
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students analyze the Model Podcast Script to determine the purpose and content of the different sections, which prepares them to conduct research for their own script. Based on this analysis, students then generate research questions and design a note-catcher to guide them in finding and recording i

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, W.7.4, W.7.7

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the purpose and content of an effective podcast script about an epidemic. (W.7.4)
- I can research to answer questions about epidemics. (RI.7.1, W.7.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model Podcast Script – W.7.4 (15 minutes) → Generate and Refine Research Questions – W.7.7 (10 minutes) → Create a Research Note-Catcher – W.7.7 (10 minutes)
