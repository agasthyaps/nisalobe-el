Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 8

L.7.4

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions**: As you enter class, read the following learning target, and respond to the questions that follow:

- I can choose language that expresses ideas precisely and concisely, recognizing and eliminating wordiness and redundancy.

Use context, background knowledge, and if necessary, a dictionary, to define the following words:

**precisely:**

With total accuracy; exactly

**concisely:**

With few words; being short and to the point

**eliminating:**

Leaving out; removing

**wordiness:**

Using too many words

**redundancy:**

Unnecessary repetition of words

Restate the learning target in your own words:

Responses will vary, but may include: use language that tells your ideas clearly and briefly, cutting out any extra or repeated words.

Reread the narrative lead of your podcast script, and make notes in the margins of places where you might write more precisely, concisely, or eliminate wordiness and redundancy.

Responses will vary.
