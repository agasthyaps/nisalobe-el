(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions**: As you enter class, answer the following questions.

1.  Read the following sentence from page 21 of *Patient Zero*, and answer the question that follows.

    “He issued a **proclamation** banning all public gatherings, even funerals.”

    What is the definition of the word **proclamation** as it is used on page 21?

<!-- -->

1.  <u>announcement to the public</u>

2.  amount of money

3.  letter

4.  newspaper report

5.  Read the following sentence from page 28 of *Patient Zero*, and answer the question that follows.

    “Commercial air traffic to and from India stopped, the stock market in India crashed, trade halted, and the media went crazy **speculating** about the possibility that plague could spread to other countries, triggering a massive pandemic.”

    What is the definition of the word **speculating** as it is used on page 28?

6.  running

7.  changing

8.  getting angry

9.  <u>making a guess</u>

Source: Peters, Marilee. *Patient Zero*. Annick Press, 2021.
