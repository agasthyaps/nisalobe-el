# Lessons 6–7: Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Structure: *Patient Zero*, Pages 38–41
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 1: Lessons 6–7**

**Summary:** Students read pages 38–41 of *Patient Zero*, determining the gist and noting new vocabulary. Then students participate in a Language Dive during which they explore a sentence from *Patient Zero* to better understand language structures used in the text. Finally, students complete the mid-unit assess

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, RI.7.4, RI.7.5, L.7.4, L.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meanings of unfamiliar words and phrases. (L.7.4)
- I can identify the structures Peters uses to organize pages 38–41 of *Patient Zero*. (RI.7.5)
- I can explain how sections of *Patient Zero* contribute to the whole chapter. (RI.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return Module 1, End of Unit 3 Assessments (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Patient Zero*, Pages 38–40 (up to “The Grand Experiment”) and 41 (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *Patient Zero*, Page 39 – RI.7.5 (15 minutes)
