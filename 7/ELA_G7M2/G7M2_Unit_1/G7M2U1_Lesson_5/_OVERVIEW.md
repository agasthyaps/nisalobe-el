# Lesson 5: Analyze Text Structure: *Patient Zero*, Pages 32–38
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students read pages 32–38 of *Patient Zero*, determining the gist and noting new vocabulary. Then students work independently to analyze how the author structures the text, how major sections contribute to the whole and to the development of ideas. Students close the lesson checking their understand

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.5, L.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meanings of unfamiliar words and phrases. (L.7.4)
- I can identify the structures Peters uses to organize pages 32–38 of *Patient Zero*. (RI.7.5)
- I can explain how sections of *Patient Zero* contribute to the whole chapter. (RI.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Patient Zero*, Pages 32–38 (up to “Know Your Enemy”) – SL.7.1 (15 minutes) → Analyze Structure: *Patient Zero*, Pages 32–38 (up to “Know Your Enemy”) – RI.7.5 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
