# Lesson 14: Text-Based Discussion: Individuals, Events, and Ideas: *Patient Zero*, Chapter 4
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** Students prepare for their text-based discussion by reviewing their note-catchers on interactions among individuals, events, and ideas in chapter 4 of *Patient Zero*. Then they participate in a text-based discussion about these interactions and how they impact our scientific and social ideas about d

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.3, SL.7.1, SL.7.1d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can quote accurately from the text to support my ideas during a text-based discussion. (RI.7.1)
- I can acknowledge new information expressed by others and, when necessary, modify my own views. (SL.7.1d)
- I can analyze how individuals, ideas, and events influence one another. (RI.7.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Prepare for a Text-Based Discussion – RI.7.3 (5 minutes) → Text-Based Discussion – RI.7.3, SL.7.1d (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
