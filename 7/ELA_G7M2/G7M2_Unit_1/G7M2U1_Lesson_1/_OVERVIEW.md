# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: Epidemics
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students participate in the Infer the Topic protocol by engaging with the texts, images, and ideas they will examine throughout the module. Also, students encounter the performance task and the guiding questions for the module, as well as the module’s anchor text.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from the resources. (RI.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Infer the Topic – RI.7.1 (15 minutes) → Introduce the Performance Task and Module Guiding Questions – SL.7.1 (10 minutes) → Launch the Text: *Patient Zero* – RI.7.5 (10 minutes)
