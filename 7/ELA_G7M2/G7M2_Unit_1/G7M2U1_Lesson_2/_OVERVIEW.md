# Lesson 2: Analyze Text Features and Structures: *Patient Zero*, Pages 9–14 and 17
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students begin reading the anchor text of the module, *Patient Zero*, determining the gist and noting new vocabulary. Then with modeling and guidance, students begin to analyze how the author structures the text, how major sections contribute to the whole and to the development of ideas.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.5, L.7.4c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meanings of unfamiliar words and phrases. (L.7.4c)
- I can find the gist of pages 9–14 and 17 of *Patient Zero*.
- I can identify the structures Peters uses to organize pages 9–14 and 17 of *Patient Zero*. (RI.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4c (5 minutes) → Reflect on the Module Guiding Questions – SL.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Patient Zero*, Pages 9–14 (up to “The Fear Builds”) and 17 (15 minutes) → Analyze Structure: *Patient Zero*, Pages 9–14 and 17 – RI.7.5 (15 minutes)
