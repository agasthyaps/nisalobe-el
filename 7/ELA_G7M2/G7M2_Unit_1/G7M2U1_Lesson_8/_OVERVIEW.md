# Lesson 8: Analyze Interactions among Individuals, Events, and Ideas: *Patient Zero*, Pages 40–45, 47, and 49
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students read pages 40–45, 47, and 49 of *Patient Zero*, determining the gist and noting new vocabulary. Then they begin to analyze the interaction of individuals, events, and ideas in the text. Students conclude the class by discussing their ideas about these interactions, focusing on acknowledging

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the impact of a specific word choice on meaning and tone. (RI.7.4)
- I can identify key individuals, events, and ideas in a text. (RI.7.3)
- I can analyze how individuals, ideas, and events interact with one another. (RI.7.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Patient Zero*, Pages 40–45, 47, and 49 (15 minutes) → Analyze Interactions among Individuals, Events, and Ideas: *Patient Zero*, Pages 40–45, 47, and 49 – RI.7.3 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
