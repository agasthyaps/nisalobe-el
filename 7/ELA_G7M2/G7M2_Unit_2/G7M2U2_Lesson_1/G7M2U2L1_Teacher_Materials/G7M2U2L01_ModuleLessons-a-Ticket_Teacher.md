1.  How does this term relate to *Patient Zero*?

What are some sicknesses that get spread through contagion?

*Patient Zero* described epidemics that spread rapidly among people.

Cholera and typhoid were examples of a contagion.

2.  What could the term **social contagion** mean?

Hint: The word **social** means in groups or communities.

Social contagion might mean things that spread socially, or in social groups.
