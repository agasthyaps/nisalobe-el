Language Dive: “Are Social Epidemics Real?” Paragraph 3 Note-Catcher

RL.7.8, L.7.1a

(Example for Teacher Reference)

These examples suggest **that it only takes a few people to spread a behavior or idea,** just like a disease.

1.  Complete both of these sentence frames with the same infinitive verb phrase to explore how word order affects the focus of a sentence.

I believe that it only takes a few people **<u>to make change</u>**.

infinitive verb clause

I believe that **<u>to make change</u>** only takes a few people.

infinitive verb clause

2.  Complete this sentence frame to compare behaviors and ideas to diseases, in your own words:

**<u>Theories</u>** suggest that **<u>ideas and emotions can be “caught,”</u>** just like a disease.

subject independent clause
