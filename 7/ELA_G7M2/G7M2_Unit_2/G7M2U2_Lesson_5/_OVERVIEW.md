# Lesson 5: Analyze Argument: “Conflicting Ideas”
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students reread “Conflicting Ideas” and work independently to analyze and evaluate the argument in preparation for the mid-unit assessment in the following lessons. They conclude the lesson by practicing their collaborative discussion skills.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.8, SL.7.1d, L.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify the main claim, points, evidence, and reasoning in a text. (RI.7.8)
- I can acknowledge new information expressed by others and, when warranted, modify my own views. (SL.7.1d)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read Aloud “Conflicting Ideas” (10 minutes) → Identify Main Claim, Points, Evidence, and Reasoning: “Conflicting Ideas” – RI.7.8 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
