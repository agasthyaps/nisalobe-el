# Lesson 11: Write an Informative Essay: Draft Proof Paragraph 2
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students analyze strong topic and concluding sentences in the Proof Paragraphs of the Model Informative Essay. Students conclude the lesson by drafting the second Proof Paragraph of their peer essay, focusing on the topic and concluding sentences.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, W.7.2b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write Proof Paragraph 2 of my pair informative essay with a strong concluding sentence. (W.7.2b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.2b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Strong Concluding Sentences – W.7.2b (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Pairs: Draft Proof Paragraph 2 – W.7.2b (25 minutes)
