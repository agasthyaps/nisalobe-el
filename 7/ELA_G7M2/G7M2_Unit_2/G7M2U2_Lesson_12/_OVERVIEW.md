# Lesson 12: Write an Informative Essay: Draft Conclusion
**Grade 7: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** Students use the Painted Essay® structure to analyze the conclusion paragraph of the Model Informative Essay. Then they participate in a Language Dive on a sentence from the conclusion of the Model Informative Essay. Finally, students draft the conclusion paragraph of their peer essay, focusing on r

**Focus Standards:** W.7.2f, L.7.1a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write the conclusion of my informative essay, restating the focus of the piece and adding a reflection. (W.7.2f)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.2f (5 minutes) → Work Time → The Painted Essay®: Sort and Color-Code the Parts of the Conclusion – W.7.2f (10 minutes) → Language Dive: Model Informative Essay, Paragraph 4 – W.7.2f, L.7.1a (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
