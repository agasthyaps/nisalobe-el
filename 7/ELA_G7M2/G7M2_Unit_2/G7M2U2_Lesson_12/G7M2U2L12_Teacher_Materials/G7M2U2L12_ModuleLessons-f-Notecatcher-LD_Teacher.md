Language Dive: Model Informative Essay, Paragraph 4 Note-Catcher

W.7.2f, L.7.1a

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**<u>It seems clear</u>** that computer research can help us make sense of how and why animals move.

1.  Complete this sentence frame to write and talk about something in your life.

(**It seems clear** + conjunction **that** + dependent clause**.**)

It seems clear that **the more I learn, the more I realize I have yet to learn**.

2.  Complete this sentence frame to write a conclusion statement for your essay in your own words with your partner.

(**It seem**s + adjective + conjunction **that** + noun phrase + verb phrase + noun phrase**.**)

It seems **obvious** that **the study of crime detection** can help us make sense of **epidemics**.
