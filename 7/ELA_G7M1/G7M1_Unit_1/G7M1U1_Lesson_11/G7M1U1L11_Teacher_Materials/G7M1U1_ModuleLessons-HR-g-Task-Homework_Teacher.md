Homework: Summary and Theme:  
*A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 8

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Answer the following questions to help you analyze the theme of chapter 8.

1.  What does the author mean when she writes that Akeer’s laugh “was like music” (45)?

2.  It was loud.

3.  <u>It made Nya happy.</u>

4.  It went on for a long time.

5.  It sounded strange to Nya.

6.  Describe the way Nya’s mood changes over the course of her section. Use evidence from the text.

She is happy that her sister is recovering, and Akeer’s laugh sounds “like music” to her (45). But Nya also becomes concerned as she listens to the nurse explain about drinking clean water to her mother. The author writes, “Nya could see the worry” in her mother’s eyes, because they both know that it is very difficult for them to get clean water (45).

7.  How do the words **abundance** and **impressive** contribute to understanding Salva’s feelings as he arrives at the island of fishermen on page 47?

8.  They show that he is jealous about where the fishermen live.

9.  They show why he wants to leave the group and join the fishermen.

10. <u>They show how excited he is to see all the food the fishermen have.</u>

11. They show that he is uncertain about whether the fishermen will help.
