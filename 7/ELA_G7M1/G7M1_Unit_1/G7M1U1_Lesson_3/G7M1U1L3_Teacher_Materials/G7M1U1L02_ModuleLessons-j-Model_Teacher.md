Gists: *A Long Walk to Water*

(Example for Teacher Reference)

This chart lists sample gists for each chapter and section of *A Long Walk to Water*.

| Chapter | Nya                                                                                                                  | Salva                                                                                |
|---------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 1       | walking somewhere with container in the heat                                                                         | in school, hears gunshots, runs away                                                 |
| 2       | takes thorn out of foot                                                                                              | walking with a group; not his family; soldiers take men; groups leaves him behind    |
| 3       | gets to pond, fills container, begins long walk home                                                                 | stays with old woman for a few days, must leave with other walkers                   |
| 4       | stops at home, goes back to pond with little sister Akeer                                                            | leaves the old woman’s house, joins a new group of people escaping                   |
| 5       | family moves to lake; must dig hole and wait for water                                                               | meets new friend Mariel, walk together in lion-country                               |
| 6       | likes lake: no walk to water; mother hates lake: no house, and husband/son in danger                                 | uncle in group, shoots an antelope, all get sick; Mariel missing...lion?             |
| 7       | Akeer has bad stomachache; can kill children or the elderly; walk her long way to clinic?                            | scared of lions, uncle will protect; reach Nile and make boats                       |
| 8       | Akeer better but must boil water and can’t—not enough                                                                | stay in middle of Nile w/mosquitos, eat well, about to go in desert—hardest part yet |
| 9       | strangers come to village to talk to chief about water                                                               | can’t make it through desert but uncle tells him to walk step by step                |
| 10      | strangers say they will find water in between two trees                                                              | one day from camp, uncle will leave to fight, soldiers kill uncle                    |
| 11      | village clears land; how will water come from dry ground?                                                            | makes it to camp, very crowded, looks for family, sees mother?                       |
| 12      | crane comes; villagers making gravel; Nya still walk to pond                                                         | family dead; at camp for 6 years; soldiers chase to river with crocodiles            |
| 13      | trouble drilling but boss keeps them working                                                                         | leads boys to new camp in Kenya; 1½ years                                            |
| 14      | water sprays out of hole but is dirty                                                                                | new camp terrible; learns to read English and play volleyball; going to NY           |
| 15      | have to dig more, put in pipes, gravel, pump, cement = few days                                                      | truck to Nairobi, planes to Frankfurt, NY, Rochester, nice new family                |
| 16      | men clearing land for new building??                                                                                 | 6 years in Rochester: study business, play volleyball; finds father, goes to visit   |
| 17      | making a school for boys AND girls                                                                                   | visits with dad, has idea to help Sudan, asks many people for help                   |
| 18      | all drink water; well is for all; boss is Dinka; brought water to many places in Sudan; Nya thanks him; he is Salva! |                                                                                      |
