Synopsis: *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 2

RL.7.2

**Name:** **Date:**

- Nya is jabbed in the foot by a thorn.

- Salva runs and walks for a long time.

- Other people are walking, too.

- They organize by village, but no one from his family is there.

- They arrive at the rebel camp, and the men are forced to go with the rebels.

- The women and children sleep in a barn that night.

- Salva is left all alone; the others walk on without him.
