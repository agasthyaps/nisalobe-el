# Lesson 14: Prepare for Text-Based Discussion: Themes: *A Long Walk to Water*
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read chapter 11 of *A Long Walk to Water* and record vocabulary words and the gist of the chapter. Then students prepare for the end of unit text-based discussion, in which they analyze how themes develop in *A Long Walk to Water*.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, SL.7.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify how themes in *A Long Walk to Water* have developed in chapter 11. (RL.7.2)
- I can prepare for a text-based discussion about how themes are developed in *A Long Walk to Water*. (RL.7.2, SL.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.8 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 11 (15 minutes) → Prepare for a Text-Based Discussion – RL.7.2 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
