Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lessons 15–16

SL.7.1

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following questions.

1.  Read the Discussion Norms anchor chart.

**Part A**

Which of the norms do you think you might find challenging today?

Responses will vary.

**Part B**

What can you do to overcome this challenge?

Responses will vary.

**Part C**

What will help you to feel you can succeed at this discussion?

Responses will vary. Possible response: Being prepared (having good notes) and understanding how I will be graded and what the expectations are for me will help me succeed. As will being brave to speak up and use discussion norms.

2.  What value does today’s discussion assessment have for you?

Responses will vary. Possible response: This discussion will help me learn to discuss topics in this and other classes. Also, the discussion topic has value to me because I am very interested in this book and the topic of the world water crisis. I think it is important for all people to know about this problem and what can be done about it.

3.  What does this discussion assessment mean to you beyond the work you are doing in the classroom?

Responses will vary. Possible response: This discussion will help me learn to discuss topics in other classes and in other settings like work and at home and in high school.
