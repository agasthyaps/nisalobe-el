# Lesson 5: Close Read: “The Lost Boys of the Sudan”
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in a close read of the article “The Lost Boys of the Sudan.” During this close read, students identify main ideas and supporting details as they focus on information related to the Sudanese Civil War and the stories of the Lost Boys, who escaped the fighting and 

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.2, L.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine central ideas and how they are developed in “The Lost Boys of the Sudan” informational text. (RI.7.2)
- I can objectively summarize “The Lost Boys of the Sudan” informational text. (RI.7.2)
- I can use evidence from both texts to make connections between “The Lost Boys of the Sudan” and *A Long Walk to Water*. (RL.7.1, RI.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Close Read: “The Lost Boys of the Sudan” – RI.7.2 (25 minutes) → Language Dive: “The Lost Boys of the Sudan,” Paragraph 4 – RI.7.2 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
