Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 13

SL.7.1

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following questions.

What are some words or phrases you can say to hear more about what your partner or group member is saying in a discussion?

Can you say more about that?

Can you give an example?

Why do you think that?

What makes you think that?

Where do you see that in the text?

What are some words or phrases that stop the discussion?

I don’t agree.

That’s ridiculous.

You don’t get it.

This is stupid.
