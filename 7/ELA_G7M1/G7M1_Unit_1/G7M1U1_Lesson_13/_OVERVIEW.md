# Lesson 13: Generate Discussion Norms
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue their work examining how theme is developed in *A Long Walk to Water*. Then students work together to generate discussion norms based on reflections of their own previous text-based discussions and observations of a group discussion.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, SL.7.1, SL.7.1a, SL.7.1b, SL.7.1c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify how themes in *A Long Walk to Water* have developed in chapter 10. (RL.7.2)
- I can generate norms for a productive discussion. (SL.7.1, SL.7.1a, SL.7.1b, SL.7.1c)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 10 – RL.7.1 (15 minutes) → Analyze Themes: *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 10 – RL.7.2 (5 minutes) → Generate Discussion Norms – SL.7.1 (15 minutes)
