# Lesson 9: Introduce Themes: *A Long Walk to Water*
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue reading *A Long Walk to Water*, focusing on chapter 7. Then students begin a discussion of themes in relation to the novel. Students conclude the lesson by analyzing a model summary and identifying the components of effective summaries.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify themes in *A Long Walk to Water* and how they have developed over the course of the text. (RL.7.1, RL.7.2)
- I can identify the characteristics of an effective summary. (RL.7.1, RL.7.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 7 (15 minutes) → Introduce Theme – RL.7.2 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
