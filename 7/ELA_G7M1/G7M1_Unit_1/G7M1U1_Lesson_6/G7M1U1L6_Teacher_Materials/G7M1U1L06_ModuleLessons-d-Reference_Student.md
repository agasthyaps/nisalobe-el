Lesson 6: *A Long Walk to Water* Illustrations  
by Jim Averbeck

These illustrations by artist Jim Averbeck may be used to support ELLs and other students with comprehension of the novel, beginning in chapter 4 of *A Long Walk to Water*. Also, any students may access these images for the Performance Task children’s ebook they create if they cannot draw or find online images they can manipulate.
