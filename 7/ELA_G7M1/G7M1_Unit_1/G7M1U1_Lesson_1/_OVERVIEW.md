# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: The Lost Children of Sudan
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in the Infer the Topic protocol by engaging with the texts they will read throughout the module. Also, students encounter the performance task and the guiding questions for the module, as well as the module’s anchor text.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.1, RI.7.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can use evidence to infer the topic of this module from the resources. (RL.7.1, RI.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Infer the Topic – RI.7.1 (15 minutes) → Introduce the Performance Task and Module Guiding Questions (10 minutes) → Launch the Text: *A Long Walk to Water* (10 minutes)
