# Lesson 7: Analyze Point of View: *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 5
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read chapter 5 of *A Long Walk to Water* and then answer text-dependent questions to analyze how setting shapes character and plot and how the author develops and contrasts Nya and Salva’s points of view.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.3, RL.7.6, L.7.4, L.7.4a, L.7.4c, L.7.4d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify strategies to determine the meaning of unfamiliar vocabulary. (L.7.4)
- I can analyze how the setting shapes the characters and plot in chapters 4 and 5 of *A Long Walk to Water*. (RL.7.3)
- I can explain how the author develops and contrasts the points of view of Marial and Salva in chapter 5 of *A Long Walk to Water*. (RL.7.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 5 (15 minutes) → Analyze Setting, Characters, Plot, and Point of View: *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 5 – RL.7.3, RL.7.6 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
