Chapter 7 of *A Long Walk to Water* is about Nya and Salva relying on others to help them survive their dangerous environments. Nya’s sister Akeer’s illness is getting worse.  
The family must decide whether to try and bring her to a medical clinic that is far away. Salva and his group continue their journey. Salva is very upset and scared because his friend was taken by a lion. His uncle reassures him that he will protect him. The group reach the river, and they work together to build boats to take them across.

This chapter shows how people have to work together to survive harsh environments. But even when they do, not all will survive.
