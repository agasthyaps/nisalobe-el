# Lesson 4: Write a Narrative: Analyze a Model
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read chapter 18 of *A Long Walk to Water*, the concluding chapter of the novel. Students then analyze the narrative children’s book *Nasreen’s Secret School* in order to determine the criteria for writing their own stories about a Lost Boy or Girl of Sudan.

**Focus Standards:** W.7.3, W.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can generate criteria for an effective narrative. (W.7.3, W.7.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 18 – SL.7.1 (15 minutes) → Analyze a Model: *Nasreen’s Secret School* – W.7.4 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
