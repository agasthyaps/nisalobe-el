(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following prompt.

Now that you have practiced comparing and contrasting audio and texts, what are some of the ways authors show readers how to read texts?

Authors show readers how to read texts through the use of italics, quotation marks, question marks, commas, ellipses, drawing out words, creating sound effects, and varying sentence length.
