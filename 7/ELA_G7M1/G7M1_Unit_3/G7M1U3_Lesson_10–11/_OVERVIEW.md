# Lessons 10–11: Performance Task: Create an Ebook
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 3: Lessons 10–11**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students use technology to create their ebook of their narrative about a Lost Child of Sudan. Then students share their work with a partner, noting successes and challenges with technology.

**Focus Standards:** W.7.3, W.7.4, W.7.5, W.7.6, W.7.10

**Learning Targets:**
- I can use technology to create an ebook. (W.7.5, W.7.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Create Ebook – W.7.6 (60 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Partner Share – W.7.5 (25 minutes)
