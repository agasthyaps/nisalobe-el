# Lesson 1: Compare Audio to Text: Part I
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue reading the novel *A Long Walk to Water* (chapter 16) and listen to an audio version of the book to compare and contrast the effects of each medium. Students conclude the lesson by sharing their ideas on how the audio compares to text.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.7

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare and contrast a written story to the audio version of the story and analyze the effect of the techniques. (RL.7.1, RL.7.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 16 – SL.7.1 (15 minutes) → Compare Audio to Text – RL.7.7 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
