- The men and Nya’s father clear the land in front of Nya’s house for a new building.

- Salva notices how different life is in America with the paved roads, electricity, and all of the white people.

- His father is in a remote part of southern Sudan.

- It takes months of planning for Salva to be able to return to Sudan.

- Salva flies to New York City, Amsterdam, and then Kampala, Uganda, and then onto Juba, Sudan.

- Everything feels the same, and yet everything feels different.
