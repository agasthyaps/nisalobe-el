(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following prompt.

Now that you have been thinking about the plot of your story, what do you think is the best way to start the story and show readers the setting and characters?

Answers will vary. Possible response: I plan to start my story jumping right in with the lost girl running away from the war and starting her journey. This will help me introduce the setting of the journey and war. It will also help me introduce my main character.
