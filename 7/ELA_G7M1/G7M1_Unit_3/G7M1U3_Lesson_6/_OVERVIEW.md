# Lesson 6: Write a Narrative: Plan Plot
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students use the model narrative and their notes and research related to *A Long Walk to Water* to plan the plot of their own narratives about a Lost Child of Sudan. Students also participate in a Language Dive focusing on the meaning of figurative language in a sentence in *Nasreen’

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.4, W.7.3a, W.7.3e, W.7.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning and impact of figurative language. (RL.7.4)
- I can plan the plot of my narrative to mirror the story of a Lost Boy or Girl of Sudan. (W.7.3a, W.7.3e)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: *Nasreen’s Secret School*, Page 22 – RL.7.4 (10 minutes) → Explore Model Plot – W.7.3 (10 minutes) → Plan Plot – W.7.3 (15 minutes)
