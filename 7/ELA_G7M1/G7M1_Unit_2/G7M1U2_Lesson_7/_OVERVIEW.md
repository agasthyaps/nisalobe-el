# Lesson 7: Write a Compare and Contrast Essay: Analyze a Model
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students analyze a model informative essay to identify the gist and the author’s purpose of the essay. Then students explore the Painted Essay® structure to generate criteria for their own essays.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, RI.7.2, W.7.2, W.7.4, W.7.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the central ideas of a model essay. (RI.7.2)
- I can use the Painted Essay® structure to analyze a model. (W.7.2, W.7.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Determine Central Ideas in the Model Essay – RI.7.2 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Explore the Painted Essay® – W.7.2 (20 minutes)
