# Lesson 2: Analyze Main Ideas in a Video
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read *A Long Walk to Water* as well as broaden their understanding of the context for the events in the novel by watching and analyzing a short documentary clip. Students determine the main idea(s) and supporting details of an excerpt from the video *God Grew Tir

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.9, SL.7.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the main ideas and supporting details in a clip from *God Grew Tired of Us*. (SL.7.2)
- I can explain how the ideas in the video answer our questions about the Lost Boys of Sudan. (RL.7.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *A Long Walk to Water*, Chapter 13 (15 minutes) → Analyze Main Ideas and Supporting Details in Video – SL.7.2 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
