Brief Summary

The clip 11:07–13:30 of *God Grew Tired of Us* is mostly about . . .

One detail that shows this is . . .

Another detail that shows this is . . .

These details show that this video clip is mainly about . . .

This clip of the video *God Grew Tired of Us* describes the new camp the boys went to. They were safer, but life was still difficult. The video shows that the boys were away from the war. But it also shows images of the boys fighting over food. Also, the narrator describes how a “new chapter of survival was just beginning” for the boys. One boy backs this idea up by describing how he doesn’t have enough to eat in the camp. All of these images show that life was better in the camp than on the journey or in their war-torn home. But it was still difficult to survive.
