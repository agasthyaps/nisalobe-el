# Lesson 11: Write a Compare and Contrast Essay: Plan Conclusion
**Grade 7: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students reconstruct the conclusion paragraph from the model essay to deepen their understanding of this part of the essay. Then students participate in a Language Dive that guides them through the meaning of the conclusion sentence from the Compare and Contrast Model Essay. Finally,

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.9, W.7.2f, W.7.5, W.7.9a, W.7.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan a conclusion that includes a strong reflection. (W.7.2f, W.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.2f (5 minutes) → Work Time → The Painted Essay®: Sort and Color-Code the Parts of the Conclusion – W.7.2f (10 minutes) → Language Dive: Conclusion – W.7.2f (10 minutes) → Plan a Conclusion – W.7.2f (15 minutes)
