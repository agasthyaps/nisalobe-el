Strategies to Answer Selected Response  
Questions Anchor Chart

RL.7.1, RI.7.1

(Example for Teacher Reference)

- Read the question very carefully.

- Cover the answers, and try to think of the answer yourself. Then <u>read</u> the answers and find the one that is most like yours.

- As you read through each answer, think back to the text. What happened in the text that makes you think that might be right?

- Cross out answers that you know are incorrect.
