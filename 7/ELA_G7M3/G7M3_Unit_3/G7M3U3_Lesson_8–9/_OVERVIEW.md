# Lessons 8–9: Create a Museum Exhibit: Gather Materials and Draft Curator’s Statement
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 3: Lessons 8–9**

**Summary:** Students gather texts and artwork related by theme for their Harlem Renaissance exhibit. They draft their curator’s statement and the label for the visual piece of their exhibit. Next, students engage in a Tuning Protocol to give and receive feedback on their curator’s statement and label. Finally, 

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, W.7.4, W.7.5, SL.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify themes developed across works of the Harlem Renaissance. (RL.7.2)
- I can write and revise a curator’s statement and label for a museum exhibit on the Harlem Renaissance. (SL.7.4)
- I can offer kind, specific, and helpful feedback to my peers. (W.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Gather Materials – RL.7.2 (20 minutes) → Draft Curator’s Statement and Label – W.7.4, SL.7.4 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
