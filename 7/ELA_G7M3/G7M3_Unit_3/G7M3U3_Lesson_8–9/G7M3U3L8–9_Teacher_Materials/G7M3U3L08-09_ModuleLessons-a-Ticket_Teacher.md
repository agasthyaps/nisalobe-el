Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lessons 8–9

SL.7.4

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, respond to the following prompts.

Recall your work presenting your podcast in the previous module. What are some of the ways you made your presentation effective? How will these techniques apply to presenting your exhibit?

Answers will vary, but may include: Some of the ways to make an oral presentation effective include speaking clearly, making eye contact, and using sentences that are easy to understand. I can apply this to my exhibit to make sure I’m speaking clearly and in a way that shows my excitement about the works I’m presenting.
