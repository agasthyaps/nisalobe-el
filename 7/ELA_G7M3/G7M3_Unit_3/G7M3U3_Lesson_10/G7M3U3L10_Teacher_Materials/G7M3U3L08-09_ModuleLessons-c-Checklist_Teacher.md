Presentation Checklist

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Learning Target**: I can make a presentation.

**Standards I’m Tracking:** SL.7.4, SL.7.5, SL.7.6, L.7.3

| Standard | Characteristics of an Effective Presentation                                                                                      | Characteristics of This Presentation                                                                                                                                                                                                   | Date Completed |
|----------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|----------------|
| SL.7.4   | I present claims and findings, emphasizing salient points.                                                                        | **I clearly introduce the concept that connects the pieces in my exhibit and explain how the works I selected adhere to my unifying theme. It also uses language that shows appreciation and enthusiasm for the works I’ve selected.** |                |
| SL.7.4   | I present claims and findings in a focused, coherent manner.                                                                      | **My presentation organizes my findings into my curator’s statement and labels. Ideas transition smoothly between the sections.**                                                                                                      |                |
| SL.7.4   | I include pertinent descriptions, facts, details, and examples.                                                                   | **My curator’s statement includes the overall theme and the works that are in the exhibit. The labels include details and examples from each work that help my audience understand the connection between the work and the theme.**    |                |
| SL.7.4   | I use appropriate eye contact.                                                                                                    | **While presenting, I make eye contact with my audience in order to increase engagement.**                                                                                                                                             |                |
| SL.7.4   | I use adequate volume.                                                                                                            | **While presenting, I use a loud enough voice so that all can hear me.**                                                                                                                                                               |                |
| SL.7.4   | I use clear pronunciation.                                                                                                        | **While presenting, I am careful to speak clearly.**                                                                                                                                                                                   |                |
| SL.7.5   | I include multimedia components and visual displays in presentations to clarify claims and findings and emphasize salient points. | **My presentation includes a visual piece to help convey the theme I chose.**                                                                                                                                                          |                |
| SL.7.6   | I adapt speech to a variety of contexts and tasks, demonstrating command of formal English when indicated or appropriate.         | **While speaking during my presentation, I use formal English when appropriate and informal English when appropriate.**                                                                                                                |                |
| L.7.3    | I use the conventions of English when speaking.                                                                                   | **While speaking during my presentation, I use standard English when appropriate so everyone can understand me.**                                                                                                                      |                |
