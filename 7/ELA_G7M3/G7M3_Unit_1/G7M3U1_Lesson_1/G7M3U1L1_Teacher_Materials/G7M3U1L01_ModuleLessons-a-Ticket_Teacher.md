Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 1

L.7.4

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, respond to the following prompts.

What do you already know about the words **Harlem** and **Renaissance** and/or the phrase **Harlem Renaissance**?

Answers will vary, but may include: I know that Harlem is a place in New York. I know that there was a renaissance during Medieval times.

What would you like to know about the phrase **Harlem Renaissance**?

Answers will vary, but may include: I would like to know what the Harlem Renaissance is, where it took place, and why it matters.
