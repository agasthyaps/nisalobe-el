# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: The Harlem Renaissance
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students examine texts and artwork to infer the topic of Harlem Renaissance. Finally, they explore the anchor text *One Last Word*, reading the introduction to further their understanding of the Harlem Renaissance.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.1, RI.7.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from the resources. (RL.7.1, RI.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Infer the Topic – RL.7.1, RI.7.1 (15 minutes) → Introduce the Performance Task and Module Guiding Questions – SL.7.1 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
