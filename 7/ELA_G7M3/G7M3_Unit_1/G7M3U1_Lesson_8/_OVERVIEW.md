# Lesson 8: Analyze Structure, Language, and Theme: “Calling Dreams”
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students work as a class to analyze the structure and language in “Calling Dreams.” Finally, students work in pairs to determine a theme and write a paragraph about how it develops over the course of the poem.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, RL.7.4, RL.7.5, L.7.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how the structure of “Calling Dreams” contributes to its meaning. (RL.7.4, RL.7.5)
- I can determine the meaning of figurative language in “Calling Dreams.” (RL.7.4, L.7.5)
- I can identify a theme and explain how it is developed over the course of “Calling Dreams.” (RL.7.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Analyze Structure: “Calling Dreams” – RL.7.5 (20 minutes) → Read and Analyze Language: “Calling Dreams” – RL.7.4, L.7.5c (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
