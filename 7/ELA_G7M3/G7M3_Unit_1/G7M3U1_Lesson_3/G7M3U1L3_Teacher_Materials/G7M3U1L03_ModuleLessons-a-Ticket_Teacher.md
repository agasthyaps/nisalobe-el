Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 3

RL.7.7

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, listen to the song “Love Will Find a Way,” and respond to the prompts below.

1.  How do the music and singing affect the meaning of the words?

The music and singing are slow and have a lot of emotion in them. This makes the written words more dramatic.

2.  What techniques does the singer use to convey the meaning of the words?

The singer sings more strongly at the hopeful parts and quieter at others. This emphasizes the parts where he is expressing hope.
