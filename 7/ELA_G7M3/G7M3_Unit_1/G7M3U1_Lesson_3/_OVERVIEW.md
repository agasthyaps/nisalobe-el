# Lesson 3: Compare Text and Music: *Shuffle Along*’s “Love Will Find a Way”
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students continue exploring the musical *Shuffle Along* by working in pairs to read and analyze the lyrics and accompanying scene of “Love Will Find a Way.” Then students listen to the song and determine musical techniques and their effects on the song’s meaning. Lastly, students determine a theme i

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, RL.7.4, RL.7.7, L.7.5a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of figurative language in “Love Will Find a Way.” (L.7.5a)
- I can compare the written version of “Love Will Find a Way” to its audio version, analyzing the effects of techniques in each medium. (RL.7.7)
- I can identify themes in “Love Will Find a Way.” (RL.7.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.7.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Figurative Language: “Love Will Find a Way” – RL.7.4, L.7.5a (20 minutes) → Compare Text and Music: “Love Will Find a Way”– RL.7.7 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
