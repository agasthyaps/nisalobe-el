(For Teacher Reference)

Students read the lyrics to “Lift Every Voice and Sing” by James Weldon Johnson. Then they analyze the text for figurative language and meaning. Students also listen to the performance of “Lift Every Voice and Sing,” comparing and contrasting the written lyrics to its audio version. Students then respond to a short constructed response question, analyzing the effects of techniques. Students are required to include textual evidence to support their responses throughout the assessment.
