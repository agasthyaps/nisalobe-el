# Lessons 5–6: Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Versions of “Lift Every Voice and Sing”
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lessons 5–6**

**Summary:** Students complete their mid-unit assessment by answering selected and constructed response questions to analyze and compare the text and music versions of “Lift Every Voice and Sing.” Students participate in a Language Dive, deeply exploring the language structures in a line of the poem. **Focus Standards:** RL.7.1, RL.7.2, RL.7.4, RL.7.7, L.7.5a, L.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of figurative language in “Lift Every Voice and Sing.” (RL.7.4, L.7.5)
- I can compare and contrast the written version of “Lift Every Voice and Sing” to its audio version, analyzing the effects of techniques in each medium. (RL.7.7)
- I can identify themes in “Lift Every Voice and Sing.” (RL.7.2)
- I can select a research reading text that I want to read.

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Versions of “Lift Every Voice and Sing” (30 minutes) → Track Progress (10 minutes) → Language Dive: “Lift Every Voice and Sing,” Lines 20–22 – RL.7.4 (10 minutes)
