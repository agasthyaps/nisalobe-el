# Lessons 10–11: End of Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Structure, Language, and Theme: “I Shall Return”
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lessons 10–11**

**Summary:** Students complete their end of unit assessment by answering selected and constructed response questions to analyze the structure, language, and theme of the poem “I Shall Return.” Then students synthesize their analysis by completing a note-catcher in preparation for the discussion in the following 

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.1, RL.7.2, RL.7.4, RL.7.5, L.7.4, L.7.5c, L.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how the structure of “I Shall Return” contributes to its meaning. (RL.7.4, RL.7.5)
- I can determine the meaning of figurative language in “I Shall Return.” (RL.7.4, L.7.5)
- I can identify a theme and explain how it is developed over the course of “I Shall Return.” (RL.7.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Review End of Unit 3 Assessment (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Structure, Language, and Theme: “I Shall Return” (30 minutes) → Track Progress – RL.7.4 (10 minutes)
