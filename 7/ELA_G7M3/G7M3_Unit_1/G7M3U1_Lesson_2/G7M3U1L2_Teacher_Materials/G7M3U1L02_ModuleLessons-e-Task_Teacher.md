33. Write a brief response comparing the text and music versions of “Election Day.” How do the techniques in each medium affect the meaning of the song? (RL.7.7)

“Election Day” is a song about the excitement people feel about choosing new politicians. The text and music both highlight this excitement with their own techniques. For example, the text repeats the phrase “election day” to highlight the excitement. Also, the singer pauses on the phrase “election day” to show excitement. The text also talks about how even though it is “dark and cold,” people will still be excited on election day. Additionally, the singer sings “hooray” loudly to show his excitement. These techniques all combine to show the energy and excitement of the song.
