# Lesson 2: Compare Text and Music: *Shuffle Along*’s “Election Day”
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students begin to explore the iconic musical theater show *Shuffle Along* by working as a class to read and analyze the lyrics and accompanying scene of “Election Day.” Finally, students listen to the song and determine musical techniques and their effects on the song’s meaning.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.4, RL.7.7

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the impact of repetition on the first scene in *Shuffle Along*. (RL.7.4)
- I can compare the written version of “Election Day” to its audio version, analyzing the effects of techniques in each medium. (RL.7.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.7.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Build Background Knowledge: *Shuffle Along* – RI.7.1 (10 minutes) → Read and Analyze “Election Day” – RL.7.4 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
