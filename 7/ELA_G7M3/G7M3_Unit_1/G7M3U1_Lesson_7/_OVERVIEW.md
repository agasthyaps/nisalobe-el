# Lesson 7: Analyze Structure, Language, and Theme: “The Negro Speaks of Rivers”
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students work as a class to analyze the structure, language, and themes in “The Negro Speaks of Rivers.” Next, students work as a class to determine a theme and write a paragraph about how it develops over the course of the poem. **Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, RL.7.4, RL.7.5, L.7.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how the structure of “The Negro Speaks of Rivers” contributes to its meaning. (RL.7.4, RL.7.5)
- I can determine the meaning of figurative language in “The Negro Speaks of Rivers.” (RL.7.4, L.7.5)
- I can identify a theme and explain how it develops over the course of “The Negro Speaks of Rivers.” (RL.7.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Analyze Structure: “The Negro Speaks of Rivers” – RL.7.5 (20 minutes) → Read and Analyze Language and Theme: “The Negro Speaks of Rivers” – RL.7.2, RL.7.4 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
