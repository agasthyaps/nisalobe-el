Collaborative Discussion: Analyze Structure, Language, and Theme in Poetry and Art

RL.7.2, RL.7.4, RL.7.5, L.7.5, SL.7.1

**Name:** **Date:**

**Directions:** In a group (size determined by your teacher) discuss the following prompts:

**Prompt:** How do the poems “The Negro Speaks of Rivers” and “I Shall Return” develop similar themes? How are these themes related to the artworks *Ethiopia Awakening* and *African Phantasy: Awakening*?

Discuss the poems “The Negro Speaks of Rivers” and “I Shall Return” and the artworks *Ethiopia Awakening* and *African Phantasy: Awakening*. How does the language and the structure of the poems create meaning? What are the thematic connections between the poems and the artworks? Be sure to demonstrate your discussion skills and to share, expand, and learn from their ideas.

As you discuss, remember to

- refer to specific parts of the poems and artwork to support your ideas.

- wait and listen when others are speaking; allow others to speak.

- ask and answer questions with relevant information.

- bring the discussion back to the topics of these prompts as necessary.

- acknowledge new information and modify your views as necessary.
