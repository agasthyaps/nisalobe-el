Card Swap Cards

L.7.4

**Teacher Directions:** Cut apart the cards ahead of time, and provide one card per student. Students will meet with a partner. They will read the word and the definition, and create a sentence using the word. Then, students will hear their partner’s word, definition, and sentence. Then, students swap cards with their partners and find a new partner.
