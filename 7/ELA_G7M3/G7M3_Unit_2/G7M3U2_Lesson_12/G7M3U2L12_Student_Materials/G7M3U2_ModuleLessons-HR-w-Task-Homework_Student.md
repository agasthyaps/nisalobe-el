**Other Definitions:**

**phrase:** group of words that modifies or tells more about a noun, verb, or adverb in a sentence

**independent clause:** group of words with a subject and verb that tells a complete thought

**dependent clause:** group of words with a subject and verb that doesn’t tell a complete thought; modifies or tells more about the independent clause
