# Lesson 12: Write a Literary Argument Essay: Draft Conclusion
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** Students use the Painted Essay® structure to more deeply analyze the conclusion of the model literary argument essay. Next, students participate in a Language Dive about the conclusion of the model literary argument essay. Finally, students draft the conclusion of their pair literary argument essay.

**Focus Standards:** W.7.1e, W.7.5, L.7.1b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write the conclusion of my argument essay, restating the main claim and adding a reflection. (W.7.1e)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.1b (10 minutes) → Work Time → The Painted Essay®: Sort and Color-Code the Parts of the Conclusion – W.7.1e (10 minutes) → Language Dive: Model Argument Essay, Conclusion – W.7.1e (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
