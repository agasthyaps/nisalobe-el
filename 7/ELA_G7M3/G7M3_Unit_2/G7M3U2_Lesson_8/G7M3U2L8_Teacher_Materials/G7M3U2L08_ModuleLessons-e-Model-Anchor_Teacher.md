W.7.1

(For Teacher Reference)

**Introduction**

- Introduction that catches the readerâ€™s attention and gives context

- The introduction includes a main claim that states an opinion that can be proven or disproven with evidence and reasoning

**Proof Paragraphs**

- States a point

- Provides evidence and reasoning from the text(s) that support the point

- Elaboration/Reasoning: What do these facts and evidence show? Why?

- Restatement and/or reflection about how this point, evidence, and reasoning support the main claim

**Conclusion**

- What? Restates the main claim and most important evidence and reasoning

- So what? Reflects on the larger meaning behind the main claim
