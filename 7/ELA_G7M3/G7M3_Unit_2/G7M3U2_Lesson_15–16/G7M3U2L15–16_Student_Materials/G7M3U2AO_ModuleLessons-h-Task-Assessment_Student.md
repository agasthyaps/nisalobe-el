**Remember the following definitions:**

| Sentence Types                |                                                                                     |
|-------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **simple sentence**           | one independent clause                                                              |
| **compound sentence**         | two or more independent clauses; joined by a comma and a conjunction or a semicolon |
| **complex sentence**          | one independent clause with one or more dependent clause                            |
| **compound-complex sentence** | two or more independent clauses with one or more dependent clause                   |

**Other Definitions:**

**phrase:** a group of words that modifies or tells more about a noun, verb, or adverb in a sentence

**independent clause:** a group of words with a subject and verb that tells a complete thought

**dependent clause:** a group of words with a subject and verb that doesnâ€™t tell a complete thought; modifies or tells more about the independent clause
