**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 2: Lessons 13–14**

**Summary:** Students complete the first part of their end of unit assessment by independently planning and writing a literary argument about how three works from the Harlem Renaissance most clearly develop the theme of dreams giving life purpose and meaning.
