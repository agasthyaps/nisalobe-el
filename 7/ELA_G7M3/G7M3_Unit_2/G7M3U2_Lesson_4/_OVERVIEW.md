# Lesson 4: Close Read: “The Boy and the Bayonet,” Part I
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students are led in a close read of the first part of the short story “The Boy and the Bayonet.” In the close read, students explore how story elements interact and how the author develops and contrasts points of view of different characters. After the close read, students explore the themes emergin

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.3, RL.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can explain how the setting shapes the characters and plot in “The Boy and the Bayonet.” (RL.7.3)
- I can identify the points of view of Bud, Hannah, and “little sister” in “The Boy and the Bayonet.” (RL.7.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Close Read: “The Boy and the Bayonet” – RL.7.3, RL.7.6 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Explore Themes: “The Boy and the Bayonet” – RL.7.2 (5 minutes)
