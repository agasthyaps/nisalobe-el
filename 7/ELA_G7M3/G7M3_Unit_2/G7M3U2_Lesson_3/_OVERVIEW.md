# Lesson 3: Read and Analyze “His Motto,” Part II
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students finish reading “His Motto” and analyze how story elements interact and how the author develops and contrasts points of view. Finally, students analyze the themes and their development as well as the habits of character they see displayed by the characters in the story.

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.2, RL.7.3, RL.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can explain how the setting shapes the characters and plot in “His Motto.” (RL.7.3)
- I can explain how an author develops and contrasts the points of view of Durmond, Hilton, and the “youth” in “His Motto.” (RL.7.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Analyze “His Motto” – RL.7.3, RL.7.6 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Analyze Themes: “His Motto” – RL.7.2 (5 minutes)
