**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** Students use the Painted Essay® structure to more deeply analyze the introduction of the model literary argument essay. **Learning Targets:**
- I can write an introduction for my essay giving context on the Harlem Renaissance, acknowledging a counterclaim, and clearly stating the main claim of the piece. (W.7.1a)
