# Lesson 10: Write a Literary Argument Essay: Draft Proof Paragraph 1
**Grade 7: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** Students sort sentences from the model literary argument essay by type of sentence. Next, they draft the first Proof Paragraph of their pair literary argument essay, incorporating different types of sentences. Finally, students share their best evidence from their first Proof Paragraph in small grou

**Focus Standards:** RL.7.1, W.7.1b, L.7.1b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write Proof Paragraph 1 of my pair argument essay using logical reasoning and relevant evidence from the text to support my point. (W.7.1b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.1a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Sort Sentences from the Proof Paragraphs – L.7.1b (15 minutes) → Pairs: Draft Proof Paragraph 1 – W.7.1b (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
