Entrance Ticket: Unit 2, Lesson 2

RL.7.4

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the following quote from the story “His Motto,” which you will read today. Then, respond to the prompt below the quote.

“I WILL STUDY AND MAKE READY, AND MAYBE MY CHANCE WILL COME.”

What does this quote mean to you? What does it suggest about the story you’ll be reading today?

Answers will vary, but may include: This quote means that it is important to be prepared for when your opportunity comes. It suggests that the story has a character who works hard waiting for an opportunity to arrive.

Source: Dixon, Lottie Burell. “His Motto.” *The Upward Path*. Ed. Myron T. Pritchard and Mary White Covington. Harcourt, Brace, and Howe, 1920. *Project Gutenberg*. Web.
