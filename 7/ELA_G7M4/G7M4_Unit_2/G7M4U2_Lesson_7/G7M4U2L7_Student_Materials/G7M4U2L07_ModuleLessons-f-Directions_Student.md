W.7.1

**Name:** **Date:**

**Write a Pair Argument Essay**

Throughout this unit, you will work with a partner to plan and write an essay to address this prompt: Write an essay supporting the claim that we should reduce plastic pollution by targeting the end of the plastic life cycle.

**REMEMBER:** A well-written, argument essay

- clearly states a main claim and stays focused throughout the essay;

- acknowledges alternative or opposing claims;

- uses accurate and relevant examples, details, and quotations to support points;

- explains the evidence and reasoning;

- groups information logically, in a way that makes your thinking clear to the reader;

- follows the standard rules of writing (spelling, punctuation, and grammar); and

- uses a formal style.

Use the following documents to assist you in writing your essay:

- Texts from Module 4, Units 1 and 2: *A Plastic Ocean*, *Trash Vortex*, “Five Weird Materials That Could Replace Plastic,” “Five Things You Can Do to End Plastic Pollution,” and “Boyan Slat: The Great Pacific Garbage Patch Kid.”

- Argument Writing Plan graphic organizer

- Vocabulary log (from Module 1, Unit 1, Lesson 2, Work Time A)

- Online or print dictionaries (including ELL and home language dictionaries)
