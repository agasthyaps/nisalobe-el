# Lesson 10: Write an Argument Essay: Draft Proof Paragraph 2
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** As a class, students study the model argument essay for transitional phrases and clauses. Then, students work with a partner to draft Proof Paragraph 2 of their pair argument essay.

**Focus Standards:** W.7.1c, L.7.2a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write Proof Paragraph 2 of my pair argument essay with phrases and clauses that show the relationship between the points, reasons, and evidence. (W.7.1c)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.7.2a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Transitional Phrases and Clauses – W.7.1c (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Pairs: Draft Proof Paragraph 2 – W.7.1c (30 minutes)
