Debate Directions

SL.7.3, SL.7.4

**Name:** **Date:**

**Debate Question:**

Which part of the plastic life cycle is the best place to target to reduce plastic pollution?

**Directions:**

1.  The Moderator states the prompt. (30 seconds)

**Present Positions and Cross-Examine**

2.  The Beginning Position Presenter states the position. (2 minutes)

3.  The Middle and End Cross-Examiners ask questions that the Responder on the Beginning team answers. (2 minutes)

4.  The Middle Position Presenter states the position. (2 minutes)

5.  The Beginning and End Cross-Examiners ask questions that the Responder on the Middle team answers. (2 minutes)

6.  The End Position Presenter states the position. (2 minutes)

7.  The Beginning and Middle Cross-Examiners ask questions that the Responder on the End team answers. (2 minutes)

**Rebuttals and Summaries**

8.  The Beginning Summarizer states their closing rebuttal and summary. (2 minutes)

9.  The Middle Summarizer states their closing rebuttal and summary. (2 minutes)

10. The End Summarizer states their closing rebuttal and summary. (2 minutes)

11. The Moderator concludes the debate. (30 seconds)
