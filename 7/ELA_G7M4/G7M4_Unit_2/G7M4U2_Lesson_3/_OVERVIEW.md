# Lesson 3: Analyze How Authors Present Evidence Differently in Articles
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students form small groups to discuss and compare authors’ use of evidence in “Five Things You Can Do to End Plastic Pollution” and “Five Weird Materials That Could Replace Plastic.” Then students continue working on the plastic life cycle debate, learning how to make a rebuttal and summary.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, RI.7.9, SL.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare how two authors emphasize different evidence when presenting information on plastic pollution. (RI.7.9)
- I can present my claims about plastic pollution in a clear way, supported by evidence. (SL.7.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Discuss to Compare Evidence – RI.7.9 (15 minutes) → Answer Questions to Compare Evidence – RI.7.9 (10 minutes) → Debate Rebuttal and Summary Practice – SL.7.4 (10 minutes)
