**Introduction**

- Includes a main claim that states an opinion that can be proven or disproven with evidence and reasoning

**Proof Paragraphs**

- State a point

- Provide evidence and reasoning from the text(s) that support the point

- Provide elaboration and reasoning: What do these facts and evidence show? Why?

- Acknowledge alternative or opposing claims

- Restate and/or reflect on how the point, evidence, and reasoning support the main claim

**Conclusion**

- What? Restates the main claim and most important evidence and reasoning

- So what? Reflects on the larger meaning behind the main claim
