# Lesson 4: Analyze How Authors Present Evidence Differently in Articles and *Trash Vortex*
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students independently answer questions to analyze how authors present evidence differently in articles and in *Trash Vortex* in preparation for the mid-unit assessment. Then students practice all aspects of the plastic life cycle debate: presenting a position, asking and answering cross-examination

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.9, W.7.1, SL.7.3, SL.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare how two authors emphasize different evidence when presenting information on plastic pollution. (RI.7.9)
- I can delineate and evaluate a speaker’s claims about plastic pollution. (SL.7.3)
- I can present my claims about plastic pollution in a clear way, supported by evidence. (SL.7.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Compare Evidence – RI.7.9 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Practice Debate – SL.7.3, SL.7.4 (25 minutes)
