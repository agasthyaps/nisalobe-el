# Lessons 5–6: Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Analyze How Authors Present Evidence Differently
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 2: Lessons 5–6**

**Summary:** Students complete their mid-unit assessment by reading a new article, “Boyan Slat: The Great Pacific Garbage Patch Kid,” and answering selected and constructed response questions to analyze the development of central idea, author’s point of view and purpose, and how authors present evidence differen

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.2, RI.7.4, RI.7.6, RI.7.9, L.7.4, L.7.5b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how authors treat the same topic differently by looking at how they present evidence and interpret facts. (RI.7.9)
- I can delineate and evaluate a speaker’s claims. (SL.7.3)
- I can present my claims in a clear way, supported by evidence. (SL.7.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Analyze How Authors Present Evidence Differently (40 minutes) → Track Progress (10 minutes) → Language Dive: “Boyan Slat: The Great Pacific Garbage Patch Kid” – RI.7.6 (10 minutes)
