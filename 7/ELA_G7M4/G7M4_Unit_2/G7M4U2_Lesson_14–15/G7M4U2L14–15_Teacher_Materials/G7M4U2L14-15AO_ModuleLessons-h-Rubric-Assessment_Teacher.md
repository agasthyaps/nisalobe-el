End of Unit 2 Assessment, Part II: Revise an Argument Essay

Students revise the draft of their argument essays about reducing plastic pollution in the middle of the plastic life cycle, focusing on the proper placement of modifying phrases and the use of commas with coordinate adjectives. Students who used a word processor to write their pieces will revise using the same tool. Direct them to use track changes to show their revisions.

Use the rubric and the changes highlighted on the draft to assess student progress.

|                                      | Advanced                                                                                             | Proficient                                                        | Developing                                              | Beginning              |
|--------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|------------------------|
| Revises for Rhetorical Effectiveness | Makes revisions suggested as well as additional revisions that improve the quality of the piece      | Responds thoroughly to feedback, making most revisions suggested  | Responds to feedback, making some revisions suggested   | Few or no changes made |
| Edits for Conventions                | Makes corrections suggested and identifies and corrects other errors in grammar, usage, or mechanics | Responds positively to feedback, making all corrections suggested | Responds to feedback, making most corrections suggested | Few or no changes made |
