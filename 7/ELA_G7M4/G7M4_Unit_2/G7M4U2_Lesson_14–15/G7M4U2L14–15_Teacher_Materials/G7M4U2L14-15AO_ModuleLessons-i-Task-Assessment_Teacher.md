Part I

**Directions:** Choose the two phrases that go together to make a complete sentence without a dangling modifier. Combine the phrases to make the sentence on the line below. Write the new combined sentence on the line provided, adjusting capitalization as necessary. Remember that a modifying phrase describes a noun (person, place, thing, or idea). Dangling modifiers are phrases that are not properly associated with the noun they modify. (L.7.1, L.7.1c)

1.  plastic pollution can be reduced

by buying other materials as much as possible

we can help ensure a future that is free of plastic pollution

Answers will vary. Possible response: By buying other materials as much as possible, we can help ensure a future that is free of plastic pollution.

2.  having the responsibility to ban plastic

plastic pollution will end

governments should act now

Answers will vary. Possible response: Because they have the power to ban plastic, governments should act now.

3.  scientists and activists are finding new ways to clean up the ocean

although they encounter challenges

the waters may someday return to its natural state

Answers will vary. Possible response: Although they encounter challenges, scientists and activists are finding new ways to clean up the ocean.
