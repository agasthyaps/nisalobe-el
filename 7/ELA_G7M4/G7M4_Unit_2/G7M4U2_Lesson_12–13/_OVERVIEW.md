# Lessons 12–13: End of Unit 2 Assessment, Part I: Write an Argument Essay
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 2: Lessons 12–13**

**Summary:** Students complete the first part of their end of unit assessment by independently planning and writing an argument essay about the middle of the plastic life cycle being the best place to reduce plastic pollution.

**Focus Standards:** W.7.1, W.7.5, W.7.6, W.7.9b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan and write an argument essay about addressing plastic pollution in the middle of the plastic life cycle. (W.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 2 Assessment, Part I: Write an Argument Essay (75 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – W.7.1 (10 minutes)
