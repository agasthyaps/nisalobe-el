Homework: Reflect on Writing Process

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Respond to the following prompts.

1.  How has your writing improved from planning to writing to revision?

Answers will vary, but may include: I used to not know how to begin. Now my piece has a strong introduction, and I have included more facts, examples, and details in my writing.

2.  What are your strengths as a writer?

Answers will vary, but may include: I am good at writing for a particular purpose and addressing an audience in my writing.

3.  What are your goals as a writer?

Answers will vary, but may include: I am learning how to write to support a claim.
