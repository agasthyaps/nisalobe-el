# Lessons 11–12: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Pitch a Documentary Clip
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 3: Lessons 11–12**

**Summary:** Students work in their triads to practice their documentary pitch, focusing on the effective use of presentation skills, visuals, and claims and findings. Then students complete their end of unit assessment by delivering their documentary pitch presentation. Students self-assess on their presentatio

**Focus Standards:** SL.7.4, SL.7.5, SL.7.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can present my documentary pitch in a formal, focused and clear way, emphasizing important points. (SL.7.4, SL.7.6)
- I can use appropriate eye contact, adequate volume, and clear pronunciation to present. (SL.7.4)
- I can use visual displays in my storyboard to clarify and emphasize points. (SL.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.7.4 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Practice Documentary Clip Pitch – SL.7.4 (10 minutes) → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Pitch a Documentary Clip (45 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
