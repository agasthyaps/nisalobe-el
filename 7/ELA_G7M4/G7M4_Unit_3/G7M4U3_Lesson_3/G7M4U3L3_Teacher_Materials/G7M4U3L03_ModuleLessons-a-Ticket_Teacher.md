Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 3

L.7.3a

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, read the following learning target, and respond to the questions below:

- I can choose language that expresses ideas precisely and concisely, recognizing and eliminating wordiness and redundancy.

Use context, background knowledge, and if necessary, a dictionary, to define the following words:

**precisely:**

with total accuracy; exactly

**concisely:**

with few words; short and to the point

**eliminating:**

leaving out; removing

**wordiness:**

using too many words

**redundancy:**

unnecessary repetition of words

Restate the learning target in your own words:

Answers will vary, but may include: use language that tells your ideas clearly and concisely, cutting out any extra or repeated words.

Reread the narrative lead of your documentary script. Make notes in the margins to indicate where you could write more precisely or concisely or eliminate wordiness and redundancy.

Answers will vary.
