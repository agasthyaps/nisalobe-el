# Lesson 2: Create a Documentary Clip: Draft a Narrative Lead
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students analyze the narrative lead in the Model Documentary Script, determining the writing elements (hook, point of view, description, etc.) and their effects on engaging the audience and giving context. Then each member of the triad independently drafts a narrative lead for their documentary scri

**Focus Standards:** W.7.3, W.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine criteria for an effective narrative lead in a documentary. (W.7.3)
- I can write the narrative lead for a documentary script using effective techniques and relevant descriptive details. (W.7.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.7.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model Narrative Lead in a Documentary Script – W.7.3 (10 minutes) → Draft a Narrative Lead for a Documentary Script – W.7.3 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
