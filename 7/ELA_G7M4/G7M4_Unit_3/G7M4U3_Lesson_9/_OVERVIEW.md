# Lesson 9: Pitch a Documentary Clip: Create a Storyboard
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** Students work independently to create their panels for their storyboard, collaborating with their triad members as necessary. Then students share their best panel with their triad, explaining what makes it an effective representation of their plan for the documentary film clip.

**Focus Standards:** SL.7.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can create a storyboard with images, captions, and my documentary clip script on plastic pollution. (SL.7.4, SL.7.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.7.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Create a Storyboard – SL.7.5 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Share Storyboard – SL.7.5 (5 minutes)
