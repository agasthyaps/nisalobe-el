# Lesson 1: Create a Documentary Clip: Analyze a Model Documentary
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students watch a film clip of *A Plastic Ocean* to review documentary film techniques and their effect on developing meaning. **Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, RI.7.7, SL.7.1, SL.7.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can explore a model documentary and generate ideas for my own documentary clip. (RI.7.7)
- I can determine the purpose and analyze the content of an effective documentary script about plastic pollution. (SL.7.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Review Documentary Techniques – RI.7.7 (10 minutes) → Analyze a Model Documentary Script – SL.7.4 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
