(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Answer the questions below about the clip you viewed in this lesson from the film *A Plastic Ocean*.

1.  What techniques did you see in this clip?

There are several images of Craig trying and failing to get food without plastic; voiceover; music; text to introduce the speakers and places; and graphics to illustrate straws in the ocean.

2.  How do you think the filmmakers did and did not use a script to plan and prepare for this clip? What parts could the filmmakers’ script and which parts could they not script?

The filmmakers likely used a script for their voiceover narration. Greg most likely planned on asking people at restaurants for different containers from plastic; he couldn’t have scripted those parts because he didn’t know what the other people would say.
