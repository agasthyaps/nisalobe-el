**Summary:** Students form partnerships to watch and analyze a clip of the film *A Plastic Ocean*, identifying central ideas and comparing the portrayal of a subject in the transcript to the portrayal in the film. **Focus Standards:** RI.7.7, SL.7.3

**Learning Targets:**
- I can compare the way a transcript of *A Plastic Ocean* portrays a subject to the way it is portrayed in the video. (RI.7.7)
- I can delineate a speaker’s argument and claims in *A Plastic Ocean*. (SL.7.3)
- I can evaluate a speaker’s evidence and reasoning in *A Plastic Ocean*. (SL.7.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.7.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Watch and Analyze the Video – RI.7.7 (15 minutes) → Delineate and Evaluate a Speaker’s Argument – SL.7.3 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
