# Lesson 8: Analyze Author’s Purpose: *Trash Vortex*, Chapter 1
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** As a class, students explore the anchor text *Trash Vortex*. Then they read and analyze an excerpt of chapter 1 of *Trash Vortex*, identifying central ideas and author’s purpose. Students then engage in a collaborative discussion to compare content and purpose in the documentary film *A Plastic Ocea

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine two or more central ideas and analyze their development over the course of *Trash Vortex*. (RI.7.2)
- I can determine an author’s purpose in *Trash Vortex*. (RI.7.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Launch Anchor Text: *Trash Vortex* – RI.7.2 (20 minutes) → Analyze Author’s Purpose – RI.7.6 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
