# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: Plastic Pollution
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students embark on a scavenger hunt to infer the topic of plastic pollution. Then they explore a clip of the documentary film *A Plastic Ocean*, watching the introduction to further their understanding of the plastic pollution.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.1, SL.7.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from the scavenger hunt and film. (RI.7.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Scavenger Hunt – RI.7.1 (20 minutes) → Launch the Video: *A Plastic Ocean* – RI.7.1 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
