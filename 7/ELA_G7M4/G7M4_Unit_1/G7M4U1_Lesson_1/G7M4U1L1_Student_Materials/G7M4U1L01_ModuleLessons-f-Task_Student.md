**Norms**

- I stay with my group.

- I take turns recording answers on the note-catcher.

- I hear all voices by listening carefully and waiting for my turn to speak.

- I collaborate with my group by asking questions, making comments, sharing ideas, connecting my ideas to others’ ideas, and responding to questions.

- I focus on the scavenger hunt and on the tasks, information cards, and note-catcher.

- I assume a positive intent.

- I take initiative and responsibility, taking the lead when necessary as well as allowing others to lead.

- I am responsible and safe with technology.

**Directions:** Complete as many of these tasks and read as many of these information cards as you can in 15 minutes.
