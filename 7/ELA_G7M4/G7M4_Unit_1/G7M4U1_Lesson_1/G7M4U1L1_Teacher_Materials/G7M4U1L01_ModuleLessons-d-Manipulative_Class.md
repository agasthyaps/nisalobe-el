Scavenger Hunt Resources: School Version

RI.7.1

**Note to Teacher:** Cut apart the Task and Information cards. Post the cards around the school. Provide a map of the school that shows where the task cards and information cards are located. Write the text in large letters on chart paper to make it easy to read. Leave a note by the task cards and informational cards that says, “This is for a classroom activity. Please do not move this card.”
