# Lesson 2: Watch and Analyze *A Plastic Ocean*: Size of the Problem
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students continue to explore a clip of the documentary film *A Plastic Ocean* by working as a class to identify the central ideas and to compare the way the transcript portrays a subject to the film’s portrayal. Then students have a silent discussion in writing on habits of character seen in the fil

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.7, SL.7.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify the main ideas in a clip of *A Plastic Ocean* and explain how they are conveyed. (SL.7.2)
- I can compare the way a transcript of *A Plastic Ocean* portrays a subject to the way it is portrayed in the video. (RI.7.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.7.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Watch and Analyze the Video – SL.7.2 (20 minutes) → Compare Transcript and Video – RI.7.7 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
