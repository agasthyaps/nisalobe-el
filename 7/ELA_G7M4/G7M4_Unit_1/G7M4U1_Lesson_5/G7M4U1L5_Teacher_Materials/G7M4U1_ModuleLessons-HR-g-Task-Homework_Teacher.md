Homework: Correctly Place Modifiers

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Choose the two phrases that go together to make a complete sentence without a dangling modifier. Combine the phrases to make the sentence on the line below, adjusting capitalization as necessary. (L.7.1c)

1.  people volunteered to help

once they were informed of the nurdles

the beach became clean

Once they were informed of the nurdles, people volunteered to help.

2.  confused by the plastic in the ocean

it often gets eaten

sea creatures often accidentally eat it

Confused by the plastic in the ocean, sea creatures often accidentally eat it.

3.  many people have started recycling

hoping to reduce plastic pollution

recycling has become popular

Hoping to reduce plastic pollution, many people have started recycling.
