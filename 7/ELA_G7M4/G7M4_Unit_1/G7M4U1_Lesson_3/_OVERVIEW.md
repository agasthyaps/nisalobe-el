# Lesson 3: Watch and Analyze *A Plastic Ocean*: Great Pacific Garbage Patch
**Grade 7: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students form small groups to watch and analyze a clip of the film *A Plastic Ocean*, identifying the central ideas and comparing the portrayal of a subject in the transcript to the portrayal of a subject in the film. Students then answer selected response questions about the film.

**Focus Standards:** RI.7.7, SL.7.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify the main ideas in a clip of *A Plastic Ocean* and explain how they are conveyed. (SL.7.2)
- I can compare the way a transcript of *A Plastic Ocean* portrays a subject to the way it is portrayed in the video. (RI.7.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.7.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Watch and Analyze the Video – SL.7.2 (15 minutes) → Compare Transcript and Video – RI.7.7 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
