L.8.1

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following questions.

This is a fragment:

> **To our surprise.**

What do you think a fragment is?

A fragment is an incomplete sentence. It lacks a verb or subject.

How would you revise this not to be a fragment?

Student answers will vary, but might be along the lines of, To our surprise, the donkey started speaking.
