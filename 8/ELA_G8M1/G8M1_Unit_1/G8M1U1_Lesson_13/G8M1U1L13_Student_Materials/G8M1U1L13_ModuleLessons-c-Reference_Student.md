Synopsis: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 11

**Name:** **Date:**

- Teresita’s husband draws them a map to Abuelita’s, and they begin their journey.

- The girls encounter a donkey and cart.

- Pita says that she can understand the donkey and he is talking to her.

- Odilia does not trust the donkey and thinks of Teresita’s warning about the nagual, but the other sisters trust him.

- They get on the donkey for a break in walking.

- The donkey takes them to a cave, and they are fearful—they realize the donkey is the sinister nagual.

- The nagual changes into his true form, and the sisters pass out outside of the cave under his spell.
