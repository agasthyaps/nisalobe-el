# Lesson 13: Analyze Aspects of Character: *Summer of the Mariposas*
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students begin to analyze how incidents in the text, and the way in which characters respond, reveal aspects of character.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.3

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 11 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can analyze how incidents in a story reveal aspects of character (RL.8.1, RL.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 11 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Analyze Aspects of Character – RL.8.3 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Homework
