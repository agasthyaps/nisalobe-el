# Lesson 2: Establish Reading Routines: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 1
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students are introduced to the guiding questions of the module, are introduced to and begin to use their vocabulary logs, and begin to read their class novel, finding the gist of the first chapter excerpt.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.4, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can show empathy and respect as I listen to my classmates. (SL.8.1)
- I can find the gist of the chapter 1 excerpt of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can determine the difference between academic and domain-specific vocabulary. (RL.8.4, L.8.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Launch Vocabulary Logs – RL.8.4 (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Reflect on the Module Guiding Questions (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read Aloud and Identify Vocabulary: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 1 Excerpt – RL.8.4 (15 minutes)
