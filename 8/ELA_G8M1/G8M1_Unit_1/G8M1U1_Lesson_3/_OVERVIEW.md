# Lesson 3: Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapters 1–2
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their class novel, finding gist and identifying unknown vocabulary, and are introduced to how to analyze point of view in the novel.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.6, SL.8.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify strategies to answer selected response questions.
- I can find the gist of chapter 2 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can explain what effect is created by differences in the points of view of the characters and the reader in the chapter 2 excerpt of *Summer of the Mariposas*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 2 Excerpt – SL.8.1 (15 minutes) → Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapters 1–2 Excerpts – RL.8.6 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Exit Ticket: Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 2 Excerpt – RL.8.6 (5 minutes)
