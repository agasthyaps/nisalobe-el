Synopsis: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 8

**Name:** **Date:**

- The girls second-guess their actions so far and worry Mama is in trouble with the FBI and police.

- They try to get to Abuelita’s house so they can call Mama.

- Their car breaks down and they start walking.

- They become entranced by Cecilia—a woman who lures them into her house and offers them rest, sweets to eat, and beautiful clothing.

- They see on a news report that the investigation to find them is underway and getting closer.

- Odilia realizes something is not right but has become very drowsy from the sweets that Cecilia gave.

- Odilia calls out for La Llorona as she begins to fall asleep.
