# Lesson 6: Launch Independent Reading
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their class novel and are introduced to independent research reading.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.4, RI.8.10, L.8.4, L.8.4a, L.8.4c, L.8.4d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of new vocabulary. (RL.8.4, L.8.4)
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 4 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can select a research reading text that I want to read. (RI.8.10)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 4 Excerpt (20 minutes) → Launch Independent Research Reading – RI.8.10 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
