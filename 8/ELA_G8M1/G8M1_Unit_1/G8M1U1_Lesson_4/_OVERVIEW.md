# Lesson 4: Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 3
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their class novel, find the gist of chapter 3, identify unknown vocabulary, and analyze point of view.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.4, RL.8.6, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify strategies to determine the meaning of unfamiliar vocabulary. (RL.8.4, L.8.4)
- I can find the gist of chapter 3 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can explain what effect is created by differences in the points of view of the characters and the reader in the chapter 3 excerpt of *Summer of the Mariposas*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Strategies to Determine the Meaning of Unfamiliar Vocabulary – RL.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 3 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 3 Excerpt – RL.8.6 (15 minutes)
