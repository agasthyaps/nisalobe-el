Complete the sentence frames to create similes.

I feel **brave** as **a lion ruling the jungle**.

My family is **smiling** like **they just won the lottery**.

Grade 8 is as[^1] **tiring** as **running all day long**.

[^1]: Note: sometimes **as** is used before an adjective in the first part of a simile, in addition to the second **as** when making a comparison.
