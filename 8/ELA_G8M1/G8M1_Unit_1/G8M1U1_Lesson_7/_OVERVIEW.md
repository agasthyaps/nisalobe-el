# Lesson 7: Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 5
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their class novel, find the gist of chapter 5, identify unknown vocabulary, and analyze point of view.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of new vocabulary. (RL.8.4, L.8.4b, L.8.6)
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 5 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can explain what effect is created by differences in the points of view of the characters and the reader in the chapter 5 excerpt of *Summer of the Mariposas*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 5 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Analyze Point of View: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 5 Excerpt – RL.8.6 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
