# Lesson 12: Introduce Themes: *Summer of the Mariposas*
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read chapter 10 of their class novel, are introduced to theme, and begin to identify themes in their class novel.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 10 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can identify themes in *Summer of the Mariposas* and how they have developed over the course of the text. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 10 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Introduce Themes: *Summer of the Mariposas* – RL.8.2 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Homework
