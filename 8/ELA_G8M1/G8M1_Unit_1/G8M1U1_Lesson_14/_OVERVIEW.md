# Lesson 14: Analyze Aspects of Character and Generate Discussion Norms: *Summer of the Mariposas*
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 1: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students analyze aspects of character and prepare for a text-based discussion.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.3, W.8.8, SL.8.1, SL.8.1a, SL.8.1b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can quote accurately from the text. (W.8.8)
- I can analyze how incidents in *Summer of the Mariposas* reveal aspects of the characters. (RL.8.1, RL.8.3, SL.8.1)
- I can generate norms for a productive discussion. (SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.8 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Aspects of Character – RL.8.3 (15 minutes) → Generate Discussion Norms – SL.8.1 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
