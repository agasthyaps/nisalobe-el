# Lesson 4: Compare and Contrast Depictions of La Llorona, Part I
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** In this lesson students will continue to read *Summer of the Mariposas* and continue to identify both new vocabulary and the gist of chapters 18 and 19. Then, students will begin to compare the depictions of La Llorona in *La Llorona* and *Summer of the Mariposas*.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.3, RL.8.9

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of an excerpt of chapters 18 and 19 of *Summer of the Mariposas.*
- I can compare the depiction of La Llorona in *La Llorona* with the depiction in *Summer of the Mariposas*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapters 18–19 Excerpts (20 minutes) → Model: Compare Depictions of La Llorona – RL.8.9 (5 minutes) → Partner Work: Compare Depictions of La Llorona – RL.8.9 (10 minutes)
