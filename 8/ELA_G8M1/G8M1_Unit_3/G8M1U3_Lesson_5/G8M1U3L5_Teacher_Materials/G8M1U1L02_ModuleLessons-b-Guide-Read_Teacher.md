Text Guide: *Summer of the Mariposas*

(For Teacher Reference)

This guide is meant to support in-class reading of *Summer of the Mariposas* by providing comprehension questions and examples of vocabulary with which students may struggle.  
Be sure students provide evidence from the text to support their responses to the questions.

Unit 1

**Lesson 2, Chapter 1 and Prologue**

Invite students to turn to the first page of the text. Read aloud the definition of *mariposas* as students read it silently. Draw students’ attention to the word *transformation* in the definition, and ask them to consider this connotation as they read. Ask students “What do you think the butterflies will symbolize in this text?”

Read from page 5 “Juanita reacted first” to page 10 “I read that somewhere.” Then read from page 17 “There’s a wallet” to page 21 “The same question,” pausing to define the following vocabulary and ask the following questions as needed.
