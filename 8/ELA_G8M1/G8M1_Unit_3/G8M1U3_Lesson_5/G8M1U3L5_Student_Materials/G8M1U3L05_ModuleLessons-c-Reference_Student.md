Chapter 20

RL.8.1, RL.8.2

**Name:** **Date:**

- News reporters begin to show up at the girls’ house. Mama calls the FBI agent to come and help the family deal with this.

- The girls go to church, and while praying to the Virgen, Odilia discovers beautiful white roses that someone had placed in a vase and brought to the Virgen and can’t stop thinking about them.

- Odilia realizes during a conversation with her mother that the roses she had given to her at the border were actually meant for La Llorona.

- Odilia takes the roses and leaves her house quickly to deliver them to the La Llorona.
