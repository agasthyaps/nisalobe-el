# Lesson 5: Compare and Contrast Depictions of La Llorona, Part II
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson students will continue to read *Summer of the Mariposas* and continue to identify both new vocabulary and the gist of chapter 20. Then, students will begin to contrast the depictions of La Llorona in *La Llorona* and *Summer of the Mariposas*.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.3, RL.8.9

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 20 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can compare and contrast depictions of La Llorona in *La Llorona* with those in *Summer of the Mariposas*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.9)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 20 Excerpt (10 minutes) → Compare and Contrast Depictions of La Llorona – RL.8.9 (10 minutes) → Whole Group Share – RL.8.9 (5 minutes)
