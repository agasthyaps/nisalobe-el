Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 14

W.8.6

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following question.

What features can you add to your webpage to make it clear and more engaging? Consider what you can add to engage your audience and make them interested in your topic.

Answers may vary, but will be along the lines of the following: One visual representation of the story. A clear title with a large font. Medium type font for the narrative and essay. A subheading for the essay.
