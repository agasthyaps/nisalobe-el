Criteria for an Effective Webpage  
Anchor Chart

W.8.6

(Example for Teacher Reference)

- Title of webpage is at the top of the page and has a larger font than the rest of the text. Suggested font size: 18.

- Text is chunked into short paragraphs that make it easy to read.

- Font of text is large and easy to read. Suggested font size: 14.

- Layout of text has left alignment, which is helpful because we read from left to right.

- Webpage includes one visual that represents the text.

- Essay is separate from the narrative and has its own subtitle.

- Webpage offers further information for the audience to read and learn more about the topic.
