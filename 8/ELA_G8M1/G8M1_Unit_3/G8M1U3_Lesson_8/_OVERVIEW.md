# Lesson 8: Write a Compare and Contrast Essay: Draft Proof Paragraph 1
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read *Summer of the Mariposas* chapter 21, and draft the first Proof Paragraph for their compare and contrast essay.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.2b, W.8.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 21 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can draft the first Proof Paragraph of a compare and contrast essay. (W.8.2b, W.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.2b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Discuss: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 21 Excerpt (10 minutes) → Plan Proof Paragraph 1 – W.8.5 (10 minutes) → Draft Proof Paragraph 1 – W.8.2b (15 minutes)
