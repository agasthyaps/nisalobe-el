# Lesson 6: Painted Essay®: Analyze a Model
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students analyze a model compare and contrast essay using the Painted Essay® structure to generate criteria for their own essays.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.9, RI.8.1, W.8.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze a Painted Essay® model to generate criteria of an effective compare and contrast essay. (RI.8.1, W.8.2)
- I can analyze a model essay to determine criteria for my essay on a modernized monster. (RI.8.1, W.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduce Painted Essay® – W.8.2 (20 minutes) → Model: Analyze the Model Essay – W.8.2 (5 minutes) → Partner Work: Analyze the Model Essay – W.8.2 (10 minutes)
