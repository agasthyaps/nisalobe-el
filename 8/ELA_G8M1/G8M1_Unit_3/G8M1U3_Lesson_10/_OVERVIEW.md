# Lesson 10: Write a Compare and Contrast Essay: Draft a Conclusion
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students draft the conclusion paragraph for their compare and contrast essay.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.2f, W.8.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan and draft the conclusion of a compare and contrast essay with a strong reflection. (W.8.2f, W.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.2f (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: Model Essay: “Peuchen,” Paragraph 4 – W.8.2f (10 minutes) → Plan Conclusion – W.8.5 (15 minutes) → Draft Conclusion – W.8.2f (10 minutes)
