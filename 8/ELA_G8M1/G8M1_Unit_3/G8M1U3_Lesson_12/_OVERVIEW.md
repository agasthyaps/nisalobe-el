# Lesson 12: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Write a Compare and Contrast Essay
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 3: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students receive feedback on their mid-unit assessment and take their end of unit assessment, which asks them to write a second essay explaining how they modernized their monster from Latin American folklore.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.9, RI.8.1, W.8.2, W.8.4, W.8.9a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write an essay that compares and contrasts the depiction of a monster in folklore from Latin America and in my modernized narrative. (RL.8.9, W.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return Mid-Unit 3 Assessments with Feedback (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Write a Compare and Contrast Essay – W.8.2 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
