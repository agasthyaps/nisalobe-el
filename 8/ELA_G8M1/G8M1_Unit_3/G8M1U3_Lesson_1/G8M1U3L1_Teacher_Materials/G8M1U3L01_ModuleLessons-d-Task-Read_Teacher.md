Close Reading Culminating Task:  
*Summer of the Mariposas*, Author’s Note

RI.8.1, RI.8.2

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Carefully read and answer the questions below.

1.  Which of the following is the central idea of this text?

2.  The knowledge and wisdom of the Aztecs was extraordinary and their myths and legends should be remembered.

3.  <u>McCall wrote the novel in order to highlight modern and ancient myths and legends of Mexico and to reimagine La Llorona to emphasize a theme that a mother’s love is, in fact, pure.</u>

4.  The bond between mothers and their children is extraordinary and mothers are very important.

5.  McCall wrote the novel in order to reveal to the world that La Llorona was, in fact, a pure historical figure who did not murder her own children; she was a loving mother and should be remembered fondly.

6.  Choose the two sentences in the text that best introduce the central idea and copy them below:

“I wrote *Summer of the Mariposas* with the intention of showcasing both our modern and ancient mitos and leyendas by juxtaposing them against one of the greatest stories ever told, *The Odyssey*.” (page 335)

“Above all else, I wrote this story because I wanted to celebrate the extraordinary bond between children and their mamas.” (page 337)

7.  How has the author developed this central idea over the course of the text?

8.  <u>By explaining why she chose to modernize La Llorona in contrast with how she is usually depicted and explaining the theme emphasized through this modernization.</u>

9.  By explaining the story of Malintzin and emphasizing what we know about her historical figure in contrast with the stories that have been told and the way in which she has become associated with La Llorona.

10. By explaining how all parents make mistakes and it is important that the are able to tell their own stories and get a second chance, like Mama does in the story.

11. By explaining the beauty and power of Aztec culture and the need for modern writers to retell and reimagine ancient myths and legends.
