Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 1

RL.8.4, L.8.4a

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following questions.

Read this sentence from page 335 of *Summer of the Mariposas*:

“I wrote *Summer of the Mariposas* with the intention of showcasing both our modern and ancient **mitos y leyendas** by juxtaposing them against one of the greatest stories ever told,  
***The Odyssey***.”

What does **juxtaposing** mean? What strategy did you use to determine the meaning of the word?

Answers will vary, but will be along the lines of *juxtaposing* means “placing different things side by side to compare or contrast them for an interesting effect.” Students may reference using different strategies to determine the meaning of the word, such as using context clues, rereading, using resources, etc.
