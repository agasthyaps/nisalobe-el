<tr class="even">
<td><strong>What Does It Look Like?</strong></td>
<td><ul>

<li><p><strong>Cleaning up after myself</strong></p></li>
<li><p><strong>Putting things back in the right place after moving them around</strong></p></li>
<li><p><strong>Recycling</strong></p></li>
<li><p><strong>Throwing litter in the trash</strong></p></li>
</ul></td>

<li><p><strong>“Is that something that you could recycle rather than throw in the trash?”</strong></p></li>
</ul></td>
