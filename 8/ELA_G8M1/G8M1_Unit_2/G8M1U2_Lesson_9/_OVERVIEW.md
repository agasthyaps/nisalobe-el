# Lesson 9: Write a Narrative: Plan Plot
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students will review the elements of plot and use these elements to build upon the plan for their own narrative. Finally, they will have time to share their storyboard with a partner.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.3a, W.8.3e, W.8.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan the plot of my new scene for *Summer of the Mariposas* that results in the same outcome as the original scene. (W.8.3a, W.8.3e)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Model: Plan Plot – W.8.3a (10 minutes) → Partner Share: Plan Plot – W.8.3e (10 minutes) → Practice: Plan Plot – W.8.5 (15 minutes)
