# Lesson 7: Research a Monster from Latin American Folklore
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students will choose a Latin American monster to research and will visit one of four workstations to explore informational materials about their monster of choice. Then, students will choose which monster they wish the write about in their narrative piece.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.7, W.8.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can research to choose a monster from folklore of Latin America for my narrative. (W.8.7, W.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Research Model: Monster from Latin American Folklore – W.8.7 (15 minutes) → Research Practice: Monster from Latin American Folklore – W.8.8 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
