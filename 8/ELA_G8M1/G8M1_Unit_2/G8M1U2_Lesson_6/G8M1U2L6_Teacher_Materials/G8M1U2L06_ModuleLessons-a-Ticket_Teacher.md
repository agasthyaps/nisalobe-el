Entrance Ticket: Unit 2, Lesson 6

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following questions.

In this classroom we are an academic community—a group of people working together toward goals related to education.

Read the following academic mindset:

- **I belong in this academic community.**

What helps you to feel you belong in this classroom?

Student answers will vary, but could include answers similar to my classmates listen to my ideas; people respect my work and help create a productive work environment; we all work hard and do our best, etc. . . .

What prevents you from feeling like you belong here?

Student answers will vary, but could include answers similar to when my classmates are disruptive or disrespectful of our working environment, I feel like I don’t belong; if other students don’t do work or take work seriously, I don’t feel like I belong; if people shut down my ideas or ignore me, I feel disconnected from our community, etc.
