# Lessons 4–5: Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Determine Theme and Write Literary Summary
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 2: Lessons 4–5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, which may occur over two periods, students will critique a literary summary in order to prepare to write their own. Students will continue to read their class novel, identifying unknown vocabulary and determining the gist of the chapter. Students will then take their mid-unit assessm

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, RL.8.4, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 16 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can determine a theme and analyze its development in *Summer of the Mariposas*, chapter 16. (RL.8.2)
- I can write an objective summary of *Summer of the Mariposas*, chapter 16. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2, RL.8.10)

**Agenda:** Opening → Return End of Unit 1 Assessments with Feedback (5 minutes) → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Whole Class Critique: Literary Summary – RL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 16 Excerpt – RL.8.2 (15 minutes)
