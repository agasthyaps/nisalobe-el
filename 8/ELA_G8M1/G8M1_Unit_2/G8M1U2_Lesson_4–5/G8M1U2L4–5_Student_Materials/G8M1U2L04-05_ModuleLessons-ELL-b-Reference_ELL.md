Synopsis: *Summer of the Mariposas*,  
Chapter 16

**Name:** **Date:**

- The girls leave Abuelita’s house to go back to the United States and their mother. Abuelita drives the girls to the border.

- The girls realize they don’t have any documentation, like birth certificates, to show at the border.

- Odilia finds a church and decides she and her sisters must pray to the Virgen de Guadalupe to help them.

- As the girls pray to Tonantzin, Odilia’s magical earring starts to buzz and vibrate.

- Tonantzin appears and tells the girls that they must bring flowers to “the mother” to remind her who she is.
