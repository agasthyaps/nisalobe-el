# Lesson 2: Analyze Development of Theme and Summarize *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 14
**Grade 8: Module 1: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students will continue to read their class novel, identifying unknown vocabulary and determining the gist of the chapter. Students will utilize a note-catcher to track how a theme is developed in chapter 14 and will then write an objective summary paragraph for the chapter.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can demonstrate understanding of the excerpt of chapter 14 of *Summer of the Mariposas*.
- I can determine a theme and analyze its development in *Summer of the Mariposas*, chapter 14. (RL.8.2)
- I can write an objective summary of *Summer of the Mariposas*, chapter 14. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 14 Excerpt (15 minutes) → Identify Development of Theme: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 14 – RL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Write a Summary: *Summer of the Mariposas*, Chapter 14 – RL.8.2 (10 minutes)
