Entrance Ticket: Unit 2, Lesson 1

RL.8.2, RL.8.4

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following question.

What words can we use to complete this sentence so that it reflects the theme, being kind and pure of heart leads to a fuller, more meaningful life?

> **Student answers will vary. Examples are given below:**

La Llorona said we have to be **brave** and **selfless**.

La Llorona said we have to be **smart** and **helpful**.

La Llorona said we have to be **honorable** and **true**.

Why did you select those two words?

Student answers will vary. Showing *bravery* means you do your best in challenging situations, and being *selfless* means you are focused on supporting others besides yourself. These characteristics lead someone to being kind and pure of heart, which fits with the theme.
