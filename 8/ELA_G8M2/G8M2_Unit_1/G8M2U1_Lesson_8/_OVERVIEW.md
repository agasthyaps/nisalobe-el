# Lesson 8: Analyze Purpose and Point of View: Local Food
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their anchor text and analyze the author’s purpose and point of view.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the author’s point of view and purpose in an excerpt from *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*. (RI.8.6)
- I can explain how the author acknowledges conflicting viewpoints in the text. (RI.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 6 (15 minutes) → Analyze Author’s Purpose and Point of View – RI.8.6 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
