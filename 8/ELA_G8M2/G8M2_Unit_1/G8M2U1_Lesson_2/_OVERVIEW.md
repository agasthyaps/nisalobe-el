# Lesson 2: Analyze Purpose and Point of View: *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Introduction
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students are introduced to their anchor text and begin to analyze the author’s purpose and point of view.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.4, RI.8.6, L.8.1a, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the author’s point of view and purpose in an excerpt from *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*. (RI.8.1, RI.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 1, and Analyze Purpose and Point of View – RI.8.6 (20 minutes) → Language Dive: *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Page 11 – RI.8.6 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
