# Lesson 7: Delineate and Evaluate an Argument: Industrial Organic Farming
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their anchor text and delineate the author’s argument, and also begin to delineate a speaker’s argument.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.8, SL.8.3

**Learning Targets:**
- I can delineate and evaluate the argument in *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, assessing whether the reasoning is sound and the evidence is relevant and sufficient. (RI.8.8)
- I can delineate a speaker’s argument and specific claims, evaluating the soundness of the reasoning and relevance and sufficiency of the evidence and identifying when irrelevant evidence is introduced. (SL.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 5 (15 minutes) → Delineate Arguments: *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 5 – RI.8.8 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
