<td><p><strong>Social</strong>: A social motive is one focused on how people interact with one another in society, in large or small ways.</p>
<p><strong>Commercial</strong>: A commercial motive is one that is focused on money-making, consumerism, and/or marketing.</p>
<p><strong>Political</strong>: A political motive is one that is focused on the interests of a particular government, political party, or group.</p>
<p><strong>Environmental</strong>: An environmental motive is one that is focused on benefiting the environment.</p></td>
