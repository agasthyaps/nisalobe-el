# Lesson 14: Analyze Different Mediums: Local Sustainable Food
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students analyze the purpose and motives for expressing information as well as the advantages and disadvantages of using different mediums. They also analyze cases of conflicting information.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.7, RI.8.9, SL.8.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can identify the advantages and disadvantages of using different mediums to present information about local sustainable food. (RI.8.7)
- I can analyze two texts that provide conflicting information on local sustainable food and identify where they disagree on matters of fact or interpretation. (RI.8.9)
- I can identify possible purposes and motives for presenting information on local sustainable food and provide an example of each. (SL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Mediums: Local Sustainable Food – RI.8.7 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Independent Research Task – RI.8.9 (20 minutes)
