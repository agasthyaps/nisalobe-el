# Lesson 11: Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Structure, Determine Point of View, and Evaluate Arguments in Video and Text
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students take their mid-unit assessment and delineate and evaluate an author’s argument and analyze the purpose, point of view, and structure.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.5, RI.8.6, RI.8.8, SL.8.3

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the structure of a specific paragraph in a text and explain how it develops a key idea. (RI.8.5)
- I can determine an author’s point of view and analyze how the author addresses conflicting evidence or viewpoints. (RI.8.6)
- I can delineate and evaluate the argument in a text, assessing whether the reasoning is sound and the evidence is relevant and sufficient. (RI.8.8)
- I can delineate and evaluate a speaker’s argument, assessing whether the reasoning is sound and the evidence is relevant and sufficient. (SL.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Structure, Determine Point of View, and Evaluate Arguments in Video and Text (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Reflect on Learning Targets (5 minutes)
