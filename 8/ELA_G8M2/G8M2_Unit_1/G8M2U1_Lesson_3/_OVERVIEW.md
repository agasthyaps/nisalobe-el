# Lesson 3: Analyze Purpose, Point of View, and Structure: Corn in Our Food
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students continue to read their anchor text and begin to analyze structure.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the author’s purpose and point of view in a text and explain how he acknowledges conflicting viewpoints. (RI.8.6)
- I can identify the structure of a specific paragraph in a text and explain how it develops a key concept. (RI.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 2, and Analyze Purpose and Point of View – RI.8.6 (15 minutes) → Mini Lesson: Analyze Paragraph Structure – RI.8.5 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
