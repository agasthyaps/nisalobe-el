Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 13

SL.8.2

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, respond to the following prompts.

Watch the short NourishLife video by Bryant Terry called “Cooking Together.”

What purpose does Bryant Terry have for creating this video?

**Responses will vary, but may include:**

1.  <u>His purpose is to inform people of the enjoyable and beneficial aspects of cooking and eating at home.</u>

2.  His purpose is to inform people of the enjoyable and beneficial aspects of eating at restaurants.

3.  His purpose is to entertain people and make them laugh and smile.

4.  His purpose is to convince people of the dangers of eating in restaurants.

What motive might Bryant Terry have for creating this video (social, political, environmental, or commercial)? Explain your thinking with evidence in the space below.

Responses will vary, but may include: His motive may be social because he finds enjoyment from eating and cooking with his family, and he wants to spread his message because he believes others in society would benefit from this as well. Or, his motive may be commercial because he hopes to convince more people to shop for groceries locally and cook them and eat at home, supporting more local farming and produce.
