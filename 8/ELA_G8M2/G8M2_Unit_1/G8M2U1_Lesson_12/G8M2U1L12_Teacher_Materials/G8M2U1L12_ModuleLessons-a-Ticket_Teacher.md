Entrance Ticket: Unit 1, Lesson 12

RI.8.7

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, respond to the following prompts.

The definition of the word **medium** is a format for communicating or expressing information. For example, a newspaper is a print medium.

What are all of the mediums you use or interact with on a normal day that provide information for you?

Underline all of the mediums from the list below that apply.

- photographs

- books

- online books (for example: Kindle)

- newspapers

- online news articles

- video

- audio (for example: radio, speech, interview)

- infographic (for example: poster or chart)

Responses will vary, but may include:

- photograph

- book, newspaper, etc. (print)

- video

- audio (radio, speech, interview, etc.)

- poster/chart (infographic)

- social media

Which one of these mediums do you think is most effective for conveying information? Pick one from your list, and explain your choice.

Responses will vary, but may include: Audio is the most effective way to communicate information. I listen to the radio on the way to work and to podcasts when I walk my dog. I can get my news and information quickly and on the go, and that is important for active people.
