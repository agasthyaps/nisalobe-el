Homework: Analyze Mediums:  
Industrial Organic Farming

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Complete the following tasks, and answer the questions.

Review the photograph on page 135 of *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*.

Read the text sections from page 134 to page 137 (stop at “. . . bought organic brands or started their own.”)

1.  What information does the photograph on page 135 give the viewer?

2.  <u>It shows an organic TV dinner from Cascadian Farm.</u>

3.  It explains how Cascadian Farm used to be an independent company.

4.  It shows a picture of healthy-looking food growing in the fields of Cascadian Farm.

5.  It explains the history of Cascadian Farm—from a hippie commune to a major company.

6.  What information does the text on pages 134–137 give the reader?

7.  It tells about the history of many organic farms and how they were forced to grow and sell out to major corporations.

8.  It explains the major crops and growing procedures used at Cascadian Farm.

9.  It tells about the treatment of animals and farmworkers at Cascadian Farm.

10. <u>It explains the rise of agribusiness and tells how Cascadian Farm grew from its hippie roots to a large corporate company.</u>

11. What is one advantage of presenting this topic in print text format on pages 134–137? (RI.8.7)

12. <u>It provides layers of details that help illuminate the topic for students.</u>

13. The speaker’s body language and tone help the reader understand the author’s purpose.

14. It is easy for students to copy and paste parts of the document as a way of taking notes.

15. The text level matches all readers who encounter the text.
