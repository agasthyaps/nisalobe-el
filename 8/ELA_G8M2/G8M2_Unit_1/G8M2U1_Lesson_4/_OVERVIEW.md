# Lesson 4: Delineate and Evaluate an Argument: Industrial Farming
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 1: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read their anchor text and are introduced to how to delineate and evaluate an author’s argument.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.8, L.8.4c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can delineate the argument in a section of *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*. (RI.8.8)
- I can evaluate an argument, assessing whether Michael Pollan’s reasoning is sound and the evidence presented on the topic of industrial farming is relevant and sufficient. (RI.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.4c (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 3, and Track Gist and Key Details (15 minutes) → Delineate an Argument – RI.8.8 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
