**Focus Standards:** W.8.1, W.8.4, W.8.6, L.8.1, L.8.2, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan and write an argument to support a claim with clear reasons and relevant evidence. (W.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Write an Argument Essay: Defending a Healthy Food Choice (75 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – W.8.1 (10 minutes)
