# Lesson 5: Write a Practice Argument Essay: Create a Plan
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students are introduced to their practice essay prompt, read a section of their anchor text to determine aligned evidence, and begin to plan their essay.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, W.8.1, W.8.4, W.8.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can annotate and determine the gist of a practice essay prompt. (W.8.4)
- I can identify evidence that supports a claim. (RI.8.1)
- I can use my understanding of the elements of an argument essay to create a plan for a practice essay. (W.8.1, W.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduce Practice Essay Prompt – W.8.4 (5 minutes) → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 10, and Identify Evidence – RI.8.1 (15 minutes) → Plan Practice Argument Essay – W.8.5 (15 minutes)
