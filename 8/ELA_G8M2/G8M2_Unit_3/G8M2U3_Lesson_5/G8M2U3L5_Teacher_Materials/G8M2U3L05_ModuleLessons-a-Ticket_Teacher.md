Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 5

W.8.1

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following prompts.

For each claim given, come up with a supporting reason. The first one is done for you.

**Claim**: Middle school students should attend school for at least nine hours a day.

**Reason**: More time in school increases student academic gains.

**Claim**: Florida is the ideal vacation spot for families.

**Reason**:

Responses will vary, but may include: the mild climate and many beaches and attractions like Disney World make it appeal to all ages.

**Claim**: Basketball is the best example of a team sport.

**Reason**:

Responses will vary, but may include: all players have a specialized role that is critical in order for the entire team to succeed.

**Claim**: Students should have a daily opportunity to participate in some sort of exercise or physical activity as a part of their school schedule.

**Reason**:

Responses will vary, but may include: physical movement stimulates brain activity and increases the capacity for learning.
