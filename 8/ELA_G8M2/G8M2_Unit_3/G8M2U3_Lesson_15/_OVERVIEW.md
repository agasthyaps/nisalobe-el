# Lesson 15: Performance Task: Roundtable Presentations
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 15**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in their performance task, verbally presenting an infographic that represents their argument about healthy food choices to an audience of community members.

**Focus Standards:** SL.8.4, SL.8.5, SL.8.6, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can clearly present my recommendation for consumers to consider when making healthy and sustainable food choices. (SL.8.4, SL.8.5)
- I can use appropriate eye contact, adequate volume, and clear pronunciation when presenting my claims and findings. (SL.8.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Review Performance Task Anchor Chart (5 minutes) → Work Time → Roundtable Presentations – SL.8.4, SL.8.5, SL.8.6, L.8.6 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Reflect on Learning Targets (5 minutes)
