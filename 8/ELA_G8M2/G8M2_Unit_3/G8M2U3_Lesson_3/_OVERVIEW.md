# Lesson 3: Healthy and Sustainable Food Choices
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students consider evidence supporting different food choices and determine which food choice they think would most benefit their community.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can cite evidence to support how various food choices are healthy and/or sustainable. (RI.8.1)
- I can take a stance on a topic and engage in collaborative discussions with my peers about healthy and sustainable foods. (SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Consider the Evidence: Healthy and Sustainable Food Choices – RI.8.1 (10 minutes) → Four Corners: Determine a Food Choice – RI.8.1 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
