**Directions:** Review the definitions of **sustainable** you have discussed in previous lessons, listed below:

**sustainable:** causing little or no harm to the environment; being able to continue for a long time

**sustainable:** (as defined by Michael Pollan on page 159 of *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*) “sustainable . . . should be as much as possible a closed loop, recycling fertility and using renewable energy.”

**sustainable:** humans and nature living in conditions that support present and future generations
