Language Dive: Model Essay: “Transportation Choices,” Paragraph 5 Note-Catcher

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

However, if more people use public transportation, it seems **likely that the frequency of service will increase.**

1.  Make a list of linking language that shows contrast.

Responses will vary.

2.  Complete the sentence frame to talk about next weekend.

It seems likely that **I will spend time with my sister**.

(independent clause + **that** + independent clause = independent clause)

(subject + predicate = independent clause)

3.  Complete the sentence frame to write a sentence for the counterclaim paragraph of your argument essay.

**On the other hand**, if **people continue to eat processed foods every day**, it seems likely

that **their health may suffer**.

(subject + predicate = independent clause)
