# Lesson 1: Analyze Language: *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read a new section of their anchor text, participate in a Language Dive, and analyze the author’s language to more deeply understand the meaning.

**Focus Standards:** L.8.4a, L.8.4b, L.8.4c, L.8.5b, L.8.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can recognize the differences among connotations of words with similar meanings in *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*. (L.8.5c)
- I can use context and the relationships among words to better understand the meanings of words and phrases in *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*. (L.8.4a, L.8.5b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.4c (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 8, and Analyze Word Connotations – L.8.5c (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Page 40 – L.8.5b (10 minutes) → Analyze Language: *The Omnivore’s Dilemma*, Section 8 – L.8.5b (10 minutes)
