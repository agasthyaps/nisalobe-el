(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, complete the following prompts.

Read the following argument writing prompt that will be used in the assessment essay later in the unit.

“What is one informed action that people in your community can take to make healthy and sustainable food choices?”

You can narrow down your ideas later, but what do you think? What are two possible informed actions people could take? (Reference your Food Choices and Evidence graphic organizer as needed.)

Responses will vary but should reference food choices discussed while completing their Food Choices and Evidence note-catcher.
