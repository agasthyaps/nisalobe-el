# Lessons 10–11: Draft an Informative Essay
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lessons 10–11**

**Summary:** In these lessons students analyze the models, plan their conclusion, draft their essay, and participate in a peer critique.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.2, W.8.2f, W.8.4, W.8.5, W.8.6, SL.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan a concluding statement to an informative essay that follows from and supports the information presented. (W.8.2f, W.8.4)
- I can draft an informative essay using the Painted Essay® structure. (W.8.2)
- I can give kind, helpful, and specific feedback to my partner. (SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.2f (10 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Model and Plan Conclusion – W.8.5 (15 minutes) → Independent Writing: Draft an Informative Essay – W.8.2 (50 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
