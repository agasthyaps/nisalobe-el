# Lesson 4: Introduce Independent Research Case Studies
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students are introduced to their independent research project and read their first related text.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.7, W.8.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can conduct research to answer a question about factors that influence our access to healthy food. (W.8.7, W.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduce Case Study Research Project – W.8.7 (5 minutes) → Jigsaw Activity – W.8.8 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
