Access to Healthy Food Research:  
GMOs Note-Catcher

RI.8.1, W.8.7, W.8.8

(Example for Teacher Reference)

1.  Central Research Question:

How do GMOs influence our access to healthy food?

2.  What are some additional related focus questions that will help you explore this central research question?

What are the benefits of GMOs?

What are the potential dangers of GMOs?

What might be the long-term effects of GMOs on our health?

What might be the long-term effects of GMOs on the environment?

3.  What are some internet search terms you can use for this question?

GMOs

genetically modified foods

genetic engineering
