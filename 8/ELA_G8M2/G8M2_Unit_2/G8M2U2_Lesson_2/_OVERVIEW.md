# Lesson 2: Introduce Internet Research: GMOs
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students learn about search terms and credible sources and use these skills to research GMOs.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, W.8.7, W.8.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can assess the credibility of digital sources while conducting research. (W.8.7, W.8.8)
- I can use search terms effectively to gather information from digital sources. (W.8.7, W.8.8)
- I can conduct research to answer a question about how GMOs influence our access to healthy food. (W.8.7, W.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.8 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Research Mini Lesson: Search Terms and Credibility of Sources – W.8.8 (15 minutes) → Research GMOs and Track Access to Healthy Food – W.8.7 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
