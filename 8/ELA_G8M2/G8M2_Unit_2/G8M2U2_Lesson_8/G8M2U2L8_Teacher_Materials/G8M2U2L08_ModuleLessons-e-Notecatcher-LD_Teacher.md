“GMOs,” Paragraph 1 Note-Catcher

W.8.2a, L.8.1b, L.8.3a

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

In other ways, the safety and benefit of GMOs are still being questioned.

1.  Read the sentence, then use the sentence frame to rewrite it in passive voice.

**Students are reading articles about GMOs.**

Articles about GMOs **<u>are being read</u>** by students.

(**is**/**are** + **being** + verb + **-en**/**-ed** = passive present continuous verb)

(object + passive present continuous verb + **by** + subject = sentence in passive voice)

2.  Complete the sentence frame to draft a sentence in the focus statement of your essay that introduces the second point you will discuss.

On the other hand, **<u>the benefits of organic food are being determined</u>**.

(linking phrase + direct object + passive present continuous verb)
