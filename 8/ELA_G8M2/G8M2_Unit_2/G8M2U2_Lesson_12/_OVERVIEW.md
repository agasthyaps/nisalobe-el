# Lesson 12: End of Unit 2 Assessment, Part I: Desktop Teaching Activity Lesson Plan
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students create the plan for their desktop teaching activity as part of their end of unit assessment, add a visual, and participate in a peer critique.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, SL.8.4, SL.8.5, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can organize my research findings, ideas, and learned vocabulary to present to an audience. (RI.8.1, SL.8.4)
- I can clearly integrate multimedia and visual displays into presentations to add interest in my presentation. (SL.8.5)
- I can use appropriate eye contact, adequate volume, and clear pronunciation when presenting my claims and findings. (SL.8.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.8.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 2 Assessment, Part I: Desktop Teaching Activity: Access to Healthy Food – RI.8.1 (20 minutes) → Presentation: Prepare Visual Aids – SL.8.5 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
