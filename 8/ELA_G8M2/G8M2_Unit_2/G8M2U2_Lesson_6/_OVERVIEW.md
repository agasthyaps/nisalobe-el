# Lesson 6: Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Research Climate Change and Food Shortages
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students take their mid-unit assessment and are presented with a new research question, use the skills they have learned to answer questions, and fill in a graphic organizer with their research findings.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.4, W.8.7, W.8.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can conduct research to answer a new question about food choices and sustainability. (W.8.7, W.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Research Climate Change and Food Shortages (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress – W.8.7 (10 minutes)
