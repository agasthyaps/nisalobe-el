# Lesson 1: Introduction to Research Project: How Do GMOs Influence Our Access to Healthy Food?
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students are introduced to their group research project and read their first source on GMOs.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, W.8.7, W.8.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can cite evidence that supports the influence GMOs have on our access to healthy food. (RI.8.1, W.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduce GMO Research Project (10 minutes) → Read “To GMO or NOT to GMO?” and Track Access to Healthy Food – W.8.8 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
