# Lesson 13: Desktop Teaching Activity: Present Research Findings
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students add domain-specific vocabulary to their desktop teaching activity plans and rehearse and receive peer feedback to prompt revisions.

**Focus Standards:** SL.8.4, SL.8.5, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can use academic and domain-specific words and phrases when verbally sharing my ideas. (L.8.6)
- I can clearly communicate my research findings verbally and through visual aids. (SL.8.4, SL.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Vocabulary Jukebox – L.8.6 (10 minutes) → Peer Feedback: Practice Presentation – SL.8.4 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
