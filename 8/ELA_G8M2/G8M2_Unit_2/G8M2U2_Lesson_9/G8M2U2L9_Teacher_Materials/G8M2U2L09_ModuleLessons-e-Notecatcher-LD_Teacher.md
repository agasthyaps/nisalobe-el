Language Dive: Model Essay:  
“GMOs,” Paragraph 3 Note-Catcher

W.8.2b, W.8.3c, L.8.1a

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Although GMOs seem to improve access to healthy food,** questions about the safety of using GMOs still remain.

1.  The linking verb **seem** can be followed by an infinitive phrase functioning as an adjective. Experiment with replacing **seem** with other linking verbs. Which ones can also be followed by an infinitive phrase?

GMOs **appear** to improve access to healthy food.

| be  | appear | become | feel |
|-----|--------|--------|------|

2.  Complete the sentence frame to talk about eating healthy food.

Eating healthy seems **to have many benefits**.

(to + base form of verb + object = infinitive phrase)

(subject + seems + infinitive = statement expressing something that is likely)

3.  Complete the sentence frame to draft a topic sentence for the first Proof Paragraph of an essay that explores the pros and cons of organic food.

Although **organic food has some health benefits**, **it is more expensive for consumers**.
