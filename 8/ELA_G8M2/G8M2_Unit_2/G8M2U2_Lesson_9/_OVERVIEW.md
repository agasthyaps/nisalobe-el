# Lesson 9: Write an Informative Essay: Plan Proof Paragraphs
**Grade 8: Module 2: Unit 2: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students analyze the model essays, participate in a Language Dive, and plan their Proof Paragraphs.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.2b, W.8.3c, W.8.4, W.8.5, SL.8.4, L.8.1a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can plan Proof Paragraphs for an informative essay that include relevant details, examples, and evidence that support my focus statement. (W.8.2b, W.8.5)
- I can present my Proof Paragraphs plan to a partner, using appropriate eye contact and adequate volume. (SL.8.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.2a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Language Dive: Model Essay: “GMOs,” Paragraph 3 – W.8.2b (10 minutes) → Analyze Models and Plan Proof Paragraphs – W.8.5 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
