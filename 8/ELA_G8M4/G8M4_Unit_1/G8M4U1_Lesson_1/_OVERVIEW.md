# Lesson 1: Discover Our Topic: Lessons from Japanese American Internment
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in the Infer the Topic protocol, taking turns sharing initial notices and wonderings about the topic of Japanese American internment in pairs. **Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can infer the topic of this module from text and visual resources. (RI.8.1)
