# Lesson 9: Close Read: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 7
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in a close read of chapter 7 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. After reading, they complete a culminating task and engage in a collaborative discussion to explore a significant idea from the text.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.3, SL.8.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how the text makes connections among and distinctions between individuals, events, and ideas in chapter 7 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.3)
- I can engage effectively in a collaborative discussion about a significant idea in the text by being prepared, drawing on my notes, and following rules for collegial discussions. (SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Close Read: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 7 – RI.8.3 (20 minutes) → Culminating Task – RI.8.3 (5 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
