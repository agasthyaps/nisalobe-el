# Lesson 6: Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Vocabulary, Connections, and Distinctions: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 5
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students complete the Mid-Unit 1 Assessment, which focuses on connections and distinctions and vocabulary in *Farewell to Manzanar*. Students also receive feedback on their End of Unit 2 Assessments.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.3, RI.8.4, L.8.4a, L.8.4b, L.8.5a, L.8.5c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the figurative and connotative meanings of words as they are used in chapter 5 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.4)
- I can analyze how the text makes connections among and distinctions between individuals, events, and ideas in chapter 5 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Vocabulary, Connections, and Distinctions: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 5 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress (5 minutes)
