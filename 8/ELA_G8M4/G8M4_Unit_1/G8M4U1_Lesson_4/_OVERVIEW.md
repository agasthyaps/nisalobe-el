# Lesson 4: Analyze Filmmakers’ Choices: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Part 1
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students watch the first segment of the film adaptation of *Farewell to Manzanar* and begin analyzing how the film stays faithful to and departs from the text.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.7

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the extent to which the film *Farewell to Manzanar* stays faithful to or departs from the text, evaluating the filmmakers’ choices. (RL.8.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.7 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze *Farewell to Manzanar*, Part 1 – RL.8.7 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Peer Share: Compare Text to Film Note-Catchers – RL.8.7 (5 minutes)
