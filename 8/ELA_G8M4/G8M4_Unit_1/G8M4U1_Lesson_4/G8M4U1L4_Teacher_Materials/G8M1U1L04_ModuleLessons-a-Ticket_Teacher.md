(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the following questions.

One of our learning targets today is, “I can identify strategies to determine the meaning of **unfamiliar** vocabulary.”

What is the meaning of **unfamiliar**?

unfamiliar: not known or experienced before

What strategy did you use to determine the meaning?

Answers will vary. Students should suggest strategies such as

- read the sentence around the word (context);

- verify the inferred meaning;

- look at the affixes for clues;

- look at the root of the word for clues;

• use a dictionary.
