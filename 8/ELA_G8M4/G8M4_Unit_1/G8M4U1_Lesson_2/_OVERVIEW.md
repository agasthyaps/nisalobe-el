# Lesson 2: Analyze Connections and Distinctions: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 1
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students begin reading the anchor text, *Farewell to Manzanar*, and begin tracking connections and distinctions in the text.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.3, RI.8.4, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of unfamiliar vocabulary in chapter 1 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.4, L.8.4)
- I can analyze how the text makes connections among and distinctions between individuals, events, and ideas in chapter 1 of *Farewell to Manzanar* to develop my understanding of the text. (RI.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Farewell to Manzanar*: Foreword, Epigraphs, and Chapter 1 – RI.8.4, L.8.4 (15 minutes) → Analyze Connections and Distinctions: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 1 – RI.8.3 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
