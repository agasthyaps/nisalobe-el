# Lesson 12: End of Unit 1 Assessment: Collaborative Discussion: *Farewell to Manzanar*
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 1: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in an academic discussion that centers on the module guiding question: “What were the causes and impacts of Japanese American internment camps?” Prior to the discussion, students participate in a short QuickWrite to organize their thinking. **Focus Standards:** RI.8.4, SL.8.1a, SL.8.1b, L.8.4a, L.8.4b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of unfamiliar vocabulary in chapter 11 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.4, L.8.4)
- I can engage effectively in collaborative discussions about one of the module guiding questions by being prepared, drawing on my notes, and following rules for collegial discussions. (SL.8.1a, SL.8.1b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 11 – RI.8.4, L.8.4 (15 minutes) → End of Unit 1 Assessment: Collaborative Discussion: *Farewell to Manzanar* – SL.8.1a, SL.8.1b (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
