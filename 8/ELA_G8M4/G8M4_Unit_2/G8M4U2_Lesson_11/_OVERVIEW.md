# Lesson 11: Write a Practice Literary Argument Essay: Create a Plan
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students are introduced to their practice essay prompt and plan their essay. Students also practice spelling correctly.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, W.8.1, W.8.4, W.8.5, L.8.2c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can annotate and determine the gist of a practice essay prompt. (W.8.4)
- I can identify evidence that supports a claim. (RI.8.1)
- I can use my understanding of the elements of an argument essay to create a plan for a practice essay. (W.8.1, W.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.2c (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduce Practice Essay Prompt – W.8.1, W.8.4 (5 minutes) → Identify Evidence in Text and Note-Catchers – RI.8.1 (15 minutes) → Plan Practice Argument Essay – W.8.5 (15 minutes)
