# Lesson 9: Analyze Filmmakers’ Choices: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Part 4
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students watch the final segment of the film adaptation of *Farewell to Manzanar* and analyze how the film stays faithful to and departs from the text.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.7

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze the extent to which the film *Farewell to Manzanar* stays faithful to or departs from the text, evaluating the filmmakers’ choices. (RL.8.7)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze *Farewell to Manzanar*, Part 4 – RL.8.7 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Make Connections to Text – RL.8.7 (5 minutes)
