# Lesson 2: Analyze Point of View, Connections, and Distinctions: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 13
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read chapter 13 of the anchor text, *Farewell to Manzanar*, analyze point of view, and track connections and distinctions in the text. Students also participate in a Language Dive to explore a connection and point of view in the text.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.3, RI.8.6, L.8.5a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how the authors’ point of view is conveyed and differs from other points of view in chapter 13 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.6)
- I can analyze how the text makes connections among and distinctions between individuals, events, and ideas in chapter 13 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner –RI.8.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapter 13 – RI.8.6 (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Page 104 – RI.8.3, RI.8.6 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
