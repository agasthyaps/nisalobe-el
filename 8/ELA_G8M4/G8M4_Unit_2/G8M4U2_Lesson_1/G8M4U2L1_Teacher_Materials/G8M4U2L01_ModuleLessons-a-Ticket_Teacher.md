(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, review the foreword of *Farewell to Manzanar*, and respond to the following question.

Based on your understanding of the foreword, what was the authors’ purpose for writing the book?

Responses will vary, but may include: the authors’ purpose was to inform readers about Jeanne’s experiences during her internment at Manzanar.
