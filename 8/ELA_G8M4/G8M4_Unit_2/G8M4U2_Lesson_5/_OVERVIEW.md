# Lesson 5: Analyze Connections and Distinctions: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapters 19–20
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 2: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read chapters 19–20 of the anchor text, *Farewell to Manzanar*, and track connections and distinctions and significant ideas in the text.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the meaning of unfamiliar vocabulary in chapters 19–20 of *Farewell to Manzanar*. (RI.8.4, L.8.4)
- I can analyze how the text makes connections among and distinctions between individuals, events, and ideas in chapters 19–20 of *Farewell to Manzanar* to develop my understanding of the text. (RI.8.3)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.3, RI.8.6 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Analyze *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapters 19–20 – RI.8.3 (35 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Significant Ideas: *Farewell to Manzanar*, Chapters 19–20 – RI.8.3 (5 minutes)
