# Lessons 18–19: End of Unit 2 Assessment: Write a Literary Argument Essay: *Farewell to Manzanar*
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 2: Lessons 18–19**

**Summary:** In these lessons, students write a literary argument essay for the End of Unit 2 Assessment. They also track progress and reflect on their learning.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.7, RI.8.1, W.8.1, W.8.4, W.8.5, W.8.6, W.8.9b, L.8.2c, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write a literary argument essay about the extent to which the film *Farewell to Manzanar* stays faithful to or departs from the text, evaluating the filmmakers’ choices in conveying a significant idea. (RL.8.1, RL.8.7, RI.8.1, W.8.1, W.8.4, W.8.5, W.8.6, W.8.9b, W.8.10, L.8.2c, L.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (10 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 2 Assessment: Write a Literary Argument Essay: *Farewell to Manzanar* (65 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress (15 minutes)
