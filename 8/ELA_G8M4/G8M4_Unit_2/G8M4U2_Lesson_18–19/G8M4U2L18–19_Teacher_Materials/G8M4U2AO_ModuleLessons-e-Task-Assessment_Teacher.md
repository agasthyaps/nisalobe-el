End of Unit 2 Assessment: Write a Literary Argument Essay: *Farewell to Manzanar*

(For Teacher Reference)

Use the Grade 8 Argument Writing Rubric (see Tools Page, <https://eled.org/tools>) to assess the writing standards as well as RL.8.1 and RI.8.1. To assess RL.8.7, use the rubric and sample outlines of possible responses provided below.
