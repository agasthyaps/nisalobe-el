# Lesson 1: Determine Central Idea and Analyze Point of View: “Seeking Redress”
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students read, annotate, and discuss an informational text to understand redress efforts made by the US government after Japanese American internment.

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine a central idea and analyze its development over the course of a text about Japanese American internment. (RI.8.2)
- I can analyze how the authors’ point of view is conveyed and differs from other points of view in a text about Japanese American internment. (RI.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RI.8.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read and Annotate “Seeking Redress” – RI.8.2, RI.8.6 (20 minutes) → Read and Annotate “The Simplest Lesson of Internment” – RI.8.6 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
