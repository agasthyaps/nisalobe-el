# Lesson 9: Prepare Presentations
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students prepare, practice, and receive peer feedback on their End of Unit 3 presentations.

**Focus Standards:** SL.8.5, SL.8.6, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can integrate a visual display into my presentation to clarify information and add interest about my researched organization. (SL.8.5)
- I can adapt my speech to make a presentation of my researched organization, focusing on speaking clearly and using academic language. (SL.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.8.5 (10 minutes) → Work Time → Practice Presentation – SL.8.5, SL.8.6 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Peer Feedback – SL.8.6 (10 minutes)
