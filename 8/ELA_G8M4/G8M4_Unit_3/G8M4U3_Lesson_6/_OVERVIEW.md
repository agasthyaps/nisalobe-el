# Lesson 6: Analyze Model Activist Organization Note-Catcher and Begin Research
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 6**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students review the performance task criteria, analyze a model note-catcher, and research organizations that they can contact to conduct an interview about modern-day activism that applies lessons from Japanese American internment.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.7, W.8.8

**Learning Targets:**
- I can conduct research to answer questions about activist organizations in my community. (W.8.7, W.8.8)

**Agenda:** Opening → Review Performance Task Directions (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze Model Activist Organization Note-Catcher (10 minutes) → Research and Select Organizations – W.8.8 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
