# Lesson 5: Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Collaborative Discussion: Internment and Redress
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 3: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** In this lesson, students participate in a collaborative discussion about the redress movement and lessons from Japanese American internment for the Mid-Unit 3 Assessment.

**Focus Standards:** SL.8.1c, SL.8.1d

**Learning Targets:**
- I can engage effectively in collaborative discussions about the lessons of internment and redress. (SL.8.1)
- I can share my independent research reading with my peers. (RL.8.10, RI.8.10)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 3 Assessment: Collaborative Discussion: Internment and Redress – SL.8.1 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Track Progress: Collaborative Discussion (5 minutes)
