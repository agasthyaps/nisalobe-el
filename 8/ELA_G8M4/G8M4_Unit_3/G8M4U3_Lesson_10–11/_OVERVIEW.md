# Lessons 10–11: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Activist Organization Presentation
**Grade 8: Module 4: Unit 3: Lessons 10–11**

**Summary:** In these lessons, students participate in the End of Unit 3 Assessment, delivering presentations on how community organizations embody lessons from Japanese American internment.

**Focus Standards:** SL.8.5, SL.8.6, L.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can integrate a visual display into my presentation to clarify information and add interest about my researched organization. (SL.8.5)
- I can adapt my speech to make a presentation of my researched organization, focusing on speaking clearly and using academic language. (SL.8.6, L.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 3 Assessment: Activist Organization Presentation (70 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Reflection (5 minutes)
