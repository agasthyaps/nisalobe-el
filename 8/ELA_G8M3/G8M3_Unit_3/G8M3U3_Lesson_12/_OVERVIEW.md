# Lesson 12: Revise Narrative Interview and Analyze Model Graphic Panels
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 12**

**Summary:** Students give and receive peer feedback on their narrative interviews and analyze a model performance task.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.3, W.8.4, W.8.5, SL.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can give kind, specific, and helpful feedback to my peers. (W.8.3, W.8.5)
- I can analyze how model graphic panels illustrate information in a narrative. (SL.8.5)
- I can plan the sequence of my graphic panels that illustrate a moment in my narrative. (W.8.3, SL.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.8.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Peer Feedback: Praise, Question, Suggestion – W.8.5 (15 minutes) → Analyze Graphic Panels – SL.8.5 (10 minutes) → Introduce Model Graphic Panels Sequence Planner – SL.8.5 (5 minutes)
