# Lesson 9: Plan a Narrative: Pacing, Dialogue, and Sensory Details
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** Students plan to use pacing and sensory details for their narrative piece.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.3, W.8.3a, W.8.3d, W.8.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the characteristics of an effective narrative interview. (W.8.3, W.8.4)
- I can use pacing, sensory images, and dialogue to appeal to the senses, capture the action, and help my reader understand the experiences of my upstander. (W.8.3b, W.8.3d)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.3 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model: Pacing, Dialogue, and Sensory Details – W.8.3b (15 minutes) → Plan a Narrative: Upstander Interview Answers – W.8.3 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
