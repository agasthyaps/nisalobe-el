13. Revise this sentence by rewriting it, adding sensory details and dialogue to engage the reader.

As I walked down the street in the morning, I saw the men walk into the newspaper office.

Answers will vary, but may include: I strolled past the cafe, enjoying the warmth of the morning sun. Then suddenly, without warning, the stomping of soldiers’ boots echoed through the lane, and my heartbeat quickened with of fear. Suddenly I heard boots trampling over the threshold of the *Amsterdam Daily* newspaper office. “What is it?” I whispered silently to myself in terror. Then I heard glass shatter as the door was knocked violently off its hinges and fell with a crash to the ground, while a woman’s shriek erupted from the office.
