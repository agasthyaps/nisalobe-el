# Lesson 3: Voices of Upstanders: Marek Edelman
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students read an informational text about a Holocaust upstander, consider characteristics that make this person an upstander, and write a reflection. Students also participate in a Language Dive.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.2, W.8.4, L.8.1c, L.8.2a, L.8.2b

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine a central idea and analyze its development in “Marek Edelman Obituary” Excerpt. (RI.8.2)
- I can write a text reflection about “Marek Edelman Obituary” Excerpt. (RI.8.1, RI.8.2)
- I can use commas, dashes, and ellipses to indicate a pause, break, or omission. (L.8.2a, L.8.2b)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.2a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read “Marek Edelman Obituary” Excerpt and Identify Central Idea – RI.8.2 (15 minutes) → Write a Text Reflection – RI.8.1 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
