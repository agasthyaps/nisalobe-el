# Lesson 15: Performance Task: Create and Present a Graphic Panel Depiction of a Fictional Holocaust Upstander
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 15**

**Summary:** Students present their graphic panels to an audience for their performance task and answer questions.

**Focus Standards:** SL.8.1, SL.8.5, SL.8.6

**Learning Targets:**
- I can present my graphic panels to an audience and highlight important aspects of my upstander’s experience. (SL.8.5, SL.8.6)
- I can share major takeaways and progress about my understanding of the module guiding questions. (SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – SL.8.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Present Graphic Panels – SL.8.5 (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Share Major Takeaways from Module 3 – SL.8.1 (10 minutes)
