# Lesson 11: End of Unit 3 Assessment: Write a Narrative in Interview Form

**Summary:** Students take their end of unit assessment, in which they draft their narrative pieces.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.3, W.8.4, W.8.6, L.8.1, L.8.2

**Learning Targets:**
