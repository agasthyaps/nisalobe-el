# Lesson 1: Voices of Upstanders: Johtje and Aart Vos
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 1**

**Summary:** Students read an informational text about a Holocaust upstander, consider characteristics that make this person an upstander, and write a reflection.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.1, RI.8.2, W.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine a central idea and analyze its development in the article “Johtje Vos, 97; Sheltered Jews in Her Home in WWII Holland, Saving 36.” (RI.8.2)
- I can generate characteristics of a Holocaust upstander.
- I can write a text reflection about “Johtje Vos, 97; Sheltered Jews in Her Home in WWII Holland, Saving 36.” (RI.8.1, W.8.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read “Johtje Vos, 97; Sheltered Jews in Her Home in WWII Holland, Saving 36” and Identify Central Idea – RI.8.2 (20 minutes) → Characteristics of a Holocaust Upstander (5 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
