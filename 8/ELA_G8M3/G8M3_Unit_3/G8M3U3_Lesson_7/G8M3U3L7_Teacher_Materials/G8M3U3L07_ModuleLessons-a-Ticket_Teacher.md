Entrance Ticket: Unit 3, Lesson 7

W.8.4

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions**: As you enter class, answer the questions.

Why do people conduct interviews?

Answers will vary, but may include the following: people conduct interviews to get specific information from a reliable source, or to gain perspective from a personal experience, and to share that information with a broader audience.

If you could interview an upstander from the Holocaust, what questions would you like to ask?

Answers will vary, but may include the following: How did your experience in the Holocaust impact your life? In what ways did you persevere? What impacted your decision to take action?
