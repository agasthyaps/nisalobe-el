# Lesson 7: Analyze a Model Narrative Interview
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 3: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students analyze a model in order to determine the essential components of an effective narrative interview.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.3, W.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the essential components of an effective narrative interview. (W.8.3, W.8.4)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Review Traits of Upstanders Anchor Chart (5 minutes) → Read and Annotate a Model Narrative – W.8.3 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
