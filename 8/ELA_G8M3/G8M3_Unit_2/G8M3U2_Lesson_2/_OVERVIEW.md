# Lesson 2: Close Read: “Often a Minute”
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students closely read a new poem analyzing language, structure, and meaning. Then they compare and contrast the structures and meanings of *Maus I* and the poem.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.4, RL.8.5, L.8.1a, L.8.5, L.8.5a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the theme of a new poem and analyze its development over the course of the text. (RL.8.2)
- I can identify structural elements of a text and analyze how the structure contributes to its meaning and style. (RL.8.5)
- I can determine the meaning of words and phrases as they are used in a text, including figurative and connotative meanings, and analyze the impact of specific word choices on tone and meaning. (RL.8.4, L.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.5 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Close Read: “Often a Minute” – RL.8.4 (20 minutes) → Language Dive: “Often a Minute” Lines 15–16 – RL.8.4 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
