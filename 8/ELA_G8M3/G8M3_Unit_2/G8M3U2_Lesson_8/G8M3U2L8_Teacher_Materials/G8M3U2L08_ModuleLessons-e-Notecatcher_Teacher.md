<td colspan="2"><p>Gist Statement:</p>
<p><strong>Abe must decide if he should escape a ghetto and leave his family behind or stay with his family and face death.</strong></p></td>
</tr>
<tr class="even">
<td><p>Key Plot Elements</p>
<p><strong>Abe and his family are imprisoned by the Nazis in a ghetto.</strong></p>
<p><strong>2,000 Jews live in close quarters, share one toilet, constantly wait in lines for food and water, and die from the diseases caused by waste build up and lack of healthcare.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Abe has the chance to escape with his friend but must leave his family behind.</strong></p>
<p><strong>His mother begs him to stay so he may remain with his family and face the same death.</strong></p>
<p><strong>His father convinces him to take this opportunity to escape and return to save his family if possible.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Abe escapes but is never able to save his family.</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Character and Analysis</p>
<p>How would you describe Abe’s character?</p>
<p><strong>determined, caring, he has a sense of agency that he can try to survive</strong></p>
<p><strong>Abe has compassion and empathy for his family and doesn’t know if he should leave them.</strong></p>
<p>What in the text makes you think so?</p>
<p><strong>Abe is trying very hard to escape the ghetto, which makes him determined.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Abe feels guilty about leaving his family to die and considers staying because he cares that his mother doesn’t want him to leave.</strong></p></td>
</tr>
