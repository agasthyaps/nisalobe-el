# Lesson 8: Voice from the Ghettos
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students read and summarize a memoir of a Holocaust victim in a ghetto. Students also participate in a grammar mini lesson.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, L.8.1c, L.8.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine a theme and analyze its development in an excerpt of “ *Abe’s Story*: Excerpts and Synopsis.” (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)
- I can write an objective summary of an excerpt of “ *Abe’s Story*: Excerpts and Synopsis.” (RL.8.2)
- I can use verbs in different moods to achieve particular effects. (L.8.1c, L.8.3a)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.1c (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read Excerpt of “ *Abe’s Story*: Excerpts and Synopsis” and Track Gist, Plot, Character, and Theme – RL.8.2 (20 minutes) → Write an Objective Summary: Excerpt of “ *Abe’s Story*: Excerpts and Synopsis” – RL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
