# Lesson 14: Honor and Remember Voices of the Holocaust: Silent Commemorative Poster Share
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 14**

**Summary:** Students participate in a silent gallery walk to honor and remember voices of the Holocaust.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, W.8.10, SL.8.1

**Learning Targets:**
- I can deepen my understanding of Holocaust victims’ and survivors’ experiences by recording notices and wonders of Holocaust voices. (RI.8.1)
- I can reflect on the commemorative posters presented in the Commemorative Poster Share and name connections or new learning that increased my empathy for the Holocaust voices shared. (W.8.10)
- I can thoughtfully participate in a discussion that commemorates voices of the Holocaust. (SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner and Set Tone for Commemorative Poster Share – SL.8.1. (5 minutes) → Work Time → Silent Commemorative Poster Share – RI.8.1 (25 minutes) → QuickWrite: Reflect on Commemorative Posters – W.8.10 (5 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
