Proof Paragraphs 1a and 1b

Each paragraph has the following traits:

- Opening sentence that states the point related to the focus statement for the particular text

- Evidence: provides facts and evidence from the text(s) that support point 1

- Elaboration: what do the facts and evidence show?

- Restatement and/or reflection

- Transitional words and phrases throughout

Proof Paragraphs 2a and 2b

Each paragraph has the following traits:

- Good transitions from the previous paragraph

- Opening sentence that states the point related to the focus statement for the particular text

- Evidence: provides facts and evidence from the text(s) that support point 2

- Elaboration: what do the facts and evidence show?

- Restatement and/or reflection of the focus

- Transitional words and phrases throughout

Conclusion

- What? Restates the focus statement and points

- So what? Reflects on the topic in the prompt and its greater significance
