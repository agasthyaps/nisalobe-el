# Lesson 7: Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Structure and Meaning in a New Poem and *Maus I*
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 7**

**Summary:** Students take their mid-unit assessment, comparing and contrasting structure and meaning in a new poem and *Maus I*.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, RL.8.4, RL.8.5, SL.8.1, L.8.1a, L.8.5a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine the theme of a new poem and analyze its development over the course of the text. (RL.8.2)
- I can compare and contrast how the structural elements of two texts contribute to their meaning. (RL.8.5)
- I can determine the meaning of words and phrases as they are used in a text, including figurative and connotative meanings, and analyze the impact of specific word choices on tone and meaning (RL.8.4, L.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Mid-Unit 2 Assessment: Compare and Contrast Structure and Meaning in a New Poem and *Maus I* (30 minutes) → Closing and Assessment → Collaborative Discussion – RL.8.2 (10 minutes)
