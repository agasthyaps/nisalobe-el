Collaborative Discussion Sentence Starters  
Anchor Chart

SL.8.1c, SL.8.1d

Sentence starters for acknowledging new information in a discussion:

- “I didn’t know that, I wonder . . .”

- “That is interesting information because . . .”

- “I think what she’s saying is . . .”

- “Where did you get that information so I can learn more about . . .”

Sentence starters for using evidence to pose or respond to questions in a discussion:

- “On page X of \[Text\] . . .”

- “According to the text . . .”

- “According to the film . . .”
