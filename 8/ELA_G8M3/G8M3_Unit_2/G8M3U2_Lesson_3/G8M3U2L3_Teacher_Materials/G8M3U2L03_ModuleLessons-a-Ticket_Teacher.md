(Answer for Teacher Reference)

**Directions**: As you enter class, complete the tasks and answer the question.

1.  Read the following line from the poem “Often a Minute.”

“And I’m afraid to turn the radio on,”

What is the function of the infinitive phrase **to turn the radio on**?

2.  It is functioning as a noun describing an activity that is the subject of the sentence.

3.  It is functioning as a noun that is the direct object of the verb **be**.

4.  <u>It is functioning as an adverb describing the adjective **afraid**.</u>

5.  It is functioning as an adjective describing **the radio**.

Source: Klein, Magdalena. “Often a Minute.” *Pearls and Lace*. Translated by Susan Geroe, Fithian Press, 1996. Used by permission.
