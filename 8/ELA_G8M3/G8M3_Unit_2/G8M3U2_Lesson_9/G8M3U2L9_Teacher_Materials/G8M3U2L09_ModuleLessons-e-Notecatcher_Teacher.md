<td><p>Key Plot Elements</p>
<p><strong>Elie and the other Jewish prisoners are hungry, thirsty, and cramped on a train to Kaschau.</strong></p>
<p><strong>German soldiers count every Jewish prisoner on the train and threaten to shoot and kill them if they try to escape.</strong></p>
<p><strong>They also force them to surrender their gold, silver, and watches to the soldiers or they will be killed.</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Character and Analysis</p>
<p>How would you describe Elie’s character?</p>
<p><strong>saddened by his circumstance</strong></p>
<p>What in the text makes you think so?</p>
<p><strong>Elie describes them as falling into a trap and the Germans threatening to shoot them like dogs.</strong></p></td>
