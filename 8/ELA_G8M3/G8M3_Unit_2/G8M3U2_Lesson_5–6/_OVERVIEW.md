# Lessons 5–6: Draft an Informative Essay and Peer Critique
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lessons 5–6**

**Summary:** Students draft their literary analysis essay and participate in a peer critique and revisions.

**Focus Standards:** W.8.2, W.8.5

**Learning Targets:**
- I can write an explanatory essay to examine and analyze the structure of two texts and the impact of structure on theme. (RL.8.5, W.8.2)
- I can give kind, helpful, and specific feedback to my partner. (W.8.5, SL.8.1)
- I can revise my writing based on peer feedback. (W.8.5)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – W.8.2a (10 minutes) → Work Time → Draft Informative Essay – W.8.2 (45 minutes) → Peer Critique – W.8.5 (20 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
