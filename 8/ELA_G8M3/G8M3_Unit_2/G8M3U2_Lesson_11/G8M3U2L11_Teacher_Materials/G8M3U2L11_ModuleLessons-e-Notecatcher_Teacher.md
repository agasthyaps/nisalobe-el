<p><strong>He helps hide sixteen children from the gas chambers and survives to see liberation.</strong></p></td>

<td><p>Key plot elements</p>
<p><strong>Franziska and her mother are summoned to be sterilized because they are deaf.</strong></p>
<p><strong>After going to court, Franziska’s mother is allowed to remain unsterilized because she promises not to have more children, but Franziska is forced into sterilization.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Franziska is worried that her boyfriend, who wants children, will not want to be with her, but he continues to love her.</strong></p></td>
<td><p>Character and Analysis</p>
<p><strong>Franziska is loving and heartbroken.</strong></p>
<p><strong>She loves her boyfriend, wants to have children, and is deeply distraught that she cannot.</strong></p></td>
</tr>
