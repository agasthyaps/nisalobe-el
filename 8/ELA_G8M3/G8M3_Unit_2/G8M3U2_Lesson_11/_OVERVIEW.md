# Lesson 11: Voices of Other Survivors
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 11**

**Summary:** Students read and summarize a memoir of a Holocaust victim or survivor of their choice. Students give and receive peer feedback and apply revisions to their summary.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, W.8.5, L.8.1b, L.8.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can independently determine a theme and analyze its development in a text. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)
- I can write an objective summary of a text. (RL.8.2)
- I can give kind, helpful, and specific feedback to my partner and revise my writing based on feedback. (W.8.5, SL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.1b (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read Texts of Survivors and Identify Development of Theme – RL.8.2 (20 minutes) → Write an Objective Summary – RL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
