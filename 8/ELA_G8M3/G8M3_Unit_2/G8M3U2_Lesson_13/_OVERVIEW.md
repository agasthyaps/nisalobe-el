# Lesson 13: Create a Commemorative Poster: Visual Representations of Holocaust Survivors and Victims
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 2: Lesson 13**

**Summary:** Students choose one of the summaries they wrote in Lessons 8–11 and create a commemorative poster to honor that victim or survivor.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.2, W.8.4, W.8.6, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can determine criteria for an effective commemorative poster honoring a voice of the Holocaust. (RL.8.2)
- I can use visuals and text effectively in a commemorative poster to highlight the themes and honor a voice of the Holocaust. (RL.8.2, W.8.6)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Analyze a Model Poster and Review Criteria – RL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Select Visual Representations of Theme and Summary – W.8.6 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
