Entrance Ticket: Unit 2, Lesson 13

RL.8.4, L.8.4c

(Example for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** As you enter class, answer the questions below.

Use an online or print dictionary to find the meaning of the word **commemorate**.

To recall and show respect for (someone or something), often marked by some ceremony or observation

In the space below, brainstorm different ways people **commemorate** the experiences of others.

Answers will vary, but may include the following: people write tributes, pass down oral stories, sing songs, create memory boards, erect memorial statues and plaques, etc.

Why is it important to honor and remember the experiences of others?

Answers will vary, but may include that it is import to remember the experiences of others in order to learn about the past.
