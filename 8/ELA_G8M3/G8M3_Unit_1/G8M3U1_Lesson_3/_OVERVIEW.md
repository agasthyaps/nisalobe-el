# Lesson 3: Read “The Holocaust: An Introductory History”
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 3**

**Summary:** Students are introduced to and explore a glossary of terms from the Holocaust. They will use this glossary to help make meaning of unknown words and phrases as they read an overview of the history of the Holocaust and reflect on thinking questions.

**Focus Standards:** RI.8.4, SL.8.1, L.8.4c

**Learning Targets:**
- I can deepen my understanding of the Holocaust by determining the meaning of words and phrases using a glossary. (RI.8.4, L.8.4c)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduce the Holocaust Glossary – L.8.4c (10 minutes) → Read “The Holocaust: An Introductory History” – RI.8.4 (25 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
