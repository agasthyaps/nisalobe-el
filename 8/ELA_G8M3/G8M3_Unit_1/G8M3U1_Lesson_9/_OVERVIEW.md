# Lesson 9: Plan a Whole-Book Literary Summary of *Maus I*
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 9**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 6 of their anchor text and will plan a whole-book summary.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, L.8.1b, L.8.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can form and use active and passive voice to achieve particular effects. (L.8.1b, L.8.3a)
- I can track characters, plot, and emerging themes in *Maus I*. (RL.8.2)
- I can plan an effective literary (whole-book) summary of *Maus I*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.2 (5 minutes) → Mini Language Dive: Model Whole-Book Literary Summary – L.8.3a (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Maus I*, Chapter 6 (15 minutes) → Track Gist, Plot, Character, and Theme: *Maus I*, Chapter 6 – RL.8.2 (10 minutes)
