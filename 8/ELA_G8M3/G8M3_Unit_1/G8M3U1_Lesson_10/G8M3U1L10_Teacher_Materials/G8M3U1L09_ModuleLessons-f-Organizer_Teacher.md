<p><strong>As Art interviews Vladek, we learn about how Vladek survived the Holocaust.</strong></p>
<p><strong>When the war began, Vladek became a Polish soldier.</strong></p>
<p><strong>He was captured by the Germans and became a prisoner of war.</strong></p>
<p><strong>For months, he lived in a German prisoner of war camp.</strong></p>
<p><strong>When he returned to Poland, he found that terrible things were happening to Jews in his city, Sosnowiec.</strong></p>
