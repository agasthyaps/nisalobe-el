# Lesson 10: End of Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Passive Voice and Active Voice and Summarize *Maus I*
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 10**

**Summary:** Students take their end of unit assessment, in which they will write a summary of their anchor text.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, RL.8.10, W.8.9a, L.8.1b, L.8.3a

**Learning Targets:**
- I can use the passive voice in my writing. (L.8.1b, L.8.3a)
- I can write an effective literary summary of a novel. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner (5 minutes) → Work Time → End of Unit 1 Assessment: Analyze Passive Voice and Active Voice and Summarize *Maus I* (20 minutes) → Discuss Development of Theme in *Maus I* – RL.8.2 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
