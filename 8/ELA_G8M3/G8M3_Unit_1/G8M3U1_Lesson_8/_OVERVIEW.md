# Lesson 8: Analyze a Model Whole-Book Literary Summary
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 8**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 5 of their anchor text, will track gist, plot, characterization, and theme, and will analyze a whole-book summary in order to prepare to write their own.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2

**Learning Targets:**
- I can track characters, plot, and emerging themes in *Maus I*. (RL.8.2)
- I can identify characteristics of an effective whole-book literary summary. (RL.8.1, RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.2 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Maus I*, Chapter 5 (15 minutes) → Track Gist, Plot, Character, and Theme: *Maus I*, Chapter 5 – RL.8.2 (15 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
