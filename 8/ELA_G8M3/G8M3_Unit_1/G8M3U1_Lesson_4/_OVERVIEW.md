# Lesson 4: Close Read: *Maus I*, Pages 11–23
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 4**

**Summary:** Students closely read the first chapter of *Maus I* and will analyze how word choices impact meaning and tone and how dialogue reveals aspects of character. Students also begin to track gist, plot, and characterization.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, RL.8.3, RL.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how dialogue between characters and incidents in *Maus I* reveal aspects of Vladek’s character or provoke a decision. (RL.8.1, RL.8.3)
- I can analyze how specific word choices impact meaning and tone in *Maus I*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.4)
- I can track gist, plot, and characterization in *Maus I*. (RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Close Read: *Maus I*, Pages 11–23 – RL.8.3 (20 minutes) → Culminating Task – RL.8.3 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
