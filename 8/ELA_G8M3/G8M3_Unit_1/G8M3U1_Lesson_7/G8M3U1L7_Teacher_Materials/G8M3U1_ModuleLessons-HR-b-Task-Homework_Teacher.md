Homework: Selected and Constructed Response Questions: Analyze Voice: *Maus I*, Chapter 5

(Answers for Teacher Reference)

**Directions:** Answer the questions below about passive and active voice.

1.  On page 106, panel 1, in the sentence, “Each day we were taken to Sosnowiec to work in the German ‘shops,’” Vladek uses the passive voice. What effect does this have on the meaning of the information in the statement?

By using the passive voice to make this statement, Vladek is emphasizing the experience of being taken to work rather than the people who took them there.

2.  Change the following sentence on page 113, panel 2, from active to passive voice to shift the emphasis to the object of the verb **dragged**.

“We dragged him up to our bunker.”

He was dragged up to the bunker.
