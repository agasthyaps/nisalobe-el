Synopsis: *Maus I*, Chapter 4

**Name:** **Date:**

- Vladek finds different schemes to earn money and keep the Nazis from taking him to a camp.

- Vladek uses money, his friendships, and connections to remain alive and protect his family.

- Vladek considers sending his first born son to live with strangers to keep him alive.

- Associates of Vladek are hung in the street for giving cloth to Jews without coupons.

- Elderly people are sent to death camps.

- The Jewish people are terrified as the Nazis transfer more and more people to death camps, and Vladek is trying to stay alive.
