# Lesson 5:*Maus I*: Dialogue, Tone, and Character
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 5**

**Summary:** Students read chapter 2 of their anchor text, participate in a Language Dive, and answer text-dependent questions about how dialogue and incidents reveal aspects of character and how word choice impacts meaning and tone. Students also track gist, plot, and characterization.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.2, RL.8.3, RL.8.4, L.8.1b, L.8.3a, L.8.4

**Learning Targets:**
- I can analyze how dialogue between characters and incidents in *Maus I* reveal aspects of character or provoke a decision. (RL.8.1, RL.8.3)
- I can analyze how specific word choices impact meaning and tone in *Maus I*. (RL.8.1, RL.8.4)
- I can track gist, plot, and characterization in *Maus I*. (RL.8.2)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – L.8.4 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Read *Maus I*, Chapter 2: Track Gist and Plot (15 minutes) → Language Dive: *Maus I*, Chapter 2 – RL.8.4 (10 minutes) → Closing and Assessment
