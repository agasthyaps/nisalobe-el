# Lesson 2: Read a Graphic Novel and Build Background Knowledge
**Grade 8: Module 3: Unit 1: Lesson 2**

**Summary:** Students are introduced to their anchor text and will learn about how to read a graphic novel; they will then read the first chapter and answer text-dependent questions.

**Focus Standards:** RL.8.1, RL.8.3

**Learning Targets:**
- I can read a text in graphic novel format.
- I can answer questions about a text and cite evidence to support my analysis. (RL.8.1)

**Agenda:** Opening → Engage the Learner – RL.8.1 (5 minutes) → Work Time → Introduction: How to Read a Graphic Novel (10 minutes) → Preview *Maus I* – RL.8.1 (10 minutes) → Read *Maus I*, Chapter 1 (15 minutes)
